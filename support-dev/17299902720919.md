<h3 id="h_01HB8YZFMVJZZJH9ZJK9FPFX87">Payout Eligibility Review</h3>
<p>Payouts are not automatic. Developer Support will create a ticket for the team owner and conduct a payout eligibility review. Once approved, Developer Support will enable payouts, allowing the team to receive them.</p>
<p>Please note that if not all criteria are met, you may have the ability to remedy this. Guidance will be given on what is left to complete in order to receive payouts.<!-- notionvc: 40086daf-7aaa-4bcc-b0fc-f8c3e5df3f6a --></p>
<h3 id="h_01HB8YZFMVC6DEWD4Z02MSSQY6">Payout</h3>
<p>All payouts are processed through Stripe and revenue history can be viewed from your dashboard there. This dashboard can be accessed via your Team’s <a href="https://discord.com/developers/teams" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Developer Portal page</a> under the Payout Settings tab.<!-- notionvc: f4473006-e1e6-45f5-aafe-75097dfc970f --></p>
<p>Eligible payouts will be provided within 45 days after the end of each calendar month. For example, for offerings purchased in January, you will receive payouts within 45 days after January 31st. An eligible payout can be defined as the first $100 earned [less applicable payment processing fees and transaction fees] and for the following billing cycles, in amounts greater than <span class="discussion-id-d3f0945f-16e6-4424-a6bf-4b46c25706b8 notion-enable-hover" data-token-index="1">$25</span>. This means that if your balance is less than $25 on time of payout, the payment is deferred, and the balance is carried over to the next billing cycle until an amount of $25 or greater can be paid. <!-- notionvc: aabf015c-d75f-40f5-aaf7-30a811f58862 --></p>
<p>As described in our <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/5330075836311-Monetization-Terms" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Monetization Terms</a>, for each <span class="discussion-id-692c7d83-de7d-4980-a327-6b45731c4844 notion-enable-hover" data-token-index="1">Premium App Subscription</span> or One-Time Purchase you offer, you will receive 85% of the total payments from the user less applicable payment processing fees and transaction fees, with the remaining 15% as a platform fee to Discord for the first $1 million USD in cumulative gross sales generated by their apps on Discord, effective June 6, 2024. Once your developer team's cumulative gross sales reach $1 million USD, the Platform Fee will move back to the standard rate of 30%. We may also deduct chargebacks, refunds, and associated fees from your payout.<!-- notionvc: fe772ac4-bc04-46ea-ae48-3fd632c52c1c --></p>
