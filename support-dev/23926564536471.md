<h2 id="h_01HZTGPSDWA4PSFYB216F0ZJTW">Getting Your App Verified</h2>
<p>Verification is an important and exciting step in the development of your app! Verifying your app means you feel it is ready to scale to a public audience, and we have some requirements that you must meet to help demonstrate that preparation. Verification is required for your app to scale past 100 servers.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><img src="https://support-dev.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/24040991841943" alt="Screenshot 2024-05-30 at 9.38.21 AM.png"></p>
<p> </p>
<p>In the <strong><a href="https://discord.com/developers/applications">developer portal</a></strong>, under the Application that you wish to verify, you will see a tab labeled “App Verification.” In this tab you will find a checklist of requirements for verification. Once you meet all of these requirements, you will be able to click the “Verify App” button, and that is that!</p>
<p> </p>
<p><img src="https://support-dev.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26036176576919" alt="Screenshot 2024-08-30 at 3.53.42 PM.png"></p>
<p id="h_01HZTGYAGWDVGZQCYZPH812Z0X">Once all of these are green you are good to go!</p>
<p> </p>
<p>The most noteworthy requirement is that the owner of the development team which owns the app will need to verify their identity through Stripe, our identity verification provider. If you have questions about this, please see our <a href="https://support-dev.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/6226051178775"><strong>Stripe FAQ</strong></a>. You may be asked to periodically reverify your id with stripe.</p>
<p>If you have additional questions, don’t be afraid to reach out to our developer support team <strong><a href="https://support-dev.discord.com/hc/requests/new">here</a></strong>.</p>
