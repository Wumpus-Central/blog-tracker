<h2 id="h_01K72EYGN2S9R9NXYQBHK6WMP3">What Does the App Directory Offer Developers</h2><p>The App Directory is <em>the</em> place to grow your app and offers developers:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="eb4940a1b0d7e6d1d44ac1b6c871553cf">More active users of your app</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e424a129679ebc7a54ea0ed5184668f97">A customizable profile page to show off your app in your own way</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e4cf2d475aaaeb0103cbda0c3300c5181">A way to display your hard work to friends, communities, or even recruiters</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e10e477a308df65efd1908720ccf426ff">Not only will users be able to find your app without having to leave Discord, but the App Directory will also help you spend less time and effort promoting your app. </li>
</ul><h2 id="h_01K72EYGN2NP390DABHSP73FRN">Joining the App Directory</h2><p>Let’s jump right into how to add your app to the App Directory.</p><h3 id="h_01K72EYGN2FGWP7RD2BHDC07WA">Verifying your Application</h3><p>The first step of the process is to get your app verified. The Discovery Settings section lists all required items to build out your App’s Profile Page.</p><p>If you would like an in-depth description of what the Product Page requires and how it affects your article</a>.</p><p>View our App Directory Best Practices Guide <a href="https://discord.com/developers/docs/discovery/best-practices" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.</p><h3
