<h1 id="h_01JP33C8GH8DHDVMP0YBBBGH13">Introduction</h1>
<p>To obtain console binaries for the <a href="https://support-dev.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/30127085446039" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Discord Social SDK</a>, you'll need proper credentials and must complete our console middleware request process. Start by requesting the Discord Social SDK middleware through your console's developer portal.</p>
<h1 id="h_01JP33DR398CK8ZP3H1MA63NQC">Discord Social SDK for PlayStation™</h1>
<p><strong>Step 1.</strong> Sign in to your <a href="https://partners.playstation.net/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">PlayStation™ Partners</a> account.</p>
<p><strong>Step 2.</strong> Select ‘<strong>Discord Social SDK’</strong> from the <strong>Tools &amp; Middleware</strong> directory to confirm developer status.</p>
<p>After completing the request, proceed to the "What Next?" section below.</p>
<h1 id="h_01JP33DYW142NNPPFTPSA2X57G">Discord Social SDK for Xbox</h1>
<p><strong>Step 1.</strong> Sign in to your <a href="https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/games/publish?xr=footnav" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Xbox Game Dev Portal</a> account.</p>
<p><strong>Step 2.</strong> Select ‘<strong>Discord Social SDK</strong>’ from the Middleware Partner dropdown <a href="https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/games/support/request-gdkx-middleware" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a></p>
<p><strong>Step 3.</strong> Click '<strong>Submit Request</strong>'.</p>
<p>After completing the request, proceed to the "What Next?" section below.</p>
<h1 id="h_01JP33C8GJN1XX6ZVM9EHXXD9C">What Next?</h1>
<p>After the applicable console platform contacts us about your request, we <span class="discussion-id-1aff46fd-48aa-8071-b465-001c64cc5c77 notion-enable-hover" data-token-index="0">may</span><!-- notionvc: a3e9814d-e2c6-4909-8fc8-19e184e36b0b --> contact you at the email address associated with your console developer portal account with a form link to gather more information from you.</p>
<p>We may share this information with the console platform. Please note that you are <strong>not</strong> required to share confidential information about your game--providing temporary names, franchise names, or codenames will suffice where titles are requested.</p>
<p>Once your form submission has been reviewed, if approved, we'll set up your application with the appropriate permissions to access the console binaries and notify you directly.</p>
