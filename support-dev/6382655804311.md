<h1 id="h_01JRE0RKMQQ1X1Q59A11MS2XPW">What are Select Menus for Apps?</h1>
<p>Select menus let apps give you choices to pick from. On desktop, this looks like a dropdown menu. On iOS and Android, you'll see a pop-up sheet to pick your choice.</p>
<p>Depending on how the app works, you can pick either one choice from the list or multiple (for when you just can't decide on one flavor of ice cream).</p>
<div class="book-box">If you want to get the full details, head on over to the <a href="https://discord.com/developers/docs/interactions/message-components#select-menus" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">API documentation</a> in our Developer Portal.</div>

<h2 id="h_01JRE0RKMQSGMNA5SHKH3G1SZ4">I run or moderate a server - do I need to allow or change any permissions to enable apps with select menus to work properly in my server?</h2>
<p>No new permissions are needed to enable select menus in your servers.</p>
<h2 id="h_01JRE0RKMQ87YT9H7YR8KN8YJR">As a developer, do you have any tips for how I can make my app transition to select menus in a way that doesn't confuse or impact my users?</h2>
<p>As with all new features, users would probably benefit from a quick heads up if the way they're used to interacting with your app is about to change. We recommend creating a private or controlled testing channel or server for a small group of users, making sure things work properly, and then posting to users in your support server that select menus have arrived. Every app is different, so do what works best for you!</p>
