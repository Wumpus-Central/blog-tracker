<p>Transferring ownership of your developer product is a significant action that affects both you, applicable developer team members, and any applications you or your team manages. This guide will walk you through the process and important considerations.</p><hr><h2 id="h_01K4ZT4PD58S0DE3D1753FX3Q7">Before You Start</h2><p>Only the <strong>current owner</strong> of a developer product can request an ownership transfer. Team members cannot initiate transfer requests on behalf of the owner.</p><p> ⚠️ <strong>Note:</strong> If your developer team owns verified applications, transferring ownership will require <strong>removing verification</strong> from those applications. The new owner will need to reapply for verification after the transfer is complete.</p><h3 id="h_01K4ZT4PD7HYBFTWHEF1VMBZWF">What You'll Need</h3><p>Submitting a request to Developer Support: <a href="https://dis.gd/developer-support/teams-and-ownership">https://dis.gd/developer-support/teams-and-ownership</a> is only required for Verified Apps or Teams that own Verified Apps. Before submitting a transfer request, gather the following information:</p><ul>
<li>
<strong>App ID</strong> of the application involved in the transfer</li>
<li>
<strong>Team ID</strong> of the developer team you want to transfer</li>
<li>
<strong>User ID</strong> of the person who will receive ownership</li>
<li>
<strong>Reason</strong> for the transfer</li>
<li>
<strong>Consent</strong> from the person receiving ownership</li>
</ul><h2 id="h_01K4ZT4PDAXA3K9A4A9KFSJFKT">The Transfer Process</h2><h3 id="h_01K4ZT4PDAX82W4QHT0NA1DE8V">Step 1: Dual-Party Consent Required</h3><p>After the <strong>current owner</strong> requests a transfer, Discord requires <strong>consent from the recipient</strong> before any ownership transfer. Both parties have <strong>30 days to respond</strong> to our requests.</p><ul><li>If either party says no or doesn't respond within 30 days, the transfer request will be denied.</li></ul><h3 id="h_01K4ZT4PDBPAH1NCQRV78SSNT6">Step 2: Verification Removal</h3><p>Once consent is reached, if your team owns any verified applications, we'll need to:</p><ul>
<li>Remove verification from all applications owned by the team</li>
<li>Delete any associated identity verification information</li>
<li>Allow the new owner to reapply for verification after the transfer</li>
</ul><h3 id="h_01K4ZT4PDC2S7TYHBVNZB4FGQN">Step 3: Transfer Execution</h3><p>Once both parties consent and verification is removed (if applicable), the transfer will be processed through Discord's Developer Portal.</p><h2 id="h_01K4ZT4PDCGBKV38PVWTB6EGFS">Submitting Your Request</h2><p>To request a developer team ownership transfer:</p><ol>
<li>
<strong>Contact Discord Support</strong> through our <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/requests/new">support form</a>
</li>
<li>
<strong>Select the appropriate category</strong> for developer product ownership transfers</li>
<li>
<strong>Provide all required information</strong> listed above</li>
<li>
<strong>Wait for our response</strong> with next steps</li>
</ol><figure style="width: 90.15%;" class="wysiwyg-image wysiwyg-image-resized"><img style="aspect-ratio: 2983/1775;" src="https://support-dev.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/34905356136471" alt="image (4).png" width="2983" height="1775"></figure><h2 id="h_01K4ZT4PDFRKZTXJJ25WQQ9FV1">Moving Applications to Your Team</h2><h3 id="h_01K4ZT4PDFYYSMSEPDRE5VWP9Q">Individual to Team Transfer</h3><p>If you currently own verified applications individually and want to transfer them to your team:</p><ol>
<li>Ensure your team has completed identity verification</li>
<li>Navigate to your application settings</li>
<li>Go to the "General Information" section</li>
<li>Look for the "Transfer App to Team" option below</li>
<li>Select your team as the new owner</li>
<li>Confirm the transfer</li>
</ol><figure style="width: 51.57%;" class="wysiwyg-image wysiwyg-image-resized"><img style="aspect-ratio: 893/627;" src="https://support-dev.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/34905356138007" alt="image (3).png" width="893" height="627"></figure><p> ⚠️ <strong>Important:</strong> Transferring verified applications to a team may require re-verification if the team doesn't have up-to-date identification on file. For verified applications, you'll need to reach out to <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=12094720423319">Developer Support</a> for assistance.</p><h2 id="h_01K4ZT4PDHTW6WPGQCTFH0DX44">Important Limitations</h2><h3 id="h_01K4ZT4PDHY5J8VC98ASCEWYDQ">Account Standing Requirements</h3><p>We cannot process ownership transfers unless all team members are in good account standing, all applications owned by the team are in good standing, and none of the applications are currently banned or suspended.</p><h3 id="h_01K4ZT4PDHGF6H5REJPN397J3B">Recent Activity Restrictions</h3><p>Please note that ownership transfers are limited to one request per developer product within a 90-day period. Additionally, if there have been recent account compromise inquiries, your transfer request may be delayed or prevented until those issues are resolved.</p><h3 id="h_01K4ZT4PDJMBJBD870EGYCANKD">Special Circumstances</h3><p>If you're unable to access your account due to issues like 2FA lockout, additional verification steps may be required. Contact support for guidance on these situations.</p><h2 id="h_01K4ZT4PDJ3DANB5STBMAN8XEV">What Happens After Transfer</h2><p>Once the transfer is complete:</p><ol>
<li>
<strong>Ownership changes immediately</strong> in the Developer Portal</li>
<li>
<strong>The new owner gains full control</strong> of the team and its applications</li>
<li>
<strong>Previous verification is removed</strong> (if applicable)</li>
<li>
<strong>The new owner can reapply for verification</strong> when ready</li>
</ol><h2 id="h_01K4ZT4PDK5NAY44ESDQHRKDMJ">Common Issues and Solutions</h2><h3 id="h_01K4ZT4PDKN7XD8C3TFWRY7041">Transfer Requirements</h3><p>When transferring applications to a team:</p><ul>
<li>Both individual owner and recipient must consent to transfers</li>
<li>Verified applications will lose verification status if the team lacks proper identity verification</li>
<li>The transfer process may take some time to complete</li>
</ul><h3 id="h_01K4ZT4PDM2VAYZD2WXA7NPFXN">Verification Concerns</h3><p>If you're concerned about losing verification status:</p><ul>
<li>Complete team identity verification before transferring</li>
<li>Plan transfers during low-activity periods</li>
<li>Have re-verification documentation ready</li>
</ul><div class="info-box"><p>If you have questions about the transfer process or encounter any issues, please review our developer documentation for additional guidance or contact Discord Support through our support form. Please allow up to 48 hours for an initial response during business days.</p></div><h2 id="h_01K4ZT4PDNHR283KJ2ZAG4ZGXT">Frequently Asked Questions</h2><p><strong>Q: Can I transfer ownership to multiple people?</strong></p><p>A: No, developer teams can only have one owner at a time.</p><p><strong>Q: How long does the transfer process take?</strong></p><p>A: The process typically takes 30-60 days, depending on how quickly both parties respond to consent requests.</p><p><strong>Q: Can I cancel a transfer request?</strong></p><p>A: Yes, you can contact support to cancel a pending transfer request before it's completed.</p><p><strong>Q: What if the recipient doesn't respond?</strong></p><p>A: If the recipient doesn't respond within 30 days, the transfer request will be automatically denied.</p><hr><p><em>This article applies to standard developer team transfers. Different processes may apply for teams using Discord Premium App Subscriptions or other monetized features.</em></p>
