<h1 id="h_01JABJ6250SWSC6ZP1P3CW8QGD">What is Spark: Hero Tactics?</h1>
<p><strong>Spark: Hero Tactics</strong> is an Activity developed by <a href="https://discord.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Discord</a>. Compete in fast-paced card battles on a 3x4 grid with players from around the globe. Cast creatures, constructs, and powerful spells to smash enemy fortresses. Craft your deck, upgrade unique heroes, and unlock their ultimate powers. Compete in seasonal challenges, earn rewards, and rise in the ranks. With endless quests and evolving strategies, every match is a new adventure!</p>
<h1 id="h_01JABJ6CQMHQSHYF22J00RRTYK">How to Start Spark: Hero Tactics</h1>
<p><span style="color: #2e3338;">Spark: Hero Tactics is available on the Discord desktop app, iOS and Android mobile apps, and browser app. There are many different ways you can launch Spark: Hero Tactics in Discord for you and your friends! Whether in a server’s voice channel, in a direct message (DM), or a group message, you can check out <a href="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/26593412574359" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">How to Use Apps</a></span><span style="color: #2e3338;"> for instructions on how to start any Activity.</span></p>

<h4 id="h_01JABW5BHJT512186X8R2P0A70" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;">Example of Spark: Hero Tactics Gameplay</span></h4>
<h2 id="h_01JABJ5GZX9H696KJQFKSEDRWF">The Basics</h2>
<p>Spend <strong>Spark</strong>, a magical resource, to cast <strong>Creatures</strong> and <strong>Spells</strong> in fast paced battles on a grid-based board. Use your Hero’s<strong> Ultimate</strong> for powerful effects! The fortress you are defending, and the one you are attacking have different Health and Damage stats depending on the <strong>Hero</strong> chosen. Demolish your opponent’s <strong>Fortress</strong>, and defend your own to win!</p>
<h2 id="h_01JABJ5GZX223A02FT8CER1XNN">Building a Deck</h2>
<p>Select a Hero, each with unique and powerful Ultimates. Then select <strong>15 Creatures, Spells </strong>or<strong> Construct cards</strong> to add to your deck. You can have up to 3 copies of the same card in a deck.</p>
<h2 id="h_01JABJ5GZX1HYFX8Y40BWWCY9G">Battling, Spark &amp; Cards</h2>
<p>In battle, Spark generates and is used to cast Creature or Spell cards. Each Creature and Spell has a different Spark cost. </p>
<p>Cast Creature cards on your Fortress to summon them. Friendly and opponent creatures will move and attack on their own. Creatures have their own Health and Attack stats, which can be boosted when combined with Spells, Heroes, Ultimates, and other cards on the board.</p>
<p>Spells have different Spark costs, and require a target to use (friendly or foe). Spells have one-time effects on targets, such as increasing or reducing Health or Attack stats, boosting creature speed, and much more.</p>
<p>Strategize with different combinations of Heroes, Creatures, and Spells to battle your way across the board, defend your Fortress, and bring the enemy Fortress to zero heath to win! For more information, check the <strong>How To</strong> section inside the game’s settings menu, and see <a href="#h_01JABKG9NM3M8KEDZP4WX7YVCP">Game Terminology</a> below.</p>
<h2 id="h_01JABJ5GZX24SSFEM7Q8TE0B4X">Leveling Up</h2>
<p>Here's how you can grow stronger and dominate the battlefield:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Player XP</strong>: Battle to earn XP and <strong>Power</strong>! Win matches to gain Power and increase your <strong>Rank</strong>. At the end of each season, claim rewards based on your achieved Rank. As you level up, unlock rewards such as card packs, copper, and gold.</li>
<li>
<strong>Discover New Locations</strong>: With each level gained, you’ll unlock new Locations, discovering more powerful cards and facing formidable opponents. </li>
<li>
<strong>Hero XP:</strong> Earn Hero XP through battles. As your Heroes level up, you’ll earn cosmetic upgrades, stat increases, ability upgrades, and valuable resources! </li>
<li>
<strong>Level Up Cards</strong>: Level up your cards to earn cosmetic upgrades and resources. Use resources to expand your card collection, unlock new Heroes, or power  up your existing ones!</li>
</ul>
<p>The more you play, the stronger you become. So gear up, strategize, and conquer the Spark universe!</p>

<p><strong><span style="color: #2e3338;">Q: Is there an age requirement?</span></strong></p>
<p><span style="color: #2e3338;">A: There is currently no specific age requirement for Spark: Hero Tactics beyond the general age requirements for using Discord’s services, as described in our </span><a href="https://discord.com/terms#2" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Terms of Service</a><span style="color: #2e3338;">. This includes our </span><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/4410339366295-Paid-Services-Terms" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Paid Services Terms</a><span style="color: #2e3338;">, which apply to any purchases you make through Discord. Accordingly, if you are under 18 (or the legal age of majority in your place of residence), you need your parent’s or guardian’s permission to make in-app purchases.</span></p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #2e3338;">Q: How do I anonymize my display name and avatar in Spark: Hero Tactics</span></strong></p>
<p><span style="color: #2e3338;">A: After completing the tutorial, your first Competitive battle will prompt you to choose how you’d like to be displayed in-game. Select the </span><strong><span style="color: #2e3338;">Anonymize</span></strong><span style="color: #2e3338;"> option to receive a random avatar and display name. If you do not select the Anonymize option, your display name and avatar from your Discord User Profile settings will be shown. You can adjust this setting at any time in the Activity’s </span><strong><span style="color: #2e3338;">Settings</span></strong> <strong><span style="color: #2e3338;">menu</span></strong><span style="color: #2e3338;">.</span></p>
<div class="info-box"><span style="color: #2e3338;"><strong>NOTE</strong>: Friendly mode doesn't offer anonymization since you will be playing with people in the same space you’re using the Activity. In this mode, your display name will be your Discord display name, and your avatar will either be your Discord avatar or the one you’ve set within Spark: Hero Tactics.</span></div>
<p><strong><span style="color: #2e3338;">Q: If I make purchases in Spark: Hero Tactics on one server, will they be available when I play Spark: Hero Tactics on different servers?</span></strong></p>
<p><span style="color: #2e3338;">A: Yes, purchases you make in Spark: Hero Tactics are tied to your Discord account for use in the Activity wherever you play it. This means any items you buy while playing on one server will be available to use when you play on any other server with the same Discord account. </span></p>
<p><strong><span style="color: #2e3338;">Q: Can I trade my cards in, or trade them with a friend?</span></strong></p>
