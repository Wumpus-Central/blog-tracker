<h1 id="h_01F51PQ0RA5SKPWAABV4FW2K2K">What is Poker Night?</h1>
<p>The Poker Night Activity is a Texas hold 'em style game we developed here at Discord. You can play with up to<strong> 7 players total</strong> per game (you + 6 others), and have up to 17 additional spectators max.</p>
<h2 id="h_01J86WBQYATKN44DM97VCQBBF2">Starting Poker Night In Discord</h2>
<p>There are many different ways you can launch Poker Night in the Discord app for you and your friends! Whether in a server’s voice channel or in a direct message, you can check out <a href="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/26593412574359" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">How to Use Apps</a> for instructions on how to start any Activity.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HRDC5A10DQSHVQQPWG01PMKY"><span style="color: #434343;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Before starting any Discord Activity on Desktop, please make sure to turn on Hardware Acceleration in User Settings &gt; Advanced.</span></h3>

<h2 id="h_01HRDC6B7FXFAVCVY8CR1VX0FC">How to Play Poker Night</h2>
<h3 id="h_01HRDC6B7FTEK7QM5NQ6S7KP2R"><span style="color: #434343;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">These instructions may look slightly different on mobile devices. </span></h3>
<p>1. After launching the game, you can tap on <strong>Casual </strong>(located on the board) to change the game type to <strong>Showdown </strong>or stick to <strong>Casual</strong>, or tap on <strong>Join Table </strong>to start the game.</p>
<p><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502258213271" width="624" height="345"></p>
<p>2. Here is a breakdown of each game mode.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Casual: </strong>No limitation to your number of buy-ins (play as much as you want). Bots allowed. Winnings reset when you leave the table. Customizable Table settings.</li>
<li>
<strong>Showdown: </strong>Buyins are from limited Chip Reserve. No bots allowed. Winnings distributed to your Chip Reserves. Compete with your Server for the top spot on the Showdown Letterboard.You can learn more about Showdown in the section below.</li>
</ul>
<p>Once you’ve decided on which game mode to play, tap on <strong>Join Table.</strong> For this example, we’ll be playing <strong>Casual</strong>.</p>
<p><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502289147159" width="624" height="345"></p>
<p>3. You can invite other players or tap on <strong>Add Bot</strong> in the top left to add bots to the table.</p>
<p>4. If you want to remove a bot from the table, hover over their profile avatar and tap on it when you see the <strong>X </strong>appear.</p>
<p><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502258213783" width="261" height="104"></p>
<p>5. If you want to change any of the game settings, tap on <strong>Edit Table Settings</strong> in the bottom right. After deciding on your settings, tap on <strong>Change Settings</strong> to save.</p>
<p>6. Here you can change the the following settings:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Turn Timer: </strong>How much time each player has for their turn. Minimum of 15 seconds can be selected up to 90 seconds per turn.</li>
<li>
<strong>Starting Bank: </strong>Amount of currency a player starts with. You can start with a minimum of $100 up to $10,000.</li>
<li>
<strong>Big Blind: </strong>The minimum buy-in bet for all players. You can have a minimum of $5 up to $500.</li>
</ul>
<p><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502258214039" width="511" height="439"></p>
<p>7. After everyone (including the bots) joins the table and you’re happy with your game settings, you can tap on <strong>Start Game</strong> to start.</p>
<p>8. Each player starts with 2 cards each round, and you can <strong>Call</strong> (or <strong>Check</strong>), <strong>Fold</strong>, or <strong>Raise</strong> the bid.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Call (or Check): </strong>Match the current bid or current raise</li>
<li>
<strong>Fold: </strong>Discard your hand and forfeit the current pot.</li>
<li>
<strong>Raise:</strong> Raise the current bid higher</li>
</ul>
<p><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502258214295" width="624" height="355"></p>
<p>9. After each player places their bets for the round, you can either show your cards or fold, depending on your hand. Once everyone shows their cards or folds, the round ends.</p>
<p>10. The game continues until someone wins all of the money from the pot at the end of a round.</p>
<p><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502289148311" width="624" height="351"></p>
<p>11. If you’re unsure of how to play the game while in the middle of playing, you can also tap on the <strong>cogwheel</strong> in the top right corner and then tap on <strong>How To</strong> for a guide in-game.</p>
<p><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502289148823" width="624" height="352"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="h_01GRYW20YR3GAYSZKQ608GTAQH">What Is The Poker Shop?</h2>
<p>In the latest update in February 2023 we replaced the Gift Tokens feature with a new in-game Poker Shop where you can claim free content packs or purchase content packs to customize your gameplay with stickers, card backs, dealer skins, and themes. You'll use real currency to purchase anything in the Poker Shop.</p>
<p><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502289149207" alt="update-poker-night-poker-shop.png" width="510" height="466"></p>
<h2 id="h_01GRYW28FMQB3QEPZRCT3GM8H2">What Are Content Packs?</h2>
<p>Content Packs are bundles of in-game cosmetic content with a theme. For example, the Lucky Stars Content Pack has dealer skins, stickers, and card backs.</p>
<p>Some Content Packs will be free to claim while others require purchasing with real currency.</p>
<p><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502258215063" alt="updated-poker-night-activity-example.png"></p>
<h2 id="h_01GRYW2F4CT3GQ8E76DN09KG9F">What Is Showdown Mode?</h2>
<p>In Showdown Mode players must buy in using funds from their chip reserve. When leaving the game, winnings are deposited into the player's chip reserve. The chip reserve is used to rank players in the same server on the Showdown Leaderboard.</p>
<p>The winner of the previous Showdown Leaderboard Season gains the crown to be worn for the duration of the current Season.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HRDC5A10QEATKH2PF8A0J2Z7">Note: In an update in February 2023, levels were reset and are no longer associated with Showdown Mode rankings. The level is now tied to the user, and will be displayed at all times as a number next to their player avatar.</h3>
<h1 id="h_01F4ZHYENSF3VFJR8BEMF8ZPC2">Poker Night FAQ</h1>
<p><strong>Q: Why is Discord adding games into voice channels?</strong></p>
<p>A: We started Discord because we are all about bringing people together around gaming. As Discord has grown, we've seen more and more people use Discord to come together around games. For instance, we’ve seen people use Discord to not only play League but also to hang out and play more casual, social games with friends like <em>Jackbox</em> and <em>Among Us</em>.</p>
<p><strong>Q: What is Poker Night?</strong></p>
<p>A: Poker Night is a Texas hold 'em style game with up to 7 other players.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Is there an age requirement?</strong></p>
<p>A: Yes, you must be at least 18 years old to launch or play Poker Night.</p>
<p><strong>Q: How do I play?</strong></p>
<p>A: To start a game, first join one of the voice channels. Once there, you should see the Activity Launcher (it looks like a rocket ship) in the voice channel controls. Click the Activity Launcher to open the Activity Shelf," and then select "Poker Night".'</p>
<p><strong>Q: If I buy something in Poker on one server, will my purchases transfer when Poker is played on other servers?<br></strong></p>
<p>A: Yes, whatever you buy when playing on a server will be usable in Poker Night on any other servers that have Poker Night unlocked. What you buy is associated with your Discord user account.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Are there any paid features in the game?</strong></p>
<p>A: We offer a couple of paid extras in the game, but they are all centered around adding customization and themes. These extras do not change the flow of the game or add any external benefit outside of being cosmetic and fun.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Where can I report bugs and problems with the game?</strong></p>
<p>A: Check out our Bug Report article to access the form here: <a href="https://dis.gd/bugs" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://dis.gd/bugs</a> Make sure to follow the form when submitting a report for any issues you experience with Activities.</p>
<p><strong>Q: I bought something in the Activity, can I get a refund?</strong></p>
<p>A: If you purchased content in Poker Night, any purchases you made in Poker Night from February 1, 2022 to October 3, 2022 will be refunded as <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/4418993678999" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">we move activities to other servers</a>. We do not offer refunds for other In-App Purchases, as listed in our <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/articles/360012668071" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Refund Policy</a>.</p>
