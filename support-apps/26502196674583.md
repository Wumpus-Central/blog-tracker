<h1 id="h_01FB7GSDX29HY45M5SCMK2YBYK">What is Letter League?</h1>
<p>Letter League is an Activity that we've developed here at Discord. Letter League is a game where you and your friends take turns placing letters on a shared game board to create words in a crossword-style. Spelling words with high earning letters and placing letters on special spaces earn players more points, so get your dictionaries and thesauri ready!</p>
<h1 id="docs-internal-guid-e34cbe31-7fff-f85b-f509-29e7349c4bb2">Starting Letter League In Discord</h1>
<p id="docs-internal-guid-e6c69d7e-7fff-c181-7952-d192b8501787">There are many different ways you can launch Letter League in the Discord app for you and your friends! Whether in a server’s voice channel, in a direct message (<strong>DM</strong>), or a group message, you can check out <a href="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/26593412574359" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">How to Use Apps</a> for instructions on how to start any Activity.</p>

<h1 id="docs-internal-guid-40d965eb-7fff-fe2a-2021-eb828a441fce">How to Play Letter League</h1>
<h3 id="docs-internal-guid-b3af4c01-7fff-1dc5-74c2-c35d85e9187a"><span style="color: #434343;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">These instructions may look slightly different on mobile. </span></h3>
<h3 id="h_01HRQVJBGHZHSBN0908FD429DQ"><span style="color: #434343;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Note: When playing with more than 20 people you may experience some technical issues. For the best experience, we recommend games with up to 6 players. </span></h3>
<p>1. After launching the game, you can adjust your game settings by selecting the <strong>hamburger</strong> icon in the upper right-hand corner and the <strong>cogwheel</strong> button to navigate to <strong>Edit Game Settings</strong>. </p>
<p>While there, you can select the length, scoring, and the timer settings you would like to set for your game.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span id="docs-internal-guid-fa741d3d-7fff-599e-1ae3-454d8db45f5f"><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502196671127" width="611" height="344"></span></p>
<p id="docs-internal-guid-ffdda9f2-7fff-7287-15ec-10e00e04484a">2. You can also choose between two different scoring settings: </p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Wild</strong>: The default setting, when a letter is multiplied it keeps its improved score. Letters will be colored in to show which multipliers have been applied to them, so the next time someone scores big you can use those new and improved letters for yourself. </li>
<li>
<strong>Classic</strong>: Letters each have a point value in the lower right. When you play a word, you score the points of all the letters you used. Play letters on multiplier squares to get higher scores!  </li>
</ul>
<p>For this example, we will be playing <strong>Wild</strong></p>
<p>3. Once the game begins, use the set of letters located near the bottom to create words by selecting a tile and then selecting its destination or dragging it there.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span id="docs-internal-guid-73cf82fd-7fff-c72d-8d23-9aed6f5ae731"><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502196671511" width="623" height="350"></span></p>
<p id="docs-internal-guid-dedc4b61-7fff-de0e-3819-a89bf648eaf7">The numbers on each tile represents the amount of points you would score when placing those letters on those tiles. You can increase your scoring by placing your letters on squares that improve your word’s total score. </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span id="docs-internal-guid-f87c0a14-7fff-3543-0c42-fe01ff1460f6"><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502196671639" width="348" height="174"></span></p>
<p id="docs-internal-guid-302a9df4-7fff-4b63-0085-d8cc8319c7b2">4. If a player is unable to form a valid word, they can either swap their tiles by pressing the <strong>Swap</strong> button located above their set of letters, or they must pass their turn by selecting the <strong>Pass Turn </strong>button located underneath the timer.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span id="docs-internal-guid-d341b0d2-7fff-80fb-bb0a-509a7251ecac"><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502163921687" width="609" height="342"></span></p>
<p id="docs-internal-guid-bd2ae634-7fff-9530-444f-e060c326a2c8">5. The player with the most points at the end of the game wins!</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span id="docs-internal-guid-3c333fea-7fff-d0b3-65ae-3b9e05b58956"><img src="https://support-apps.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/26502196671895" width="612" height="343"></span></p>
<p id="docs-internal-guid-72fcf911-7fff-053e-53f7-69bb2ea31e67">6. Need a refresher of how to play the game? Press the <strong>hamburger icon </strong>in the top right corner and then tap on the <strong>instructions</strong> icon to view the <strong>Game Info</strong>.</p>

<h1 id="h_01F4ZHYENSF3VFJR8BEMF8ZPC2">Letter League FAQ</h1>
<p><strong>Q: Why is Discord adding games into voice channels?</strong></p>
<p>A: We started Discord because we are all about bringing people together around gaming. As Discord has grown, we've seen more and more people use Discord to come together around games. For instance, we’ve seen people use Discord to not only play <em>League</em> but also to hang out and play more casual, social games with friends like <em>Jackbox</em> and <em>Among Us</em>.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Is there an age requirement?</strong></p>
<p>A: There is no age requirement for Letter League.</p>
<p><strong>Q: How do I play?</strong></p>
<p>A: To start a game, first join one of the voice channels. Once there, you should see the Activity Launcher (it looks like a rocket ship) in the voice channel controls. Click the Activity Launcher to open the Activity Shelf, " and then select "Letter League".'</p>
<p id="docs-internal-guid-4c38edd6-7fff-0b52-a51d-73e6000160c5"><strong>Q: How many players can play Letter League?</strong></p>
<p>A: There can be more than 20 players within a game. However, you may experience some technical issues. We recommend games up to 6 players for the best experience.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Where can I report bugs and problems with the game?</strong></p>
<p>A: You'll be able to submit a bug report by going to <a href="https://dis.gd/bugreport" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://dis.gd/bugreport</a></p>
<p>For instructions on how bug reports should be submitted, please see our Help Center article: <a href="https://dis.gd/bugs" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://dis.gd/bugs</a> </p>
<p><strong>Q: How do I view/delete my Activities on Discord data? What data is tracked?</strong></p>
<p>A: You can view data for Discord-developed Activities like <em>Letter League</em> through the <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/articles/360004027692" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Discord Privacy and Safety settings</a>. To enhance the gameplay experience, Letter League accesses and temporarily stores the following user data once a user joins the Activity:</p>
<ul>
<li>Discord ID</li>
<li>Avatar</li>
<li>Username</li>
</ul>
<p>User data is stored so long as the Activity remains active, even if the user leaves the Activity. </p>
<p>Once all users leave the Activity and it is determined the Activity has ended, the user data is deleted and no longer retained by Letter League</p>
