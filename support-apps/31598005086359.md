<h1 id="h_01JV3DS14CHR9SY4R12Z73D8CP">What is Wordle?</h1>
<p>Wordle is a daily word guessing game from The New York Times, and is now officially an Activity on Discord (licensing and permissions from The New York Times).</p>
<p>Wordle puzzles change daily, the puzzle seen in the Discord Activity is the same one you would see if playing on <a href="https://www.nytimes.com/games/wordle/index.html?partnership=discord" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">The New York Times Games website</a> or app, and has the same correct answer for that day.</p>
<p>For example, if today's word is CRAFT on The New York Times Wordle website, then that would also be the word of the day in the Discord Activity. The next day, there will be a new puzzle.</p>
<h1 id="h_01JV3DTV60EKV39E0Y1ZAXERGX">How to Start Wordle in Discord</h1>
<p>Wordle is available on the Discord desktop app, browser app and mobile apps.</p>
<p>We created a Discord slash command to make launching Wordle on Discord easier! Just type /wordle in any chat, hit Enter or click the /wordle command from the list, and Enter again to start Wordle in Discord for you and your friends!</p>

<h1 id="h_01JV3EDTDNM1KY7MDMK3G0XDC5">Frequently Asked Questions</h1>
<p><strong>Q: Who can play Wordle on Discord?</strong><br>A: The Wordle Activity is accessible on Discord mobile and desktop clients.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Do I have to have a New York Times subscription to play Wordle on Discord?</strong><br>A: No, at this time it is free to play the Wordle Activity on Discord.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Will my stats on Discord sync with Wordle on NYT Games?</strong><br>A: No, at this time there is no integration. You must play on <a href="https://www.nytimes.com/games/wordle/index.html?partnership=discord" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">The New York Times Games website</a> or the <a href="https://www.nytimes.com/crosswords/apps?partnership=discord" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">NYT Games app</a> for personal stats. They will not sync between NYT Games and the Discord Wordle Activity.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Is there an age requirement?</strong><br>A: There is no age requirement for the Wordle Activity on Discord.</p>
<p><strong>Q: How do I turn off notifications from Wordle?</strong><br>A: Open the settings menu by clicking on the gear icon in the header. Then toggle to enable the “Mute Daily Reminders” setting. You will still get summaries of daily Wordle activity, but they will not show up as notifications.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Where can I report bugs and problems with the game?</strong><br>A: You can submit a bug report about this Discord Activity by going to <a href="https://dis.gd/bugreport" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://dis.gd/bugreport</a><br>For instructions on how Discord bug reports should be submitted, please see our <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500006052822-How-to-Report-a-Bug" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Help Center article</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Q: How do I view/delete my Activities on Discord data? What data is tracked?</strong><br>A: You can view data for Discord-developed Activities like Wordle through the <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360004027692-Requesting-a-Copy-of-your-Data" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Discord Privacy and Safety settings</a>. To enhance the gameplay experience, Discord Activities access and temporarily store the following user data once a user joins the Activity:</p>
<ul>
<li>Discord ID</li>
<li>Avatar</li>
<li>Username</li>
</ul>
<p>For Activities played in a single session, user data is stored so long as the Activity remains active, even if the user leaves the Activity. Once all users leave the Activity and it is determined the Activity has ended, the user data is deleted and no longer retained.</p>
<div class="warn-box">Important: Deleting your data will remove all of your progression across these Discord-developed activities.</div>
<p><strong>Q: My question isn't listed, what do I do?</strong><br>A: If all is lost, please submit a ticket to our support team at <a href="https://dis.gd/contact" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://dis.gd/contact</a></p>
