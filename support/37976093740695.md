Choose the appropriate steps for your Windows version. If you are on Windows 10 (N or KN), follow the Windows 10N flow. Otherwise, if you are on Windows 11 (N or KN), follow the Windows 11 N flow.</p><p><span id="docs-internal-guid-4f853f96-7fff-8ec3-f5dd-f060cb0da7f1">2.
