<h1 id="h_01JW9DS8TQEXBSJ2M19ERSH8HK">How to Delete a Discord Account</h1>
<div class="info-box">Discord account deletions take 15 days to process. Our Support Team is unable to speed up this process.</div>
<h2 id="h_01JW9DV4RZZYC5SX5KTFGKBCDD">Desktop/Browser App</h2>
<p>1. Go to your <strong>User Settings</strong> by pressing on the <strong>cogwheel icon</strong> in the bottom left corner of the Discord app.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32402585441943" alt="user-settings-cog-icon-delete-account-flow.png"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JW9DWHM4932BV9XBZ68M50FJ" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">Where to find the User Settings cogwheel icon on desktop/browser</h4>
<p>2. Ensure you’ve selected the My Account tab in the left-side menu.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32402556796567" alt="my-account-tab-delete-account-flow.png" width="537" height="392"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JW9DXMGZNW35FMF6BWH326GN" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">Example of the My Account tab under User Settings</h4>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">3. At the bottom of the <strong>My Account</strong> page, select <strong>Delete Account</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32402585445655" alt="delete-account-button-delete-account-flow.png"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JW9DYXECGB162G1TSCHWNGFW" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">Example of the Delete Account button to delete your account</h4>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">4. If you haven’t done so already, you must transfer the ownership of all the servers you own or delete those servers you own. You will not be able to delete your Discord account until you have done so.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32402585447191" alt="server-owner-notif-for-deleting-account.png"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JW9E04QTKSKM88SHPR0ZA5KT" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">Example of the message you’ll receive if you haven’t transferred or deleted servers you own</h4>
<div class="book-box">Check out <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/articles/216273938" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">this article</a> on how to transfer ownership of a server. Alternatively, you can learn how to delete a server <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/articles/213595197" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.</div>
<p>5. Once your ownership of your servers has been transferred, you can return to <strong>User Settings &gt; My Account</strong> and select <strong>Delete Account</strong> once again.</p>
<p>6. Enter your account’s password. If you have multi-factor authentication enabled for this Discord account, you must enter the required authentication as well.</p>
<p>7. Press on <strong>Delete Account</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32402556802839" alt="final-step-to-deleting-your-account.png"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JW9E5ERPKZ0MNPJ614VVF9XV" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">Example of the delete account window where you’ll enter your password</h4>
<h2 id="h_01JW9EPZMJG2ZQ009HSP1PXERG">Mobile App</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">1. Tap on your <strong>profile avatar</strong> in the bottom right corner of the mobile app.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32402585454231" alt="mobile-profile-avatar-delete-account-flow.png" width="438" height="65"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JW9E70Z5QW96CGSA1JHMFWK6" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">Example of where to access your profile through the mobile app</h4>
<p>2. Tap on your <strong>profile avatar</strong> again or the <strong>gear icon</strong> in the upper right corner of the app to open <strong>Settings</strong>.</p>
<p>3. Tap on <strong>Account</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32402556806167" alt="account-tab-mobile-delete-account-flow.png" width="223" height="446"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JW9E8ESVCEF97YJ8W0PQSTND" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">Account tab example which is found under User Settings</h4>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">4. Scroll to the bottom of the page and press on <strong>Delete Account</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32402556809751" alt="delete-account-button-mobile-delete-account-flow.png" width="219" height="437"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JW9E9EWEWWNHJ9B8XBB11J53" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">Example of where to locate the Delete Account button</h4>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">5. If you haven’t done so already, you must transfer the ownership of all the servers you own. You will not be able to delete your Discord account until you have done so.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32402585459351" alt="mobile-notif-for-server-owner-delete-account-flow.png" width="423" height="195"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JW9EANNJB171MN2Z4FH2Z121" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">Example of the message you’ll receive if you haven’t transferred or deleted servers you own</h4>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">6. Once your ownership of your servers has been transferred, you can go back to your User Settings by pressing on your <strong>profile avatar &gt; profile avatar again or gear icon &gt; Account</strong> and select <strong>Delete Account</strong> once again.</p>
<p>7. Enter your account’s password. If you have multi-factor authentication enabled for this Discord account, you must enter the required authentication as well.</p>
<p>8. Press on <strong>Okay</strong>.</p>

<h1 id="h_01JW9EEAAC44N47KSJBAHM14T9">What Happens After I Delete My Discord Account?</h1>
<p id="docs-internal-guid-b43cabd1-7fff-41e4-3de8-700223b9bbc2">When you delete your Discord account, the following will occur:</p>
<ul>
<li>Once you confirm that you would like to delete your account, you will be immediately logged out of your Discord account.</li>
<li>Your Discord account will be in a “pending deletion” state for <strong>15 days</strong> from the moment you confirm that you want to delete your account.</li>
<li>Once the 15-day period ends, your Discord account will be deleted from our system and it is <strong>irreversible</strong>.</li>
<li>You will not receive notifications or friend requests.</li>
<li>You will lose access to all your servers, direct messages, group messages, and purchases.</li>
<li>Your subscriptions like Nitro and/or Server Boosts will be cancelled.</li>
<li>Any Discord server you owned under your deleted Discord account will continue to exist but with a different server owner you elect.</li>
<li>Your account will be anonymized across all servers and messages.</li>
</ul>
<div class="tip-box">If you would like to cancel the deletion of your Discord account, you must restore your account within the 15-day period before your account is permanently deleted. You can learn more about how to restore your account <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/articles/115003114091" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.</div>
<h1 id="h_01JW9EH3TCP15GNRZFT1WYMP1Y">Having Trouble Deleting Your Discord Account?</h1>
<p>If you’re having issues deleting your Discord account, here are a few things to keep in mind:</p>
<ul>
<li>If you are unable to initiate the process of deleting your Discord account within the Discord app, you can <a href="http://dis.gd/support" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">reach out to our Support Team</a> for assistance on deleting your account.</li>
<li>If you are unable to access your Discord account and you suspect that your account has been compromised so you would like to delete your account, please reach out to our <a href="https://dis.gd/hackedaccount" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Support Team</a>.</li>
<li>If you log into your account and restore it after you initially confirmed the deletion of your account, the account deletion has been cancelled and the account will not be deleted.</li>
<li>If your account has been deleted because it was banned, disabled, or actioned on by Discord, you cannot restore it. You may submit an <a href="https://dis.gd/request" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">appeal</a> on your account to our Trust &amp; Safety team.</li>
</ul>
<div class="warn-box">If your Discord account has been inactive and unused for 2 years or more, it will be scheduled for deletion. You may be notified of this pending deletion through the email or phone number associated with the account. You can learn more about inactive account deletions here.</div>
