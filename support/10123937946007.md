</div><h1 id="h_01H4RGTAJV5YNE3SC3N08TQ1WC">What are Age-Restricted Commands?</h1><p><span style="color: #2e3338;"><span data-darkreader-inline-color="">Age-restricted commands are </span></span><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500000368501" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">commands from apps</a><span style="color: #2e3338;"><span data-darkreader-inline-color=""> that may contain age-restricted content, and may only be used by people 18 or older. To ensure that Discord is safe for everyone, our policies require that developers label adult content as age-restricted and not distribute it to people under the age of 18.</span></span></p><h1 id="h_01H4RGTEVTR5J9WNW76GXSHHG9">Who can use Age-Restricted Commands?</h1><p><span style="color: #2e3338;"><span data-darkreader-inline-color="">Users who are 18 and older can access them in servers and DMs.</span></span></p><h1 id="h_01H4RGTK8RJ9CBHZNEKHTZ8FKR">Where can they be used?</h1><p><span style="color: #2e3338;"><span data-darkreader-inline-color="">They can be used within </span></span><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/115000084051" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">age-restricted channels</a><span style="color: #2e3338;"><span data-darkreader-inline-color=""> in servers, and in DMs after a user has opted-in to use them.</span></span></p><h1 id="h_01H4RGTQD3JJFZG5PY4999360F">How do I access Age-Restricted Commands?</h1><p><span style="color: #2e3338;"><span data-darkreader-inline-color="">In servers, you can use these commands only within </span></span><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/115000084051" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">age-restricted channels</a><span style="color: #2e3338;"><span data-darkreader-inline-color="">. Not all apps have age-restricted commands, but if an app in your server has them, they’ll only be accessible within those designated channels.</span></span></p><p><span style="color: #2e3338;"><span data-darkreader-inline-color="">In DMs, age-restricted commands are turned off by default. If an app contains age-restricted commands, you can access them by enabling the toggle within User Settings &gt; Privacy&gt; Server Privacy Defaults.</span></span></p><h1 id="h_01H4RGTZFBRYQNBBJ181E1ZG8T">Help! I’m over 18 years old, but can’t use Age-Restricted commands!</h1><p><span style="color: #2e3338;">If you are over the age of 18 and have been locked out of an age-restricted channel, or are unable to use age-restricted commands in DMs, you can appeal by following the instructions in this </span><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041820932-Help-I-m-old-enough-to-use-Discord-in-my-country-but-I-got-locked-out" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Help Center article</span></a><span style="color: #2e3338;">.</span></p>
