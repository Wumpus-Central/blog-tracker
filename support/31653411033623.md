<h1 id="h_01JT6Y3EPJ4TG69CAETEV0QHAY">Frequently Asked Questions</h1>
<p><strong>Q: Do I need to do anything special to claim this trial? </strong><br>A: If you opened your desktop app and saw a notification saying ‘You’ve got Nitro’, then you don’t need to do anything else to redeem the one-week trial. You’re good to go. Enjoy!</p>
<p><strong>Q: Do I need a payment method?</strong></p>
<p>A: No payment method is required to start your free trial.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Can I cancel my trial before it ends?</strong></p>
<p>A: You will not be able to <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/19580873036695-How-to-Cancel-your-Nitro-Subscription" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">cancel</a> your initial trial once it begins. Your Nitro benefits will remain active until the end of the trial period. </p>
<p>During the bonus week trial, you can cancel anytime. You can manage your subscription settings through your <strong>User Settings &gt; Nitro</strong>. If you don’t cancel your subscription during your bonus week trial, your subscription will convert to a paid subscription for the plan selected. Learn more in the cancellation guide <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/19580873036695-How-to-Cancel-your-Nitro-Server-Boosting-or-other-Discord-Subscriptions" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>. </p>
<p><strong>Q: Can I claim the free Nitro trial on my mobile device?</strong></p>
<p>A: No, the trial can only be claimed through the Discord desktop app or web client. However, once you've claimed the trial on desktop, you'll be able to enjoy all Nitro features across both desktop and mobile devices.</p>
<p><strong>Q: I don't see the trial offer or did not receive it. Why?</strong></p>
<p>A: This trial is subject to eligibility restrictions. If you do not see the trial on your account, this indicates that you are not eligible at this time. Please note that the Discord support team is not able to bypass eligibility restrictions.</p>
<p><strong>Q: What happens if I purchase a subscription during my free trial?</strong></p>
<p>A: Your paid subscription will begin immediately, replacing your trial. Any remaining trial days will be forfeited. For instance, if you purchase a subscription on day three of your trial, you'll immediately switch to the paid subscription instead of using your remaining four trial days.</p>
<p><strong>Q: What happens if I complete a Quest and earn Nitro during my one-week trial? </strong><br>If you earn a one-day Nitro reward from completing a Quest while your one-week trial is active, the one-day reward will replace your remaining trial time. This means your trial will end, and you'll receive only the one-day of Nitro instead of your remaining trial days.</p>
<p><strong>Q: What is this free Nitro trial I received?</strong></p>
<p>A: Discord is currently offering a special Nitro trial to selected users. This trial grants you access to Nitro features without requiring a payment method upfront. Users who upgrade to a paid subscription at the end of their trial will receive a bonus one-week trial upon upgrading.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Is this free Nitro trial legitimate?</strong></p>
<p>A: Yes! This is an official Discord trial offer that is being granted to a limited number of eligible users.</p>
<p><strong>Q: How long does the trial last?</strong></p>
<p>A: You can enjoy your Nitro perks for the duration of your trial period. When the trial ends, you'll have the option to either subscribe to Nitro and receive a bonus one-week free or let the trial expire.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Can Discord Support give me access to the trial?</strong></p>
<p>A: No, our Support team cannot manually grant access to this trial. </p>
<p><strong>Q: How do I manage my subscription after the initial trial?</strong></p>
<p>A: When the initial trial ends, you'll have the option to either subscribe to Nitro and receive a bonus one-week trial or let the initial trial expire. If you sign up for the bonus one-week trial, your trial will convert to a paid subscription at the end of this additional week, unless cancelled before the end of the trial. You can manage your subscription settings at any time during the bonus trial through your <strong>User Settings &gt; Nitro</strong>. Learn more in the cancellation guide <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/19580873036695-How-to-Cancel-your-Nitro-Server-Boosting-or-other-Discord-Subscriptions" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Q: What happens if I cancel during the bonus trial period?</strong></p>
<p>A: If you sign up for the bonus trial by adding a payment method after your first week, you can cancel any time during the additional week trial. If you cancel, you will continue to access your Nitro trial for the remainder of the trial, but will lose access to Nitro perks at the end of the trial. </p>
<p><strong>Q: What features are limited during the free trial?</strong></p>
<p>A: During the free Nitro trial period, there are some limitations compared to a paid subscription:</p>
<ul>
<li>The two Server Boosts and the 30% off any additional future Boost purchases that normally come with paid Nitro are not included</li>
<li>You cannot purchase additional Server Boosts</li>
<li>You will not receive the Nitro Badge</li>
<li>You will not be eligible for the <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/22567595858327-Share-Nitro" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Share Nitro offer</a>
</li>
</ul>
<p>These features and options will become available once your trial ends and you subscribe to a paid Nitro plan.</p>
