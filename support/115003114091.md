<h1 id="h_01JW9AX8YFZWCECG0NMTN1H405">How to Restore a Discord Account</h1>
<div class="warn-box">
<strong>Important:</strong> You have 15 days for the deletion process to be completed. If you wish to restore your account before deletion, it must be done before 15 days. You can learn more about this by checking out the How to Delete your <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/articles/212500837" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Discord Account Help Center article</a>.</div>
<p>1. Open the Discord app on your desktop, in a web browser, or through the Discord mobile app.</p>
<p>2. Enter your account’s login information.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32402053545879" alt="desktop-login-restore-deleted-account.png" width="392" height="211"> <img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32402099207959" alt="mobile-login-restore-deleted-account.png" width="213" height="232"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JW9B05X0QPF07KVTPH8D57AQ" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">Example of the log in window on desktop/browser (left), example of the log in window on mobile (right)</h4>
<p>3. The Discord app will let you know that this account is pending deletion. If you wish to stop the deletion of your Discord account, select <strong>Restore Account</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32402053548311" alt="restore-discord-account-button.png" width="387" height="175"> <img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32402053550871" alt="final-confirmation-of-account-restoration-from-deletion.png" width="275" height="279"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JW9B1F2RAY8M0HYBKAB3D50F" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">Example of the Restore Account button on desktop/browser (left), Example of the Restore Account button on mobile (right)</h4>
<p>4. The app will then take you to the Discord account you just logged into and restored!</p>
<h1 id="h_01JW9B2C2N4CBFTYW43PSAJCRP">Having Trouble Restoring Your Discord Account?</h1>
<p>If you are having issues restoring your Discord account, there are a few things to remember:</p>
<ul>
<li>If you initiated the deletion of your Discord account, the account is in a “pending deletion” state for 15 days. Once that period of time has passed, the Discord account has been deleted from our system and cannot be restored.</li>
<li>If you try to log into your account that you initiated for deletion, the 15 day period has passed, and the Discord app does not dictate that your account is pending deletion, your account has already been deleted from our system and cannot be restored.</li>
<li>If you are trying to restore the Discord account you initiated for deletion but cannot remember your account’s password login, you will have to reset your account’s password.</li>
<li>If you previously contacted the Support Team to delete your account but now wish to cancel the account deletion, please follow up with our <a href="http://dis.gd/support" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Support Team</a>.</li>
<li>If you are trying to restore your Discord account but you are having trouble with logging into your account to cancel its deletion and you suspect that your account has been compromised, please reach out to our <a href="https://dis.gd/hackedaccount" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Support Team</a>.</li>
<li>If your account has been deleted because it was banned, disabled, or actioned on by Discord, you cannot restore it. You may submit an <a href="https://dis.gd/request" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">appeal on your account to our Trust &amp; Safety team</a>.</li>
</ul>
