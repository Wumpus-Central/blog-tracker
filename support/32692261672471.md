</div><h1 id="h_01JXATXQ3E20MQJ5GHE1AVXS4K">What is App Tracking Transparency for iOS?</h1><p>App Tracking Transparency is Apple's privacy framework that requires apps to obtain user consent before accessing device identifiers used for tracking. This helps users make informed choices about their privacy while using iOS apps.</p><h1 id="h_01JXATXQ3E1PJ03K7J3JWVRK4K">Why Does Discord Need this Permission?</h1><p>Discord uses tracking to enhance your experience with Quests and mobile features. When you allow tracking:</p><ul>
<li>You'll receive more personalized Quests that match your interests</li>
<li>Discord can better support game developers and businesses that rely on ads for discovery</li>
<li>The platform can continue providing a high-quality, free service to users</li>
</ul><h1 id="h_01JXATXQ3EZ83KBQEWP3S0W6MT">Your Privacy Choices</h1><p>When prompted about tracking, you have two options:</p><ul>
<li>
<strong>Allow:</strong> Discord can access your device identifier to provide personalized Quests and experiences</li>
<li>
<strong>Ask App Not to Track:</strong> Discord will respect your choice and not access your device identifier</li>
</ul><p>You can modify this choice at any time through your <strong>Privacy &amp; Security </strong>settings or <strong>App </strong>settings in your iOS device’s Discord app settings. Whether you allow tracking or not, you'll still have full access to Discord's core features.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><strong><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32692261667223" width="291" height="607"></strong></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JXATXQ3ETW79YVSF1N9KB2Y1"><span style="color: #666666;">App Tracking Transparency Notification</span></h4><div class="info-box">

</div><h1 id="h_01JXATXQ3ECSB3FYFHMV5XQB8G">Frequently Asked Questions</h1><p><strong>Q: Does allowing tracking affect my Discord messaging experience?</strong></p><p>A: No, your core Discord features including messaging, voice chat, and server participation remain fully functional regardless of your tracking choice.</p><p><strong>Q: Can I change my tracking preferences later?</strong></p><p>A: Yes, you can modify your tracking preferences at any time through your iOS device settings under <strong>Privacy &amp; Security &gt; Tracking</strong>.</p><p><strong>Q: What exactly is a device identifier?</strong></p><p>A: A device identifier is a unique code that helps identify your device. For iOS, this is known as the Identifier for Advertisers (<strong>IDFA</strong>) and is used to deliver personalized experiences and advertising.</p><p><strong>Q: Will Discord sell my data if I allow tracking?</strong></p><p>A: No, Discord does not sell your personal data. The tracking permission is used solely to enhance your in-app experience and provide relevant Quests and features.</p><p><strong>Q: How does tracking improve my Quests experience?</strong></p><p>A: Tracking helps Discord understand your interests and activity patterns, allowing it to suggest Quests that are more likely to appeal to you and match your gaming preferences.</p><p><strong>Q: Is my personal information safe if I allow tracking?</strong></p><p>A: Yes, Discord maintains strict security measures and follows privacy regulations to protect your personal information, regardless of your tracking preferences.</p>
