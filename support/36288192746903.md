</div><h1 id="h_01K9ZW6HMK4H3VR4NJATMS5YTQ">What is Wishlist?</h1><div class="exp-box">
<strong>Important</strong>: Wishlist is available to all users on desktop and browser. On mobile, it's currently an experimental feature available to a limited number of users.</div><p>Wishlist helps you curate a collection of Shop items you'd love to receive from your Discord community. When you add items to your Wishlist, they appear on your profile where friends can easily browse and gift them to you.</p><div class="info-box"><span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Note</strong>: Items you already own, Orb exclusive items, and bundles cannot be added to your wishlist.</span></div><h1 id="h_01K9ZW6HMN2BTFG97KVYB4VA4Z">Adding Items to Your Wishlist</h1><p>Ready to start building your Wishlist? Here are two easy ways to add items:</p><h2 id="h_01K9ZW6HMN3P42RDQZSVS3XY47">From the Shop</h2><p>Browse the Shop and hover over any item card to see the <strong>Wishlist</strong> button or visit an item's details page and press the <strong>Wishlist</strong> button to add it.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36327146671127" alt="Adding Shop Items to Your Wishlist from the Shop Tab
" width="800" height="556"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K9ZW6HMQT3WEBED64MRPX1AC"><span style="color: #666666;">Adding Shop Items to Your Wishlist from the Shop Tab</span></h4><h2 id="h_01K9ZW6HMRZ6K4R1RMEPTDCQKV">From Your Profile</h2><p>1. Press your <strong>avatar</strong> icon in the bottom-left corner, then select your <strong>avatar</strong> again to open your full profile.</p><p>2. Select the <strong>Wishlist</strong> tab, then press <strong>Add item</strong> in the upper-right corner. </p><p>3. You'll be taken to the Shop where you can browse items. Press the <strong>Wishlist</strong> button on any item you'd like to add.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36327146671511" alt="Adding Shop Items to Your Wishlist from Your Profile
" width="800" height="556"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K9ZW6HMTAVVD81ZGQQNH1NR4"><span style="color: #666666;">Adding Shop Items to Your Wishlist from Your Profile</span></h4><h1 id="h_01K9ZW6HMVTBHZXNFVCD9CNGW0">Managing Your Wishlist</h1><h2 id="h_01K9ZW6HMV6PJ1BQKH0Q04XS8B">Removing Items</h2><p>Changed your mind about an item? No problem—you can remove items from your Wishlist anytime.</p><p><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-x-large"><strong>From the Shop</strong></span></p><p>Browse to the item in the Shop and press the <strong>Wishlist</strong> button again to remove it.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36327115617175" alt="Removing Items to Your Wishlist in the Shop Tab

Open your Profile and go to the Wishlist tab.</p><p>2. Find the item you want to remove.</p><p>3. Press the <strong>Wishlist</strong> button in the upper-right corner of the item card.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36327146672151" alt="Removing Items to Your Wishlist in Your Profile Board" width="800" height="662"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K9ZW6HMZVYN6D86T0B1W77Z8"><span style="color: #666666;">Removing Items to Your Wishlist in Your Profile Board </span></h4><h2 id="h_01K9ZW6HMZ68HZ9R0V14HV2RRJ">Privacy Settings</h2><p>Your Wishlist is public by default so friends can easily see what to gift you. If you'd prefer to keep your Wishlist private, you can adjust your privacy settings.</p><p>1. Open your <strong>Profile</strong> and navigate to the <strong>Wishlist</strong> tab.</p><p>2. Press the <strong>ellipsis</strong> icon [<span style="color: #434343;"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36288200983191" width="30" height="24"></span>] in the upper-right area of your <strong>Wishlist</strong>.</p><p>3. Toggle <strong>Public Wishlist</strong> off to make your Wishlist private.</p><div class="info-box">

</div><h1 id="h_01K9ZW6HN3S6GE1RRGVC96K0AY">Gifting Shop Items from a Friend's Wishlist</h1><p>Want to surprise someone special? You can browse your friends' Wishlists and send them gifts directly from their profile. It's the perfect way to celebrate birthdays, special occasions, or just show appreciation to your community members.</p><h2 id="h_01K9ZW6HN30B5K4TGVCDR5T4A5">Finding a Friend's Wishlist</h2><p><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-x-large"><strong>Through the Full Profile</strong></span></p><p>1. Right-click your friend's <strong>avatar</strong> anywhere in Discord—from a server, direct message (<strong>DM</strong>), or your friends list.</p><p>2. Select <strong>Profile</strong> to open their full profile.</p><p>3. Navigate to the <strong>Wishlist</strong> tab —you'll see all the items they've saved.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36327115621015" alt="Viewing a Friend's Wishlist from Their Profile" width="800" height="593"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K9ZW6HN5DR9JR8GDGBPZC1WP"><span style="color: #666666;">Viewing a Friend's Wishlist from Their Profile</span></h4><div class="info-box"><span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Note</strong>: You can only view Wishlists that are set to public. If you don't see a Wishlist tab on someone's profile, they may have set their Wishlist to private or haven't created a Wishlist yet.</span></div><p><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-x-large"><strong>Through a User’s Profile in a Direct Message</strong></span></p><p>1. Open a direct message with the friend you want to send a gift to.</p><p>2. In their Profile, you’ll see the Wishlist section. </p><p>3. Select an item or view their full Wishlist to browse all their saved items and select the perfect gift. </p><h2 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K9ZW6HN73V7RHGW7KTSS3F9E"><strong><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36327115622679" alt="Viewing a Wishlist from Your Friend’s DM Profile" width="762" height="473"></strong></h2><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K9ZW6HN7RKRXZQRGQZ0N9RKW"><span style="color: #666666;">Viewing a Wishlist from Your Friend’s DM Profile</span></h4><p><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-x-large"><strong>Through the Gift Icon in a Direct Message</strong></span></p><p>1. Open a direct message with the friend you want to send a gift to.</p><p>2. Press the <strong>gift </strong>icon [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36288200986135" width="24" height="24">] located to the far right in the chat bar.</p><p>3. Browse their <strong>Wishlist</strong> in the <strong>Send a Gift</strong> window—you'll see all the items they've saved, making it easy to choose the perfect gift.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36327146673943" alt="Viewing a Friend's Wishlist within Send a Gift Window" width="800" height="539"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K9ZW6HN9X15V84A99DY5J8DM"><span style="color: #666666;">Viewing a Friend's Wishlist within Send a Gift Window</span></h4><h2 id="h_01K9ZW6HN95MPETZZ7ZRB3GA34">Sending a Shop Gift</h2><p>Once you've found the perfect item on your friend's Wishlist, sending it is simple!</p><p>1. Select the item you want to gift from their Wishlist or select the <strong>Send Gift </strong>button.</p><p>2. Add a personal message, a sound effect, and an emoji confetti (all optional) to make your gift extra special.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36327146674327" alt="Example of Sending a Gift from a Friend’s Wishlist" width="800" height="593"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K9ZW6HNB5A4YR68XMXRSWP09"><span style="color: #666666;">Example of Sending a Gift from a Friend’s Wishlist</span></h4><p>3. Press <strong>Next</strong>, then complete the purchase to send it on its way.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img class="wysiwyg-image-resized" style="aspect-ratio: 624/452; width: 100%;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36288192736919" alt="Next Button" width="624" height="452"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K9ZW6HNBKYT0J1AJAM2XR0J7"><span style="color: #666666;">Next Button</span></h4><p>Your friend will receive a direct message about their gift, and the item wil be marked with a checkmark on their Wishlist since they now own it!</p><h1 id="h_01K9ZW6HNC10TYWBEX51RYB4D4">Frequently Asked Questions</h1><p><strong>Q: I can't find my Wishlist on my profile. How do I see it?</strong></p><p>A: To view your Wishlist, open your full Discord profile by selecting your <strong>avatar</strong> in the bottom-left corner, then selecting your <strong>avatar</strong> again. Once your profile opens, select the <strong>Wishlist</strong> tab to see all your saved items.</p><p><strong>Q: How many items can I add to my Wishlist?</strong></p><p>A: Currently, there's no set limit to the number of items you can add to your Wishlist. Add as many favorites as you'd like!</p><p><strong>Q: Can I reorder items on my Wishlist?</strong></p><p>A: Items appear on your Wishlist in the order you added them, with the most recent additions showing first. You can't manually reorder items, but you can remove and re-add items to change their position.</p><p><strong>Q: What types of items can I add to my Wishlist?</strong></p><p>A: You can add most Shop items including avatar decorations, profile effects, and themes. However, items you already own, Orb-exclusive items, and bundles cannot be added to your Wishlist.</p><p><strong>Q: Is Wishlist available on mobile?</strong></p><p>A: Wishlist is currently available on desktop and browser only. We're testing the feature before expanding to mobile platforms.</p><p><strong>Q: Can friends see when I add items to my Wishlist?</strong></p><p>A: No, adding items to your Wishlist is private. Friends can see your Wishlist when they visit your profile (if it's set to public), but they won't receive notifications when you add new items.</p><p><strong>Q: What happens to my Wishlist items if I receive them as a gift?</strong></p><p>A: When someone gifts you an item from your Wishlist, it's automatically removed from your Wishlist since you now own it. This helps keep your Wishlist up to date!</p><p><strong>Q: Will I know if someone views my Wishlist?</strong></p><p>A: No, there's no notification when someone views your Wishlist. Friends can browse your Wishlist privately to find the perfect gift for you.</p><p><strong>Q: If I make my Wishlist private, can I still add items to it?</strong></p><p>A: Yes! You can continue adding and managing items on your Wishlist even when it's set to private. Only the visibility to others changes.</p><p><strong>Q: Can I hide specific items on my Wishlist while keeping it public?</strong></p><p>A: No, Wishlist privacy is all-or-nothing. If your Wishlist is public, all items on it are visible. If you want to keep certain items private, you'll need to remove them or set your entire Wishlist to private.</p><p><strong>Q: Can I gift someone an item that's not on their Wishlist?</strong></p><p>A: Yes! You can <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/17162747936663-Shop-FAQ#h_01J4T4Z6G3GCXP9S76GKGBR98G" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">gift any Shop item</span></a> to friends, not just items from their Wishlist. The Wishlist simply makes it easier to know what they'd love to receive.</p><p><strong>Q: What if I accidentally send a gift to the wrong person?</strong></p><p>A: Shop gift purchases are final and cannot be refunded or transferred. Double-check the recipient before completing your purchase. Please visit our <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360012668071-Refund-Policy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Refund Policy</span></a> for more information.</p><p><strong>Q: Can someone regift an item I sent them?</strong></p><p>A: No, items cannot be regifted. Once received, items are bound to that account.</p>
