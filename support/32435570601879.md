<h1 id="h_01JWV7MNG88YZKSJEB9PXR7X4X">What is ExitLag?</h1>
<p><a href="https://www.exitlag.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">ExitLag</a> is an AI-powered technology designed to optimize internet connections for online gaming by reducing latency and minimizing ping. The platform uses real-time traffic analysis to route game data through the most efficient network paths, bypassing common connection issues that cause lag. Supporting thousands of popular games, ExitLag operates on a subscription model and offers a free trial period so users can experience its effectiveness before purchasing.</p>
