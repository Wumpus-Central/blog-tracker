<h1 id="h_01JYPTA5T9MEP9H445CNZDEPD4">How to Report an Unrecognized Charge Using ‘Other’</h1>
<p>When filling out the support form by choosing the “<strong>Other</strong>” category, please review the following:</p>
<p><strong>If the charges are on your own Discord account:</strong></p>
<p>Review our <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360045765792-Reporting-Unauthorized-Discord-Transactions" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Reporting Unauthorized Discord Transactions</span></a> article to see if any situations match your case. If none apply, follow these steps: </p>
<p>1. In the <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=360000118612"><span class="wysiwyg-underline" style="color: #1155cc;">support form</span></a>, select “<strong>I have a question/issue regarding an unauthorized transaction</strong>” under “<strong>Type of Billing Issue</strong>”. </p>
<div class="tip-box">
<span style="color: #434343;">If you're unsure which Discord account was charged, select "</span><strong><span style="color: #434343;">I am unsure the Discord account associated with the unauthorized charges</span></strong><span style="color: #434343;">"</span> <span style="color: #434343;">under “</span><strong><span style="color: #434343;">Type of Issue.</span></strong><span style="color: #434343;">”</span> <span style="color: #434343;">This helps us route your request to the appropriate support team.</span>
</div>
<p>2. Select “<strong>Other</strong>” under “<strong>Type of Issue</strong>” and “<strong>Yes”</strong> under “<strong>The unauthorized charge(s) are on my Discord account</strong>”. </p>
<p>3. Under “<strong>I am writing in from the email address associated with my Discord account</strong>”:</p>
<ul>
<li>Select “<strong>Yes</strong>” if you can still access that email address</li>
<li>Select “<strong>No</strong>” if you cannot access that email address</li>
</ul>
<div class="warn-box">
<span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Important</strong>: If your Discord account was suspended and you would like to appeal the suspension, please also submit an appeal ticket through our Appeal form:</span> <a href="https://dis.gd/report" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">https://dis.gd/report</span></a><span style="color: #434343;">. </span>
</div>
<div class="warn-box">
<span style="color: #434343;">Any refunds outside </span><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360012668071-Refund-Policy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">our standard refund policy</span></a><span style="color: #434343;"> may result in disabling your Discord account per our <a href="https://discord.com/terms#10:~:text=Our%20right%20to%20terminate" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">terms</a>.</span>
</div>
<h2 id="h_01JYPTA5TC9YJ8WKVWXA0X1RAW">What to Include in Your Support Request</h2>
<p>To help us resolve your case quickly, please provide:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Transaction date and amount</strong></li>
<li>
<strong>Payment method</strong> (credit card, PayPal, Cash App, Venmo, etc.)</li>
<li>
<strong>Payment identifier</strong> (last 4 digits of credit card, PayPal invoice ID, $Cashtag, Venmo User ID, etc.)</li>
<li>
<strong>Screenshots</strong> of the Discord charges on your bank statement</li>
</ul>
<div class="info-box"><span style="color: #434343;">Any missing payment information may delay the resolution of your case.</span></div>
