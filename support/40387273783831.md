</div><h1 id="h_01KRY71A41QZ5M1N1K34BNW05T">Available Nitro Perks</h1><p>Active Game Pass subscribers who have linked their Discord account to their Game Pass account will receive these three Nitro perks every month:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e750251385e3129f286cef38a7247169e">
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/22225719947543-Discord-Quests-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Quest Orbs Multiplier</span></strong></a>: Earn Orbs faster when you complete Discord Quests. Starting May 8, 2026, your Quest rewards get a 1.2x boost automatically.</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e6213cbec7c68f7b5d5b871a29c7b23fd">
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/37097755077015-Monthly-Orbs-Drop-for-Nitro-Members-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Monthly Orbs Drop</span></strong></a>: Get 250 Orbs every month. No action needed, the extra Orbs will automatically show up in your total Orbs balance of eligible accounts. </li>
<li data-list-item-id="eb72a95ca91c7ee057391adbe4990e89f">
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/17162747936663-Shop-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Shop Discount</span></strong></a>: Enjoy the same discount on Discord Shop purchases that Nitro Standard subscribers get.</li>

If you're located in one of these regions, you won't be able to use the Nitro benefit with Game Pass. For info on Xbox Game Pass markets, see: <a href="http://xbox.com/regions" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">xbox.com/regions</span></a> </p><p><strong>Q: Which Game Pass plans are eligible?</strong> </p><p><strong>A: </strong>All Xbox Game Pass plans are eligible. Whether you're on Game Pass Essential, Premium, or Ultimate, you'll receive the same set of Discord Nitro perks.</p><p><strong>Q: Do I need a Nitro subscription to get these perks?</strong> </p><p><strong>A: </strong>No. These perks are included with your Game Pass subscription and don't require a Nitro membership. If you're curious about what else Nitro offers beyond these perks, you can check out the full list of Nitro benefits in our <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/115000435108-What-are-Nitro-Nitro-Basic" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">What are Nitro &amp; Nitro Basic?</span></a> Help Center article.<br><br><strong>Q: I already have Nitro. Can I still claim these perks?</strong></p><p>A: If you already have an active Nitro subscription, you're already receiving these perks (and more) through Nitro! This benefit is designed for users who don't currently have an active Nitro subscription.</p><p><strong>Q: How do I get my monthly Orbs drop?</strong> </p><p><strong>A: </strong>Your 250 Orbs are deposited automatically each month to eligible accounts if your Xbox and Discord accounts remain linked and your Game Pass subscription is active. Once you subscribe to Nitro, you will receive your next monthly Orbs drop 7 days after your subscription begins. </p><p><strong>Q: When will my first monthly Orbs drop occur? </strong></p><p>A: If you have Xbox Game Pass and connected your account to Discord before May 27, 2026, your first Orbs drop will arrive sometime within 30 days of May 27. If you connect your Xbox Game Pass account to Discord after May 27, 2026, your first Orbs drop will arrive within a week of connecting.</p><p><strong>Q: How can I see when I get my next monthly Orbs Drop?</strong></p><p>A: Head to <strong>Settings </strong>&gt; <strong>Connections</strong>, go to your Xbox connected account section, and select “Learn more” in the banner. A modal will pop up that shows how many more days until your next monthly Orbs Drop. </p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KRY71A58E8S5XX6A5C8XS4MA"><span style="color: #666666;"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/40562838599575" width="624" height="215"></span></h4><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KRY71JMXT8DAVCMHNBX988JQ"><span style="color: #666666;"><strong>Learn more </strong>option under Xbox Game Pass</span></h4><p><strong>Q: Will my Quest multiplier stack with other bonuses?</strong> </p><p><strong>A: </strong>The 1.2x Orb Quest multiplier applies to your Quest rewards for Quests after May 8, 2026 automatically. If you subscribe to Nitro at a later time, you will receive this perk through your Nitro tier benefits instead. If you have Nitro and Xbox Game Pass, the multiplier will not stack. </p><p><strong>Q: What happens if my Game Pass subscription ends?</strong> </p><p><strong>A: </strong>If your Game Pass subscription lapses or you unlink your accounts, your perks will deactivate. Any Orbs you've already earned will remain in your account.</p><p><strong>Q: I linked my accounts but don't see my perks. What should I do?</strong> </p><p><strong>A: </strong>Make sure your Game Pass subscription is active and that your accounts are fully linked in Discord's <strong>User Settings &gt; Connections</strong>. If everything looks correct and your perks still haven't appeared, try unlinking and re-linking your Xbox account. If the issue persists, reach out to our Support team through <a href="http://dis.gd/contact" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">dis.gd/contact</span></a> </p>
