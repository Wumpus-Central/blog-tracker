<p>Discord's age-restricted server designation helps ensure that communities centered around adult content remain accessible only to users aged 18+, as indicated in our <a href="https://discord.com/guidelines" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Community Guidelines</a>. Servers can either be self-designated as 18+ by their owners or classified as age-restricted by Discord when they focus primarily on mature themes or content. </p><div class="toc-box">

An age-restricted designation is only necessary if the majority of the server’s content is focused on 18+ content.</span></p><div class="info-box">
