<li data-list-item-id="eab1a9c078ddee539c439cdb0e7b6b5a7">

<li data-list-item-id="ebcc68db6481b1d6e547d56657fe41047">

<li data-list-item-id="ec7897b1e8c6763243c367e846e99f643">

</ul><h2 id="h_01KGGRCA753V87ZGQWQ7X6EB6J">As a Plan Member</h2><p>As a Nitro Squad plan member, you don’t need to manage the group plan. This section covers what to know when joining or leaving a Nitro Squad, along with a few limitations for members.<br><br><strong>Joining a Nitro Squad:</strong></p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="eab4382b7b6e809114a5d9b647b9b4b61">You do not need to be Discord Friends with the Nitro Squad plan owner to be invited to their Nitro Squad</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e2eff6318bd6a26b6e4dc5ca5153123b4">You will <strong>immediately forfeit</strong> all remaining subscription time if you have an existing Nitro, Basic, or Classic individual plan or free trial when you join a Nitro Squad as a member. You do not receive proration credit or refunds.</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e2e2f2c2854dfbe53f2534952e4bd9b4d">If you have an existing Nitro plan with utilized Server Boosts and you join a Nitro Squad, you will not be able to keep your Server Boosts from your original plan</li>
<li data-list-item-id="ef7419a17403240f9afd99622976cb7da">After joining a plan, you will not be able to purchase a separate Server Boost subscription.</li>
</ul><p><strong>Leaving a Nitro Squad:</strong></p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="eede387b04e8631f87a252fcf3e65ab43">You can remove yourself from a plan at any time</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e3e60b3a6f41a64938014a6cf22045f50">You lose access to Nitro immediately upon leaving</li>
<li data-list-item-id="eae00034f031fb1b907a4c160e3801856">Your 12 month window must finish before joining another group plan, including the one you just left</li>
</ul><div class="warn-box">
<strong>Important:</strong> During this Beta, we do not support downgrading from Nitro Squad to an individual Nitro plan. To switch, the plan members must leave the plan and the plan owner must cancel their Nitro Squad plan, then subscribe to an individual plan.</div><h1 id="h_01KGGREGBBC4H11W3NP3SVDGE7">Frequently Asked Questions</h1><p><strong>Q: How does badge progression work?</strong></p><p>A: Each member gets their own badge streak, starting when they create or join the plan:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e62d15281110788e1717a893d125763e1">
<strong>Nitro Badge</strong>: 7-day grace period when entering and exiting the plan to maintain streaks</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e0e2cb785928d0641b9ed3eae146d8096">
<strong>Server Boost Badge</strong>: 3-day grace period</li>
<li data-list-item-id="ea1b3e9d9f271fd827f67cf17cb7647ad">Plan owners who upgrade and members who join an existing plan keep their streak if they already have a Nitro subscription.</li>
</ul><p><strong>Q: Who can purchase additional Server Boosts?</strong></p><p>A: Plan members cannot purchase additional Boosts. Plan owners can purchase additional Boosts at the discounted rate.</p><p><strong>Q: What happens if I'm removed from a plan unexpectedly?</strong></p><p>A: We’ll send you an email to let you know. Your access to Nitro perks ends immediately, except for your Nitro Badge which follows a 7-day grace period. You must also wait for the end of your 12 month cooldowns before joining any other Nitro Squad plan.</p><p><strong>Q: When does the 12 month eligibility period begin?</strong></p><p>A: The 12 month period begins when you create or join a Nitro Squad.</p><p><strong>Q: Can I rejoin the same Nitro Squad plan if I leave?</strong></p><p>A: No. Once you leave, you cannot rejoin it. You must also wait for the end of your 12 month cooldown before joining any other Nitro Squad plan.</p><p><strong>Q: What happens to my existing Nitro subscription if I join a Nitro Squad plan?</strong></p><p>A: You will immediately forfeit any remaining time on your individual subscription with no proration or refund. Make sure you're ready to switch before accepting an invitation.</p><p><strong>Q: What happens if the plan owner's account is banned?</strong></p><p>A: It depends on the type of ban:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e473487d6c94464b95b522ce83762970c">
<strong>Limited Access or Temporary bans</strong>: No impact on the plan or members</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e6cfa75bae9dab7d4f1809fa12471d9ed">
<strong>Permanent ban</strong>: No change until the next renewal date, then the plan auto-cancels. All members will receive email notification when the subscription is cancelled</li>
</ul><p><strong>Q: What happens if my account as a member is banned?</strong></p><p>A: It also depends on the type of ban:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e14955f4f4c0b863febb078833c70ac1b">
<strong>Limited Access or Temporary ban</strong>: No impact on your membership</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e8606ff48de9f256d422eb4119e4fb3e7">
<strong>Permanent ban</strong>: You are automatically removed from the group immediately and the plan owner will receive an email notification</li>
</ul><p><strong>Q: What happens if a plan owner or plan member blocks each other?</strong></p><p>A: If either the plan owner or the plan member blocks the other, the member will be removed from the plan. This applies whether the owner blocks the member or the member blocks the owner.</p><p><strong>Q: Can I have a Nitro Squad membership and a Server Boost subscription at the same time?</strong></p><p>A: Only if you are the plan owner. Plan members cannot have additional Server Boost subscriptions while on a Nitro Squad plan. </p><p><strong>Q: What if I'm locked out of my account and can't leave/end a plan?</strong></p><p>A: Please reach out to our Support team through our support form <a href="https://dis.gd/support" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.</p><p><strong>Q: I'm a current subscriber with an existing offer. Can I still join Nitro Squad?</strong></p><p>A: Yes, you can have an existing subscription from one of our Nitro promotions and still be eligible for Nitro Squad.</p><p><strong>Q: Will my billing date change if I upgrade to Nitro Squad?</strong></p><p>A: Yes, your billing date will be adjusted based on when you create or join the Nitro Squad plan.</p><p><strong>Q: I can’t find the plan invite - where is it?</strong></p><p>A: Your Nitro Squad invite can be found in your direct message from the person who manages the plan. If you can’t find it, it might be because you’ve never DM’d the person before. Check Message Requests tab under Direct Messages</p>
