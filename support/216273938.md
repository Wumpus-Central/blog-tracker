<p>You can seamlessly transfer ownership to another server member when your community outgrows your availability, you need temporary management, or you're ready to pass the torch to dedicated leadership.</p><p>Transferring server ownership gives you the flexibility to ensure your server continues thriving, whether it's a permanent transition or temporary arrangement.</p><div class="toc-box">
<p><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-x-large"><strong>What this article covers:</strong></span></p>

</ul>
</div><h1 id="h_01JYP6RQKZTMTQXKT3XY4Z1EPR">Desktop/Browser App Instructions</h1><p>1. Select the server you want to transfer ownership to, then press the <strong>Members </strong>tab.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/33069563954455" width="307" height="207"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JYP6RQM3W5RRDG43C97Z8ET1"><span style="color: #666666;">Server Settings option</span></h4><p>2. Right-click on the server member you want to transfer the server to and press <strong>Transfer Ownership</strong>.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><strong><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/33069563957015" width="174" height="469"></strong></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JYP6RQM6W0M5F1R3C517JZF2">
    <span style="color: #666666;"><strong>Transfer Ownership</strong> option</span>
  </h4><p>3. After pressing <strong>Transfer Ownership</strong>, you’ll see a confirmation window. Select the toggle to confirm and acknowledge the transfer, then select <strong>Transfer Ownership</strong>. If you have MFA enabled on your account, you’ll be requested to enter your MFA code.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><strong><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/33069563958935" width="431" height="352"></strong></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JYP6RQM9ZT2EAWM8685Z2ZC4"><span style="color: #666666;">Transfer Ownership confirmation box</span></h4><p>4. Now your server will have a new owner! After completing the transfer, you will still be a member of the server. The access you retain will depend on the permissions tied to your new role.</p><h1 id="h_01JYP6RQMB2ZE98B405DCTEDWY">Mobile App Instructions</h1><p>1. Select the server you want to transfer, then press <strong>Server Settings</strong>.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/33069563961367" width="354" height="769"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JYP6RQMDN34PNC13JP0P6KR4">
    <span style="color: #666666;"><strong>Settings</strong> button for a server</span>
  </h4><p>2. Press on <strong>Members </strong>to access your server’s members list.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/33069562060439" width="392" height="850"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JYP6RQMFPQ3DK58QX5B4FB1P">
    <span style="color: #666666;"><strong>Members</strong> button to access your server’s members list</span>
  </h4><p>3. Tap the server member you want to transfer the server to, then select <strong>Transfer Ownership</strong>.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><strong><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/33069563966487" width="327" height="709"></strong></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JYP6RQMJXR6EF966YSG70KFW">
    <span style="color: #666666;"><strong>Transfer Ownership</strong> confirmation</span>
  </h4><p>4. You should then see a confirmation screen. After confirming your acknowledgement, press <strong>Transfer</strong>. If you have MFA enabled on your account, you’ll be requested to enter your MFA code.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/33069562064663" width="357" height="775"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JYP6RQMMJCFJ9D1M5WGR5A3Z">
    <span style="color: #666666;"><strong>Transfer</strong> button confirmation</span>
  </h4><p>5. Now your server will have a new owner! After completing the transfer, you will still be a member of the server. The access you retain will depend on the permissions tied to your new role.</p><div class="warn-box">
<strong>Important:</strong> Once a server has been transferred, Discord’s Support team is not able to transfer it back.
</div>
