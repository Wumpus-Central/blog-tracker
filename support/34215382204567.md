</div><h1 id="h_01K2QG9THH32E6JF2TENGK66CZ">What is an Early Fraud Warning (EFW)?</h1><p>An Early Fraud Warning (<strong>EFW</strong>) is a notification Discord receives from your financial institution (such as your bank or card issuer) that one or more transactions made on your Discord account were reported as potentially fraudulent.</p><p>This does not always mean your account has been compromised, but it does indicate your payment method was flagged for unusual or suspicious activity.</p><p>We take these reports seriously to protect both you and our community from potential fraud and abuse.</p><h1 id="h_01K2QG9THJ1TQBFE4CG9YMN8RV">Why did I get an EFW notice?</h1><p>You may have received an EFW notice because:</p><ul>
<li>A purchase on your account was reported to Discord as fraudulent by your bank or card issuer.</li>
<li>Your payment method was reported to Discord as being used without your authorization.</li>
<li>Your account may have been compromised, and someone else made purchases without permission.</li>
<li>A billing dispute or refund request was filed with your bank instead of contacting Discord Support.</li>
</ul><h1 id="h_01K2QG9THM02ZK9YE4P3BY0W02">What happens after an Early Fraud Warning?</h1><p>There are two possible outcomes:</p><h2 id="h_01K2QGK8S9RHZ3EKY8E8PQN6AR"><span style="color: #434343;">For Low-Risk Early Fraud Warnings:</span></h2><ul>
<li>You’ll receive an email notifying you of the fraud report.</li>
<li>If the report was made in error, contact your financial institution to resolve it.</li>
<li>Additional fraud reports in the future may lead to account suspension for review.</li>
<li>Filing chargebacks or refund requests directly with your bank may also result in account suspension. For more details, review our <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/26600878251159-Chargebacks-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Chargebacks FAQ</span></a>.</li>
</ul><div class="warn-box">
<span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Important</strong>: If you have received this low-risk violation, no immediate action is required from you.</span><br><span style="color: #434343;">However, if fraud has occurred on your account or payment source, please submit a support inquiry directly to Discord. You can refer to our </span><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360045765792-Reporting-Unauthorized-Discord-Transactions" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Reporting Unauthorized Discord Transactions</span></a><span style="color: #434343;"> article for more information.</span>
</div><h2 id="h_01K2QGKHSF3W76H7MWZ872WH4V"><span style="color: #434343;">For High-Risk Early Fraud Warnings:</span></h2><ul>
<li>Your account will be suspended pending further investigation.</li>
<li>You’ll receive an email explaining the suspension and instructions for appeal.</li>
<li>To appeal, submit a request via<a href="https://dis.gd/request"> <span class="wysiwyg-underline">dis.gd/request</span></a> and select <strong>Appeals &amp; Age Update Requests </strong>under <strong>What can we help you with?</strong>.</li>
<li>Please make sure to include the following in your appeal:<ul>
<li>The reason for the fraud report</li>
<li>Evidence of withdrawal (if applicable)</li>
<li>A screenshot of the reported transaction from your billing statement (amount, date, descriptor)</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul><div class="info-box"><span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Note</strong>: Discord receipts are not accepted as proof of payment. Screenshots must be from your payment source.</span></div><h1 id="h_01K2QG9THSARHZN1FEHFZDC3FZ">How can I avoid an EFW or suspension?</h1><ul>
<li>Use payment methods that you are authorized to use</li>
<li>Keep your Discord account secure with <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/219576828-Setting-up-Two-Factor-Authentication" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)</span></a>
</li>
<li>If you have billing issues, contact <a href="http://dis.gd/billing" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Discord Support</span></a> instead of filing a dispute with your bank</li>
<li>Monitor your account activity regularly for unauthorized charges</li>
</ul><h1 id="h_01K2QG9THVD6HH7D3WQJK68WRH">What should I do if I think an EFW notice is wrong?</h1><h2 id="h_01K2QG9THV54RZMWA53N0Y1Q6R">For Low-Risk EFWs:</h2><ul><li>Contact your bank to resolve the report. If you have billing concerns, reach out to <a href="http://dis.gd/billing" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Discord Support</span></a>.</li></ul><h2 id="h_01K2QG9THV7D1XMMN7BJRH31VM">For High-Risk EFWs:</h2><ul><li>Follow the appeal process in your suspension email.</li></ul><div class="book-box">
