</div><h1 id="h_01KM6G2ME5J6JEV5ZH42MW8ZRB">What are Favorites?</h1><div class="warn-box">
<strong>Important:</strong> Favorites is currently available as a Nitro experiment on desktop and browser. Access may vary by user, and mobile support is limited to view-only.</div><p>Favorites is a feature that places all your top channels and DMs in a place that’s easy to find. Any channel or direct message you mark as a favorite will appear in a dedicated Favorites section at the top of your server list, keeping your most important conversations separate from the rest.</p><p>You can favorite channels from any server you're in, as well as DMs and group DMs — making Favorites a handy home base for everything you actually use on a regular basis. Plus, you can group these chats into custom categories to keep everything neat and tidy.</p><h1 id="h_01KM6FTQS9BVBPHCK6CQ5ZENZQ">How to Favorite a Channel or DM</h1><div class="warn-box">
<strong>Important:</strong> Favorites are view-only on mobile. All management actions (editing, adding, moving, and renaming) require desktop or browser.</div><p>You can favorite text channels, direct messages (DMs), group direct messages (GDMs), forum channels, and voice channels. While you can’t favorite specific threads, you can favorite the text channel a thread is in. </p><div class="tip-box">
<strong>Tip:</strong> Even if you disable Favorites in Settings or unsubscribe and re-subscribe to Nitro, your Favorites will still be saved.</div><p>1. Navigate to the channel or DM/GDM that you want to favorite. </p><p>2. Right-click on what you would like to favorite and then select <strong>Add to Favorite</strong>. If you have a Category set up, you can add the item to your preferred category instead.</p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KM6FTQSBCVBMTXX4QN460M3K"><span style="color: #666666;"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/39192374037783" alt="adding_channel_favorites.gif" width="1920" height="1080"></span></h4><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KM6FTQSC0X6WXTJN3EGJ2WAK"><span style="color: #666666;">Adding a text channel to Favorites</span></h4><h2 id="h_01KM6FTQSCK4WCC3KRPEQDV77C">How to Remove a Channel or DM from Favorites</h2><p>1. Navigate to your Favorites by selecting the <strong>Favorites icon</strong> [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/39192374039447" width="24" height="24">] in the top-left corner above your server list. </p><p>2. Right-click on a channel or DM/GDM and select <strong>Remove from Favorites</strong>.</p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KM6FTQSEG5SYQS4MPHZNZXC8"><span style="color: #666666;"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/39192374042135" alt="removing_channel_favorites.gif" width="1920" height="1080"></span></h4><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KM6FTQSEK7QT6R1ENE9P2AKJ"><span style="color: #666666;">Removing a text channel from your Favorites</span></h4><h2 id="h_01KM6FTQSFYPASNQZMQRX6TJVV">How to Create a Category for your Favorites</h2><p>To organize your Favorites even more, you can create a variety of Categories. Uncategorized channels will appear at the top of your favorited items.</p><p>1. While under the <strong>Favorites tab</strong> right-click and select <strong>Create Category</strong>.</p><p>2. Name your Category and then press <strong>Create Category</strong>. </p><p>3. Now when you add a channel or DM/GDM to your Favorites, you can add them into a category instead or drag your current favorites items to the category.</p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KM6FTQSGYNJTYNR9D01B7M4Y"><span style="color: #666666;"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/39192374047639" alt="image3.gif" width="800" height="450">Create a Category for Favorites</span></h4><h1 id="h_01KM6FTQSHW6GSCPNM09MHENX5">How to View your Favorites</h1><div class="tip-box">
<strong>Tip:</strong> If you didn’t enable Favorites when you received the pop-up about the experiment, you will not see the Favorites icon  [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/39192374050711" width="24" height="24">]. You will have to head to <strong>User Settings</strong> &gt; <strong>Chat</strong> and enable or disable the <strong>Show Favorites Server</strong> toggle.</div><p>To view everything in your Favorites select the <strong>Favorite icon </strong>[<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/39192374050711" width="24" height="24">]  in the top-left corner above your server list on desktop, browser, or mobile. </p><p><br> </p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/39192359272599" width="216" height="277"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KM6FTQSMEEPVX4TQ68YPRANJ"><span style="color: #666666;">Favorites icon</span></h4><h1 id="h_01KM6FTQSN6Q8906BHCFHFJREC">Frequently Asked Questions</h1><p><strong>Q: What all can I add to my Favorites?</strong></p><p>A: Text channels, voice channels, DMs, GDMs, and forum channels. Only threads can not be favorited.</p><p><strong>Q: Does favoriting a channel notify anyone? </strong></p><p>A: No, it's personal and private.</p><p><strong>Q: Is there a limit to how many items I can favorite?</strong></p><p>A: You can add up to 200 items to your Favorites. This includes any Categories you create.</p><p><strong>Q: Can I reorder my Favorites? </strong></p><p>A: Yes, you can move a favorited channel or DM, or organize them in a category.</p><p><strong>Q: Are Favorites available on mobile?</strong></p><p>A: Yes, Favorites is available on desktop, browser, and mobile. However, you can only edit Favorites from desktop/browser. </p><p><strong>Q: Can I preview Favorites if I don't have Nitro?</strong> </p><p>A: Yes! You can preview the Favorites feature in settings even without a Nitro subscription. And if you are a past subscriber and re-subscribe, your Favorites are saved. For Nitro users, Favorites are saved even if you toggle the feature off — so when you turn it back on, everything will reappear exactly as you last set it up.</p><p><strong>Q: Can I turn off Favorites from displaying?</strong></p><p>A: Yes, if you are a Nitro user you can go to <strong>User Settings</strong> &gt;<strong> Chat</strong> and enable or disable the <strong>Show Favorites Server </strong>toggle. Your Favorite will be saved even if you toggle it off or unsubscribe and re-subscribe to Nitro. </p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/39192359277207" width="624" height="137"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KM6FTQSS306TK4J8WZ3W4K08"><span style="color: #666666;">Show Favorites Server toggle</span></h4>
