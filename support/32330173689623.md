<h1 id="h_01JVZBVNC0DDG2B8AEPMDJ0XGJ">What are Account Connections?</h1>
<p><strong>Account Connections </strong>allows you to connect your favorite external apps like Spotify, Xbox, and PlayStation directly to your Discord account. This integration displays your connected accounts on your Discord user profile, sharing what you're playing, listening to, and enjoying with your Discord communities.</p>

<h1 id="h_01JVZBVNC1HYWX4BTPFN9B4B1V">How to Connect External Accounts to your Discord Account</h1>
<h2 id="h_01JVZBVNC1PGBXR6H9RQNBJ3A5">Desktop/Browser</h2>
<p>1. Head into your <strong>User Settings</strong> by pressing the <strong>cogwheel</strong> [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330173646615" width="23" height="24">] <strong>icon</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330166863255" width="357" height="52"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVZBVNC1DGM2S2NP6B15076W" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Cogwheel icon</span></h4>
<p>2. Press on the <strong>Connections</strong> tab. To view all available connections, select the <strong>View More </strong>[<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330166863895" width="21" height="20">] <strong>icon</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330166865047" width="624" height="452"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVZBVNC14YH7K1P6VW9MP347" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Connections tab under User Settings</span></h4>
<p>3. Press on any icon of the connection that you want to add. You will then be prompted to log into that connection.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330166868247" width="453" height="303"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVZBVNC152XYWB2K9JPPBD40" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">All available connections</span></h4>
<p>4. You’ll know you’ve successfully connected when you receive a <strong>Connected your [External Account] account to Discord</strong> message.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330166868631" width="421" height="304"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVZBVNC14GP5D26782M6RTQQ" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Confirmation window that your X account has been connected to Discord</span></h4>
<h2 id="h_01JVZBVNC1PWTCPDQ10AJKMXND">Mobile</h2>
<p>1. Tap your <strong>avatar</strong> icon in the bottom-right corner of the app to navigate to your <strong>profile</strong>. </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330173652503" width="550" height="96"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVZBVNC130Q5MKFVYVCN1PHJ" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Avatar icon</span></h4>
<p>2. Tap on the <strong>cogwheel</strong> [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330173646615" width="23" height="24">] <strong>icon</strong> in the top-right corner.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330173653015" width="398" height="862"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVZBVNC180NCYYBXRWW88BNE" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Cogwheel icon</span></h4>
<p>3. Press on <strong>Connections </strong>and then press on <strong>Add </strong>in the top-right corner. To add your desired connection, tap on the icon.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330166870551" width="399" height="865"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVZBVNC1E6GDGFR55C9RSWZT" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">All available connections</span></h4>
<p>4. You’ll know you’ve successfully connected when you receive a <strong>Connected your [External Account] account to Discord</strong> message.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330166871575" width="624" height="459.4940451541869"></p>

<h1 id="h_01JVZBVNC1DA516W1GZBCNJVTT">How to Remove External Accounts from your Discord Account</h1>
<div class="info-box">
<strong>Info:</strong> Discord Support is not able to remove a connection for you. You will have to log into your Discord account to remove the connection.</div>
<p>1. Head into your <strong>User Settings</strong> by pressing the <strong>cogwheel</strong> [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330173646615" width="23" height="24">] <strong>icon</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330166863255" width="357" height="52"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVZBVNC15GF27MRHTPDAAHHP" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Cogwheel icon</span></h4>
<p>2. Press on the <strong>Connections</strong> tab. To remove a connection, press on the <strong>X icon</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330173659159" width="624" height="452"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVZBVNC13A45A8RF2FYT2ZNB" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">
<span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Press </span><strong><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">X</span></strong><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color=""> to remove connection</span>
</h4>
<p>3. You’ll receive a prompt of confirmation to remove. To fully remove it, press on <strong>Disconnect</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32330173660183" width="420" height="148"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVZBVNC1VTHQBVRGZ2ARDZ2K" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Disconnect button to confirm removal of connection</span></h4>
