</div><h1 id="h_01KQJNEWVNAN7QH4CJD132QV71">What Are Game Shops?</h1><p>Game Shops let you browse, purchase, and gift in-game items directly on Discord. You can find in-game items through the Discord Shop or in a game's official Discord server, and you can gift items to friends from places you're already hanging out, like DMs and voice channels.</p><p>Games currently available on Discord:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="ef2f589c2587174185538c3b4bc68e30d">
<strong>Marvel Rivals</strong>: Marvel Rivals Shop</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e0ddccaf43ccc8c0387e841ce63bc5ef1">
<strong>Rust</strong>: Rust Shop</li>
</ul><h1 id="h_01KQJNEWVPG87A6MGGTS47408G">Where To Find In-Game Items On Discord</h1><div class="info-box">
<strong>Note: </strong>Game Shops are currently available on the Discord desktop and web apps. See the Marvel Rivals Shop and Rust Shop sections below for details on which platforms each game supports for item redemption.</div><p>There are two ways to find in-game items on Discord:</p><h2 id="h_01KQJNEWVQH83B35TH0VEMYAGG">Through the Discord Shop</h2><p>1. Navigate to the Discord Shop by selecting the <strong>Shop tab</strong> [ ] to the right of your server list.</p><p>2. Select <strong>Game Shops</strong> at the top of the Shop.</p><p>3. Browse available games and select a game to see its items.<br> </p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/40167861384855" width="795" height="252"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KQJNEWVTN14ABEY245S58793"><span style="color: #666666;"><strong>Game Shops</strong> tab in the Discord Shop</span></h4><h2 id="h_01KQJNEWVT9ZGPJV5MMFXZ23JC">Through a Game's Official Server</h2><p>1. Visit the game's official Discord server.</p><p>2. Select the <strong>Game Shop tab</strong> towards the top of the server's channel list.</p><p>3. You can now browse all available items in the Game Shop.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><em><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/40167861385239" width="953" height="677"></em></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KQJNEWVWRPWB35ABCRYGYYPE"><span style="color: #666666;">Game Shop (Example: Marvel Rivals)</span></h4><h1 id="h_01KQJNEWVWKKB83TQF6QZ2F91A">How To Purchase Items</h1><div class="info-box">
<strong>Note: </strong>The items available in each Game Shop will refresh periodically, so check back to see what's new.</div><p>1. Find the item you'd like to purchase, either through the Discord Shop or in the game's official server.</p><p>2. Select the item and review details including the price and preview.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><em><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/40167861385623" width="725" height="377"></em></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KQJP0PT6A8JS0YPRR3CWKK68">
<span style="color: #666666;">Item detail view in a Game Shop</span> </h4><p>3. Select <strong>Buy Now</strong> and follow the prompts to complete your purchase.</p><p>4. When you buy an item, it's delivered straight to your in-game inventory. If you've already linked your game account to Discord, you're all set. If not, you'll see a prompt to link your account. Press the <strong>Link Account</strong> button.</p><h1 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KQJNEWW0C86MK59RKS92M80T"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/40167851435927" width="386" height="409"></h1><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KQJNEWW0YRF2GA602Y92M22D"><span style="color: #666666;"><strong>Link Account</strong> button</span></h4><div class="book-box">
<strong>Want to learn more?</strong> <br>For more information on linking your game account to Discord, check out this <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/27893392334359-How-Games-Integrate-Discord-Powered-Features" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Help Center article</a>.</div><p>5. Follow the prompts to fully link your account. You can gift items directly from any Game Shop on Discord.</p><p>1. Browse a Game Shop and select an item you'd like to gift.</p><p>2. Select the <strong>Gift Item</strong> button.</p><p>3. You can personalize your gift with a custom message.</p><p>4. Once you're ready, complete your purchase by following the prompts and send your gift.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/40167851440535" width="681" height="519"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KQJNEWW46QXY5VRVKPJPRHJ0"><span style="color: #666666;">Gifting a Marvel Rivals Shop item</span></h4><p>5. The recipient will receive the gift in their DMs. Before claiming the gift, they should make sure their game account is linked to their Discord account. <li data-list-item-id="e9c108df9fd470740d4276e83667aa4d8">

<li data-list-item-id="ed30f8ac223ea6576a0856eb2b8583694">

<li data-list-item-id="eeea611bacd3ec29d66bf028e9d93ca56">

<li data-list-item-id="e591ca1c343f19f15238318eae9e0d588">

</ul><h2 id="h_01KQJNEWW9VBQYCG8QCRDVHXQ8">Purchases and Refunds</h2><p><strong>Q: Can I refund a Game Shop purchase?</strong> </p><p>A: Generally, purchases in Game Shops are non-refundable. However, if you're unable to claim the in-game item on a supported platform, and you attempt to claim the item within the 14-day claim period, Discord will attempt to automatically process a refund. Check the game-specific sections below for which platforms are supported.</p><p><strong>Q: How do I see what items I've purchased?</strong> </p><p>A: You can see any purchases on your Billing tab in settings in the 'Transaction History' section.</p><p><strong>Q: What happens if I receive a gift of an item I already own in the game?</strong> </p><p>A: If you attempt to claim a gifted item that you already own in-game, the gift sender will be refunded. Note that gift receivers must attempt to claim items within 14 days to ensure Discord can issue any eligible refunds.</p><p><strong>Q: Why do I see "You already own this item or it's not supported on your platform"?</strong></p><p>A: This message appears when you already own the item, your linked game account is on an unsupported platform, or you own an item that's included in a bundle you're trying to purchase. If you already own the item, you'll only be able to purchase it as a gift for someone else.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/40167851441431" width="281" height="267"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KQJNEWWC3RYZZS5CHCGF2FYC"><span style="color: #666666;"><strong>"You already own this item or it's not supported on your platform" </strong>message</span></h4><h2 id="h_01KQJNEWWC2D0G97YGZRCAA2S1">Pricing</h2><p><strong>Q: What currency do Game Shops support?</strong> <br><br>A: Prices in Game Shops may be shown in your local currency where available. Otherwise, prices are shown in U.S. dollars (USD).</p><h2 id="h_01KQJNEWWD64M48SSGRQ8V2ZYD">Gifting</h2><p><strong>Q: Can I gift items to someone who doesn't play the game?</strong> </p><p>A: Yes, you can send a gift to any Discord user. The recipient will need to link their game account to their Discord account to claim the gift. If they don't have a game account yet, they can create one and link it to Discord.</p><p><strong>Q: How do I know what my friends want?</strong> </p><p>A: You can check a friend's Wishlist on their Discord profile. Open the Wishlist tab to see which items they've saved, and gift them something right from there. You can also add items to your own Wishlist so friends know what to get you. Learn more in our <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/36288192746903-Wishlist-FAQ"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Wishlist FAQ</span></a>. </p><h2 id="h_01KQJNEWWEEHM4W2H100GX74HV">Account Linking</h2><p><strong>Q: Can I purchase from a Game Shop without linking my account?</strong> </p><p>A: Yes, you can purchase from the shop without linking your account, but you will need to link your account to claim the item. You can find the account linking entrance in <strong>Settings</strong> &gt; <strong>Billing </strong>&gt; navigate to the item you purchased &gt; <strong>Connect Account</strong>.</p><div class="warn-box">
<strong>Rust Players:</strong> If you made a purchase and didn't get your item after linking, please launch Rust and re-link your Discord account from inside the game. You can do this by clicking 'Social' in the bottom right, then 'Find your Friends on Discord'.</div><h2 id="h_01KQJNEWWFCGTTPJRND5GPAHZE">General</h2><p><strong>Q: Which games have Game Shops on Discord?</strong> </p><p>A: Game Shops are currently available for Marvel Rivals and Rust.</p><p><strong>Q: Are Game Shops available on mobile?</strong> </p><p>A: Game Shops are currently available on the Discord desktop and web apps.</p><h2 id="h_01KQJNEWWGZ6CB48FP9VBE2MD1">Marvel Rivals Game Shop</h2><p><strong>Q: Which platforms can I redeem Marvel Rivals Shop items on?</strong> </p><p>A: Currently supported on PC desktop platforms (Steam, Epic, Loading Bay) and Xbox.
