</div><h1 id="h_01JPR4VE8AKNPT1S6735SEJ2WQ">How to Change your Discord Theme</h1><div class="exp-box">
<strong>Important</strong>: We’re currently running an experiment with <strong>Server Themes</strong>, an additional perk available to select servers that allows server admins to set a custom color theme for their server with 3 Server Boosts. To configure it, go to <strong>Server Settings &gt; Server Theme</strong>. <br><br>Once enabled, members can choose whether to view the server’s theme while they’re in that server or keep using the theme set on their own device. If you have a theme set under <strong>User Settings &gt; Display &gt; Theme</strong>, you can switch between the server’s theme and your account theme at any time.</div><h2 id="h_01JPR51MJ41MNRY57T54AYH0JF">Desktop</h2><p>First, head into your <strong>User Settings</strong> by selecting the <strong>cogwheel </strong>[<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/30777700886039" width="24" height="24">], then select <strong>Appearance</strong>.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/34960610520983" alt="image39.gif"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JPR49B3618RTDM25XRB3WV73"><span style="color: #666666;">Access Theme Settings on Desktop: Open User Settings &gt; Appearance</span></h4><p>Discord offers several default theme options:<strong> Light</strong>, <strong>Ash</strong>,<strong> Dark</strong>,<strong> Onyx</strong>, and <strong>Sync with computer.</strong></p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e99a8d4896d20eefffee0d88d7b9290ff">The <strong>Light theme </strong>features a white background with dark grey text and includes an optional <strong>Dark Sidebar</strong>.</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e480b2142540cb14fcc6340af6bab1486">The <strong>Ash theme </strong>uses a mid-gray background with white text. </li>
<li data-list-item-id="eea4a5e179004aadeb6ba15a02b140c87">The<strong> Dark theme </strong>displays a dark gray background with white text.</li>
<li data-list-item-id="ed16c5766f9ba00216099c45068e6b09e">The <strong>Onyx theme </strong>presents a gray and black background with white text.</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e615634388d215a208ed299e8ade4f217">
<strong style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;">Sync with computer </strong>(known as <strong>Automatic</strong> on mobile) matches your Discord theme to your device’s appearance setting.</li>
</ul><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/34960610525591" width="894" height="552"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JPR4B2KGG5NRMQP8EEVQ6AY6"><span style="color: #666666;">Example of Discord Theme Selection Interface on Desktop</span></h4><h2 id="h_01JPR51B5D5D9JC31RHW3RXDDX">Mobile</h2><p>Tap your <strong>avatar</strong> in the bottom-right corner of the mobile Discord app, then tap the <strong>cogwheel </strong>[<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/30777700890775" width="24" height="24">] in the upper-right corner. Then, scroll down and tap on <strong>Appearance</strong>.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/34331397664023" width="298" height="621"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JPR4CA8D9HYHHB385A5SGA2K"><span style="color: #666666;">Access Theme Settings on Mobile: Open User Settings &gt; Appearance</span></h4><p>At the top, you'll find these <strong>theme</strong> options: <strong>Light</strong>, <strong>Dark</strong>, <strong>Midnight</strong>, and <strong>Automatic</strong> (which syncs with your device settings).</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e0fca6f9849b09a4119b8c804500ac160">The <strong>Light</strong> <strong>theme</strong> uses a white background with dark grey text. You can also enable the <strong>Dark Sidebar</strong> option.</li>
<li data-list-item-id="eed277623a3bebd56fe451bc48ef9c52e">The <strong>Dark</strong> <strong>theme</strong> uses a dark grey/charcoal background with white text.</li>
<li data-list-item-id="ebce075ad1b3872cfd1de41e2062fa98b">The <strong>Midnight theme </strong>presents a gray and black background with white text.</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e6d3124e11f3b9c281cb4be8aed4fe37b">
<strong>Automatic</strong> matches your Discord theme to your mobile device's appearance settings.</li>
</ul><div class="info-box">When you enable "<strong>Sync across clients</strong>" on desktop, your theme syncs across all devices (desktop, mobile, and browser). However, themes may display slightly differently on mobile.<br>For instance, if you select Ash on desktop, it will appear as Dark on mobile.</div><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/34331439063063" width="300" height="623"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JPR4F5YWJPCDJJS5R7FNMZW6"><span style="color: #666666;">Example of Discord Theme Selection Interface on Mobile</span></h4><h1 id="h_01JPR4VE8AZ96PBKN8VCWFJ41G">How to Change the UI Density</h1><p>In the <strong>Appearance</strong> menu, scroll to <strong>UI Density</strong>. Choose between <strong>Compact</strong>, <strong>Default</strong>, or <strong>Spacious</strong> to adjust the spacing around content (and navigation spacing in the desktop app). UI density doesn’t affect message density.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/30893752262551" width="778" height="541"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JPR4GDX7VAHXKTQY6S41M68C"><span style="color: #666666;">UI Density options on Desktop</span></h4><h1 id="h_01JPR4GVRS0578ZNJD7ZB21KRS">How to Change the Message Display</h1><p>In the <strong>Appearance</strong> menu, scroll to <strong>Message Spacing &gt; Chat Message Display</strong>.  You can choose between either <strong>Default</strong> or <strong>Compact</strong> to adjust the spacing specifically between message elements.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/30893752263703" width="790" height="344"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JPR4HS490MP6HZ35HETTWR9X"><span style="color: #666666;">Chat Message Display options on Desktop</span></h4><p>Choosing<strong> Compact</strong> will remove user icons for a denser chat view.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><strong><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/34331439065111" width="809" height="551"></strong></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JPR4J78GF67W71CZ8SS35HJQ"><span style="color: #666666;">Example of Light Theme with Compact Message Display</span></h4><p>Fine-tune your app's appearance using the sliders to adjust the following:</p><ul><li data-list-item-id="e7db5da4ca1c43237d9adfff38bf5f3b3">
<strong>Zoom Level: </strong>Adjusting zoom changes the size of all Discord elements, including text. The Discord desktop app offers a slider control, while browsers use their built-in zoom function. Use Ctrl/Cmd + plus (+) to zoom in or minus (-) to zoom out.</li></ul><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/31746435181719" width="761" height="502"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JT16BF5CS2C0R0R6YSGDWJQJ"><span style="color: #666666;">Example of Decreasing Zoom Level</span></h4><ul><li data-list-item-id="ee577431b9972776d4a97b25522a51e83">
<strong>Chat Font Scaling: </strong>This setting adjusts only chat text size while keeping other elements unchanged. Note that the zoom level will amplify whatever font size you choose here.</li></ul><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/31746455730199" width="779" height="515"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JT16CPDWXANV295NREQ74YKG"><span style="color: #666666;">Example of Increasing Chat Font Scaling</span></h4><ul><li data-list-item-id="eecd488fa658fb244f654a6dfee942a33">
<strong>Message Group Spacing: </strong>Increase the space between chat messages to make conversations easier to read.</li></ul><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/31746455731607" width="786" height="519"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JT16DK8N70XJB5E32V07J585"><span style="color: #666666;">Example of Decreasing Message Group Spacing</span></h4><div class="info-box">

<strong>Important</strong>: Nitro subscribers can share, preview, and apply custom themes on the desktop app and browser client, while mobile is limited to previewing and applying.</div><p>Once you've created the perfect custom theme that reflects your unique style, you can easily share it with friends on Discord! </p><p>1.
