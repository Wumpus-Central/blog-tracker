<p>Looking for a specific message or file in Discord? Discord's powerful <strong>search</strong> feature helps you quickly find conversations, media, and links across your servers—no matter how far back when they were sent.</p><p>Whether you're tracking down an important announcement, locating a shared image, or finding that perfect meme from months ago, Discord's search filters let you narrow results by user, date, channel, and content type. This guide will show you how to use Discord's search bar effectively, apply advanced filters, and find exactly what you need in seconds.</p><div class="toc-box">

</div><h1 id="h_01K9ARXQ3FYGQBG6TG4Q1MRNQ8">What is the Search feature?</h1><p><span style="color: #2e3338;">The search function incorporates a few different tools and filters to help you find what you need in the smallest, friends only server, an expansive community-wide server, or in direct messages (<strong>DM</strong>). Since Discord won't delete your messages until you decide to, you can refer to anything sent in your server as far back as when it was created.</span></p><div class="info-box"><span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Note: </strong>Server owners and admins/mods with <strong>Manage Messages</strong> permissions can delete messages within their server.</span></div><h1 id="h_01K9ARXQ3FNF2QBYG03MKZJ3TQ">How to Use Search</h1><div class="info-box"><span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Note:</strong> Search will index within your current server or DM. </span></div><h2 id="h_01K9ARXQ3G5EKTQMXN9QQHJSXM">Desktop/Browser</h2><div class="exp-box"><span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Important</strong>: Cross-DM search is available on mobile. On desktop, it's currently in experiment and available to a limited number of users.</span></div><p><span style="color: #2e3338;">On desktop or browser, select the <strong>search bar</strong> in the upper-right corner of the app to get started. You can also hotkey your way to search success within the currently-viewed channel by pressing <strong>CTRL + F (Win) </strong>or<strong> CMD + F (Mac)</strong>. </span></p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36138588966807" alt="Example of Using Search on Desktop" width="827" height="580"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K9ARXQ3GKYEWMDRERWDCMQA3"><span style="color: #666666;">Example of Using Search on Desktop</span></h4><h2 id="h_01K9ARXQ3GSKCYZ36SNYSW49PR">Mobile</h2><p>On mobile, tap the <strong>search</strong> icon [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36138548295447" width="24" height="24">] in the upper-right corner to open the search bar and start typing.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36138548295831" alt="Example of Using Search on Mobile" width="274" height="570"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K9ARXQ3G40Y1BN31FXZEY747"><span style="color: #666666;">Example of Using Search on Mobile</span></h4><h1 id="h_01K9ARXQ3G3BXCGVEM6M0VTKX4">How to Use Search Filters</h1><h2 id="h_01K9ARXQ3GQ82XT62V003ZHMB7">Desktop/Browser</h2><p><span style="color: #2e3338;">When you open search on desktop or browser, you'll see <strong>filter</strong> options to help narrow your results:</span></p><ul>
<li class="ck-list-marker-color" data-list-item-id="ebdac3a048e4049434427b6b258bb3258"><span style="color: #2e3338;"><strong>From a specific user</strong> searches for messages sent by a particular person</span></li>
<li class="ck-list-marker-color" data-list-item-id="ec0943006e4d11c9ae49a579193936738"><span style="color: #2e3338;"><strong>Sent in a specific channel</strong> searches within a single channel</span></li>
<li class="ck-list-marker-color" data-list-item-id="e78b48b0bd1681802df29549c9fb20c1b"><span style="color: #2e3338;"><strong>Mentions a specific user</strong> finds messages that @mention someone</span></li>
<li class="ck-list-marker-color" data-list-item-id="ec4933c226569f092ab35a03d7ab46c4c"><span style="color: #2e3338;"><strong>Includes a specific type of data</strong> searches for messages containing links, embeds, polls, files, or other content types</span></li>
<li class="ck-list-marker-color" data-list-item-id="e68424e300f0901bf30db42bfe90ce81d"><span style="color: #2e3338;"><strong>More filters</strong> combines multiple search criteria for precise results</span></li>
