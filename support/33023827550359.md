<h1 id="h_01JYHHBT96YTN7JTNWCM2R587M">What is a Discord Server?</h1>
<p>A Discord server is a customizable digital space where communities can gather, communicate, and share content. Think of servers as virtual meeting places that bring people together around shared interests, hobbies, or relationships. Each server is organized with different channels dedicated to specific purposes—whether that's text conversations, voice chats, or media sharing—making it easy to keep discussions organized and accessible.</p>
<p>Discord servers are free to create and can accommodate anywhere from a small group of friends to massive communities with thousands of members. They offer flexibility in organization, moderation tools, and customization options that make them suitable for virtually any type of group or community.</p>
<h1 id="h_01JYHHBT96AZHQ9EJVPG49MNJ2">Understanding Different Types of Servers</h1>
<p>Discord offers two main server types, each designed for different community needs and scales:</p>
<h2 id="h_01JYHHBT96THW43XKZDGD1FRBX">Friend Servers</h2>
<p><strong>Friend servers</strong> are private, invite-only spaces perfect for staying connected with your close friends or smaller groups. These servers provide an intimate environment where you can chat, share memories, coordinate activities, and maintain relationships with people you know personally. The smaller scale makes them ideal for casual conversations and personal connections.</p>
<h2 id="h_01JYHHBT970XC7203923YRMKY4">Community Servers</h2>
<p><strong><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360047132851-Enabling-Your-Community-Server" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Community servers</a></strong> are larger, invite-only spaces designed to bring together people from around the world who share common interests, hobbies, or goals. These servers focus on fostering learning, discussion, and connection within specific communities—whether that's gaming, art, technology, or countless other topics.</p>
<p>Community servers come with enhanced moderation tools to help manage larger groups effectively, including:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360032807371-Server-Insights-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Server Insights</a>: Analytics to understand your community's activity, growth patterns, and member engagement</li>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/10989121220631-How-to-Protect-Your-Server-from-Raids-101" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Raid Protection</a>: Advanced security features to protect against spam, bot attacks, and disruptive behavior</li>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500000466882-Rules-Screening-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Rules</a><strong> and </strong><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/11074987197975-Community-Onboarding-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Onboarding</a>: Structured systems to welcome new members, establish community guidelines, and create screening processes</li>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360043913591-Community-Server-Welcome-Screen" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Welcome Screen</a>: Customizable introduction for new members</li>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360023968311-Server-Discovery" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Server Discovery</a>: Ability to be found in Discord's server discovery feature (if eligible)</li>
</ul>
<p>These tools make Community servers well-suited for building thriving, organized communities that can scale while maintaining a positive environment for all members.</p>

<h1 id="h_01JYHHBT9BFSEDK3NH6EJ1ADQK">Getting Started with Discord Servers</h1>
<p>Creating your first Discord server is a simple process that takes just a few minutes. Here's how to get started:</p>
<p>1. On the Discord desktop app or browser client, locate the <strong>Add a Server </strong>icon [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/33023792097559" width="24" height="24">] on the left sidebar of the app. </p>
<p>2. Select either <strong>Create My Own</strong> or choose a template. </p>
<p>3. If creating your own server, choose between:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>For a club or community</strong></li>
<li><strong>For me and my friends</strong></li>
</ul>
<p>4. Name your server and add an optional server icon.</p>
<p>5. When you’re all set, press the <strong>Create </strong>button. </p>

<h1 id="h_01JYHHVSX95BVWH0MBJH24KRBE">Understanding Key Features of Discord Servers</h1>
<p>Discord servers come with many powerful features available even without<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360028038352-Server-Boosting-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> Server Boosting</a>. Understanding these features will help you create a well-organized and engaging community space.</p>
<h2 id="h_01JYHHBT9H07916V32V7B6ZK9P">Server Channels</h2>
<p>Channels are the foundation of server organization. You can create different types of channels to help organize conversations and activities:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/4412085582359-Text-Channels-Text-Chat-In-Voice-Channels" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Text Channels &amp; Text Chat in Voice Channels</a>: For text-based communication, file sharing, and ongoing discussions</li>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/19583625604887-Voice-Channels-FAQs" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Voice Channels</a>: For voice and video communication, perfect for meetings, gaming, or casual hangouts</li>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/6208479917079-Forum-Channels-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Forum Channels</a>: Organized discussion spaces where you can create topic-specific posts without conversations overlapping (available only for Community servers)</li>
</ul>
<p id="h_01JYHHBT9JJKM375NH6P5YR6T8"><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-x-large"><strong>Advanced Channel Features</strong></span></p>
<p>To make the most of your server channels, consider these additional features:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/115001580171-Channel-Categories-101" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Channel Categories</a>: Group similar channels together for better organization</li>
<li>
<strong>Drag and drop reordering</strong>: Easily reorganize channels and categories</li>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/214836687-Discord-Roles-and-Permissions#h_01JJ7GADJD0AB33R945DMY2288" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Channel-specific permissions</a>: Control who can access specific channels</li>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/206143877-How-do-I-set-up-a-Role-Exclusive-channel" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Role-exclusive channels</a>: Create private channels for specific member groups</li>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/4403205878423-Threads-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Threads</a>: Keep conversations organized within channels</li>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/22163184112407-Polls-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Polls</a>: Create polls for community decisions and feedback</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="h_01JYHHBT9M7X0VNEBMYPTTCQ96">Server Invites</h2>
<p>Once your server is set up, you'll want to invite people to join. Discord provides several flexible ways to create and manage invites.</p>
<div class="info-box"><span style="color: #434343;" data-darkreader-inline-color=""><strong>Info</strong>: You must be the server owner or have permissions to create and manage invites. </span></div>
<p id="h_01JYHHBT9NWJE3FBFPRN9AT7D1">Here are the different types of invites you can create:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Instant Invites</strong>: One-click links that bring people directly to your server</li>
<li>
<strong>Custom Invite Links</strong>: Personalized invite URLs that are easier to remember</li>
<li>
<strong>Temporary Invites</strong>: Links that expire after a set time or number of uses</li>
<li>
<strong>Permanent Invites</strong>: Links that never expire (use with caution)</li>
</ul>

<h1 id="h_01JYHHBT9R8C15XY3WHYXWXZXF">Tools for Discord Server Owners and Moderators</h1>
<p>Effective server management requires understanding Discord's moderation tools and permission systems. These tools help you maintain a positive community environment while giving members appropriate levels of access and responsibility.</p>
<p>Essential moderation tools: </p>
<ul>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/214836687-Discord-Roles-and-Permissions" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Discord Roles and Permissions</a>: The cornerstone of Discord server moderation, allowing you to create different member tiers with specific permissions</li>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/15946797617431-Members-Page" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Members Page</a>: Get an overview of your server members, their roles, and activity</li>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/216679607-Verification-Levels" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Verification Levels</a>: Control who can send messages and participate in your server</li>
<li>
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/213507137-How-to-Prune" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">How to Prune</a>: Remove inactive members to keep your member list current</li>
</ul>
