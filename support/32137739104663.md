<h1 id="h_01JVAS81NJC93NPNHG0D1SSQJ0">What is Roblox?</h1>
<p><a href="https://www.roblox.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Roblox</a> is an online platform where users can play and create games using the platform's design tools, with millions of user-generated experiences ranging from obstacle courses to role-playing adventures. Players can customize their avatars and socialize with friends while exploring the diverse virtual worlds.</p>
<h1 id="h_01JVAS81NJPNMYVM2JCY9N7N0D">How to Connect Your Roblox Account to Your Discord Account</h1>
<h2 id="h_01JVAS81NJEAC900YE1DC7AHED">Desktop/Browser</h2>
<p>1. Head into your <strong>User Settings</strong> by pressing the <strong>cogwheel</strong> [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739047191" width="23" height="24">] <strong>icon</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739048471" width="357" height="52"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVAS81NJWNM0B2Z6DCZPBN9T" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Cogwheel icon</span></h4>
<p>2. Press on the <strong>Connections</strong> tab, select the <strong>View More </strong>[<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739058711" width="21" height="20">] <strong>icon</strong>, and then press the <strong>Roblox icon</strong>. </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739060375" width="624" height="431"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVAS81NJX1WD4FD06C596N5Z" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Connections tab under User Settings</span></h4>
<p>3. You’ll then see a spot to log into your Roblox account if you’re not already logged in. Enter your login information and then press on <strong>Log In</strong>.</p>
<p>4. If you see the correct Roblox account listed, press on <strong>Continue</strong> otherwise press on <strong>Change Account</strong> to log into a different account.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739065367" width="593" height="569"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVAS81NJN0GD26BH76JREJF9" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Roblox account confirmation</span></h4>
<p>5. You will then see a window where you need to confirm various Roblox information before finalizing the account connection. After confirming everything is correct, press on <strong>Continue.</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><strong><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739066775" width="624" height="329"></strong></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVAS81NJRX09GK5ZYTG9FXPZ" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Roblox settings</span></h4>
<p>6. Confirm everything listed is correct and press on <strong>Confirm and Give Access</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739071383" width="624" height="321"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVAS81NJ7D5DGKK1T3Q5QH85" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Confirm and Give access prompt</span></h4>
<p>6. You’ll know you’ve successfully connected when you receive a <strong>Connected your Roblox account to Discord</strong> message.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739072279" width="404" height="330"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVAS81NJM1YX2YC4AD9KDDKH" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Confirmation window that your Roblox account has been connected to Discord</span></h4>
<h2 id="h_01JVAS81NJ75CV3GT27Z0HWT8V">Mobile</h2>
<p>1. Tap your <strong>avatar</strong> icon in the bottom-right corner of the app to navigate to your <strong>profile</strong>. </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739073047" width="550" height="96"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVAS81NJ49QFQ6DTPZJE59KD" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Avatar icon</span></h4>
<p>2. Tap on the <strong>cogwheel</strong> [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739047191" width="23" height="24">] <strong>icon</strong> in the top-right corner.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739073687" width="398" height="862"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVAS81NJ77AZCV45BBZBETM7" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Cogwheel icon</span></h4>
<p>4. Press on <strong>Connections </strong>and then press on <strong>Add </strong>in the top-right corner. Scroll down until you see <strong>Roblox</strong> and select it.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739084183" width="367" height="796"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVAS81NJG2H218PCQDSMM6FA" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Roblox under connections</span></h4>
<p>5. If you’re not logged into your Roblox account already, you’ll have the option to log in. Enter your account details to proceed. If you are already logged in, press on <strong>Continue </strong>to confirm if you’re seeing the right Roblox account.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739085719" width="384" height="833"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVAS81NJ6XX00J4PTEJDYTK2" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Confirmation window of Roblox account</span></h4>
<p>6. You will then see a window where you need to confirm various Roblox information  before finalizing the account connection. After confirming everything is correct, press on <strong>Continue.</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"> <img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739089303" width="414" height="896"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVAS81NJFD9J0E11PJ6WPEA1" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Continue button to confirm connection</span></h4>
<p>7. Confirm everything listed is correct and press on <strong>Confirm and Give Access</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32137739090583" width="369" height="799"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JVAS81NJ2D1YWKA6DS1YGV46" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Confirm and Give Access confirmation</span></h4>
<p>8. You’ll know you’ve successfully connected when you receive a <strong>Connected your Roblox account to Discord</strong> message.</p>
