<p>If you come across unrecognized charges from Discord on your bank statement but don’t see the associated charges on your own Discord account(s), we’re here to help.</p><p>Before you proceed, please refer to our<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360045765792-Reporting-Unauthorized-Discord-Transactions" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> <span class="wysiwyg-underline">Reporting Unauthorized Discord Transactions</span></a> article for more information on how to accurately submit your Support request.</p><div class="warn-box">
<span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Important</strong>: </span><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=360000118612" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Please contact our billing team</span></a><span style="color: #434343;"> before filing a dispute with your bank. Filing a dispute or refund request directly with your bank for any Discord transactions may result in account suspension pending further investigation. </span><br><span style="color: #434343;">For more information, please review our </span><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/26600878251159-Chargebacks-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Chargeback FAQ</span></a><span style="color: #434343;">.</span>

</div><h1 id="h_01K0DC2VR3DSM0YXJCP5MGWDSR">What To Check First</h1><p>Before submitting a support request, take a moment to review your recent transactions and purchases. Follow these steps to identify the Discord charges to ensure we provide the right assistance:</p><ul>
<li>Look for "<strong>DISCORD*</strong>" or "<strong>PAYPAL DISCORD</strong>" on your bank statement—these are the official Discord payment descriptors.</li>
<li>For any unrecognized purchases made on Discord using your iOS device, you will need to request a refund for those purchases directly from the<a href="https://support.apple.com/en-us/118223" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> <span class="wysiwyg-underline">iOS App Store</span></a>.</li>
<li>For any unrecognized purchases made on Discord using Google Play, you will also need to request a refund directly from the<a href="https://support.google.com/googleplay" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> <span class="wysiwyg-underline">Google Play Store</span></a>.</li>
</ul><h1 id="h_01K0DC2VR66NV1T5B2SVHA3B8M">How to Submit a Support Request for Unrecognized Charges on an Unknown Discord Account</h1><p>If you're not sure which Discord account was charged using your payment method: </p><p>1. In the <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=360000118612" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">support form</span></a>, select “<strong>I have a question/issue regarding an unauthorized transaction</strong>” under “<strong>Type of Billing Issue</strong>”. </p><p>2. Select “<strong>I am unsure of the Discord account associated with the unauthorized charges</strong>” under “<strong>Type of Issue</strong>”.</p><p><span style="color: #434343;">This will help us direct your request to the appropriate support team for further assistance. </span></p><div class="warn-box">
<span style="color: #434343;">Any refunds outside </span><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360012668071-Refund-Policy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">our standard refund policy</span></a><span style="color: #434343;"> may result in disabling your Discord account per our </span><a href="https://discord.com/terms#10:~:text=Our%20right%20to%20terminate" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">terms</span></a><span style="color: #434343;">.</span>
</div><h1 id="h_01K0DC2VR886NVF9QCQ3RCMWFQ">What to Include in Your Support Request</h1><p>To help us resolve your case quickly, please provide:</p><ul>
<li><strong>Transaction date and amount</strong></li>
<li>
<strong>Purchase method</strong> (iOS App Store, Google Play Store, Other)</li>
<li>
<strong>Payment method </strong>(credit card, PayPal, Cash App, Venmo, etc.)</li>
<li>
<strong>Payment identifie</strong>r (e.g., last 4 digits of credit card, PayPal invoice ID, $Cashtag, or Venmo User ID, etc.)</li>
<li>
<strong>Screenshots</strong> of the Discord charges on your bank statement or PayPal statement</li>
</ul><div class="info-box">
<p><span style="color: #434343;">Please ensure the screenshot or photo displays the amount, date of transaction, and the merchant descriptor showing as <strong>DISCORD*</strong> or <strong>PAYPAL DISCORD</strong> on your bank statement.</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #434343;">For security and privacy reasons, unless you are writing in from the Discord account associated with the unrecognized charges, we do not accept Discord receipts as proof of payment. This screenshot must be directly from the payment source.</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #434343;">Any missing payment information may delay the resolution of your case.</span></p>
