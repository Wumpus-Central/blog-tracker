</div><h1 id="h_01KT9SXBFCMTWAR9DSV2D71B7E">What is You Bar?</h1><p><strong>You Bar</strong> is a persistent navigation element at the bottom of your Discord mobile app. It displays your <strong>avatar</strong>, <strong>username</strong>, and <strong>status</strong>, keeping your identity visible as you move through servers and conversations.</p><div class="info-box">
<strong>Note:</strong> You Bar is currently available on mobile only. The desktop experience is separate and not affected by this change.</div><h1 id="h_01KT9SXBFESKWZV7KNPWJCV0N2">How to Use You Bar</h1><p>When implementing the You Bar, we ensured that all previously available features remain accessible. Tapping the You Bar opens your profile, where you'll find the same options as before: profile editing, Quests, Shop, Settings, and more.</p><h2 id="h_01KT9SXBFFPX005N70MJQX09VK">Accessing Your Profile &amp; Settings</h2><p>1. Tap <strong>You Bar</strong> at the bottom of the screen.</p><p>2. Your <strong>profile </strong>will open, showing your avatar, username, and status.</p><p>3. To access <strong>Settings</strong>, tap the <strong>cogwheel icon</strong> [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/40981881598487" width="24" height="24">] in the upper-right corner of your profile.  </p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img class="wysiwyg-image-resized" style="aspect-ratio: 261/544; width: 42.27%;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/40981881599767" width="261" height="544" alt="Navigating to Settings"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KT9T82FV0Q97CTZPA68KZ6T1"><span style="color: #666666;">Navigating to Settings</span></h4><h2 id="h_01KT9SXBFJ9PPQPJAHSFDRP0H2">Updating Your Status</h2><p>1. Tap <strong>You Bar</strong> to open your profile.</p><p>2.
