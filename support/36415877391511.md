</div><h1 id="h_01KAVYAE7KPSW94BHE2XJ1BF5M">What is Discord Checkpoint?</h1><div class="info-box">
<strong>Note:</strong> Not everyone will get a Checkpoint—it depends on your Discord activity and your data personalization settings. Check out the <a href="#h_01KAVYAE807RSGNAZWS39V30QC">Frequently Asked Questions</a> below to learn more.</div><p>Checkpoint is Discord's first-ever year-in-review experience. It’s a celebration of all the moments that made your Discord year unique!</p><h2 id="h_01KAVYAE7NF29B1J88KR9207DX">What's included in my Checkpoint?</h2><p>Your Checkpoint includes personalized stats about your year on Discord, such as the friends you talked to most, the games you played, the communities you called home, and more.</p><p>You'll also receive an exclusive <strong>Checkpoint Avatar Decoration</strong> that you can display on your profile until <strong>January 15, 2026</strong>, as a thank you gift for being part of the Discord community! </p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36559816419607" width="624" height="480"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KAVYAE7R3K3SYVW92VDXMDMM"><span style="color: #666666;">Checkpoint 2025</span></h4><h1 id="h_01KAVYAE7SGV6842D2YZHXA5X2">How to view Checkpoint</h1><h2 id="h_01KAVYAE7SQ76NMXBRZBZGER77">Desktop/Browser</h2><p>1. Check that you have the latest version of the app. </p><p>2. Press on the <strong>flag icon</strong> [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36559816421911" width="20" height="23">] in the top-right corner to access your Checkpoint.</p><h2 id="h_01KAVYAE7V886N7NV5SMV75TE8">Mobile</h2><div class="info-box">
<strong>Important:</strong> Checkpoint has been temporarily disabled for Android. Checkpoint continues to be available on iOS, desktop and browser.</div><p>1. Check that you have the latest version of the app. </p><p>2. Tap on the <strong>You tab</strong> in the bottom-right corner.</p><p>3. Press on the Checkpoint banner to view your Checkpoint.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36559829268631" width="347" height="751"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KAVYAE7XK1A0105HHN8D7H4C"><span style="color: #666666;">Checkpoint banner on mobile</span></h4><h2 id="h_01KAVYAE7YSGSXSJQ4S9JD804P">One-time Pop-Up</h2><p>Eligible users may see a one-time modal pop-up that takes you to your Checkpoint. <br> </p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36559829269399" width="624" height="201"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KAVYAE7ZAZJE7E1BEP0J6YGP"><span style="color: #666666;">Modal pop-up for Checkpoint</span></h4><h1 id="h_01KAVYAE807RSGNAZWS39V30QC">Frequently Asked Questions</h1><p><strong>Q: Who gets a Checkpoint?</strong></p><p>A: Checkpoint is available to users who were active on Discord during 2025 and have the <strong>Use data to personalize my Discord experience</strong> setting enabled in their <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/21865322754327-Data-Used-to-Personalize-Discord"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Data &amp; Privacy settings</span></a>. We respect your privacy and only create Checkpoints for users who have chosen to enable this setting.</p><p><strong>Q: Why don't I have a Checkpoint?</strong></p><p>A: If you don't have a Checkpoint, it may be because you're new to Discord or were not active enough this year to compile stats for Checkpoint, or because you have chosen to have your data personalization <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/21865322754327-Data-Used-to-Personalize-Discord" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">setting</span></a> disabled. </p><p>Even if you don’t get a Checkpoint, you'll still be able to claim the <strong>Checkpoint Avatar Decoration</strong> as a thank you gift for being part of the Discord community! To claim the decoration, follow the steps for desktop or mobile in the <strong>How to view Checkpoint</strong> section of this article. </p><p><strong>Q: I'd like to change my data personalization setting.</strong></p><p>A: You can learn more about your data personalization setting and how to change it in this support <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/21865322754327-Data-Used-to-Personalize-Discord" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">article</span></a><span class="wysiwyg-underline">.</span> Note that changing your setting now won't give you a Checkpoint for 2025.</p><p><strong>Q: If I change my data personalization setting now, will I get a Checkpoint for 2025?</strong></p><p>A: Changing your setting now won't give you a Checkpoint for 2025. However, it would allow Discord to create personalized experiences like this for you if we offer them in the future. </p><p><strong>Q: How long will Checkpoint be available?</strong></p><p>A: You'll be able to view your 2025 Checkpoint until <strong>January 15, 2026</strong>. </p><p><strong>Q: Who can see my Checkpoint? </strong></p><p>Only you can see your Checkpoint. You can choose to share it with others if you’d like. </p><p><strong>Q: Can I share my Checkpoint?</strong></p><p>Yes! You can share your Checkpoint stats and celebrate your Discord year with friends. There's a <strong>Share</strong> button on your <strong>Summary</strong> <strong>page</strong>—just press it and choose where on Discord you'd like to share. You can also take screenshots of other pages and share them on Discord or other platforms.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img class="wysiwyg-image-resized" style="aspect-ratio: 578/376; width: 79.79%;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36559829273495" width="578" height="376"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KAVYAE89873DQQ2B73RYDF5J"><span style="color: #666666;">Checkpoint shared in a text-channel</span></h4><p><strong>Q: What about my privacy?</strong></p><p>A: We respect your privacy choices. Checkpoint is only available to users who have chosen to allow Discord to use their data to personalize their experience. This setting gives you control over whether Discord creates personalized features like Checkpoint for you.</p><p>Checkpoint is created using your activity data like games played and time spent in voice—we don't use the content of your messages.</p><p>You can review and change your data personalization settings at any time. <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/21865322754327-Data-Used-to-Personalize-Discord" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Learn more</span></a>. </p><p><strong>Q: Will Checkpoint happen every year?</strong></p><p>A: We launched Checkpoint in 2025 to celebrate Discord's 10th anniversary: a decade of the games you've played, the friendships you've made, and the communities you've built here. It's something many of you have asked for, so it felt like the perfect time to give you a personalized look back at your year with us. Plans for Checkpoint beyond 2025 are still unfolding. Stay tuned! </p><p><br> </p>
