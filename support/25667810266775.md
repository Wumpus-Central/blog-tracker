</div><h1 id="h_01J67VMBYCCNSMGT1W7YWT20KK">What was ICYMI?</h1><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/25916820975255" width="319" height="663"></p><p>The ICYMI (or In Case You Missed It) tab allowed you to catch up on server messages, activities, and more that you might have missed while you weren’t actively using Discord. <br> </p><p>Your ICYMI tab would display recently played activity from friends, status updates, messages and announcements from servers, conversation summaries and more! Some summaries may have been generated with the help of AI. </p><h1 id="h_01J67VMBYC5YYSCKSFPY1XK7M4">Frequently Asked Questions</h1><p><strong>Q: Why is the ICYMI experiment ending? </strong></p><p>A: ICYMI has helped us better understand how people want to stay connected with what’s happening in their communities. We’ll carry these learnings forward as we continue improving how users engage with their communities. As part of this evolution, we’re retiring ICYMI.</p><p><strong>Q: When exactly will it stop working?</strong></p><p>A: ICYMI will be unavailable and no longer appear in the app starting April 13, 2026. The feature will be removed automatically, no further action is needed on your end.</p><p><br> </p>
