</div><h1 id="h_01KRKKAN3ARBVDX878F8244SW0"><strong>What is the Country/Region Setting?</strong></h1><div class="info-box">
<strong>Note: </strong>The Country/Region Setting is currently a desktop experiment and is only available to a limited number of users at this time.</div><p>Country/Region is a setting on your Discord account that sets your account to a specific location. It determines:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e358fbdb206abb2d378acc8c04ed469ef">The prices and currencies you see in app</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e23c87625bedb07f6869183645200c29e">Which payment methods you can add and use</li>
</ul><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/40473463052695" width="624" height="296"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KRKQ9C37BBAZYEMRE2ZV4ADR"><span style="color: #666666;">Country/Region under the <strong>Billing tab</strong></span></h4><h1 id="h_01KRKK74VK2AQ3GAA6VR6YSNH6">How is Country/Region Determined </h1><p>Your Country/Region is set the first time you complete a purchase on Discord. Once set, it can only be changed once every 90 days. </p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e0d5f150106d8cf137db954b937294811">Your Country/Region is determined automatically based on your location and any saved payment methods. </li>
<li data-list-item-id="ed2c9b9ba0c94d863031e33dea983475c">Your Country/Region and billing address country must always match to make purchases.</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e99a97565eaaea5e61d8c857f45d07948">On mobile, your region is determined by your device system settings and the app store. </li>
</ul><h1 id="h_01KRKK74VNVDF04YQFDS98BTS3">How to Change Your Country/Region</h1><p>You can update your Country/Region on your own once every 90 days. To do so, you must:</p><p>1. Be located in the new country</p><p>2. Add a valid payment method from that country</p><p>3. Complete a successful purchase with that payment method<br><br>Keep in mind you can only add payment methods that match your Country/Region. If you have a saved method from a different country, it may not work for new purchases once your Country/Region is set.</p><h1 id="h_01KRKK7M5VKMJ1TBWWVDP2TGV6">Frequently Asked Questions</h1><p><strong>Q: What happens to my active subscriptions if I change my Country/Region?</strong></p><p>A: Your existing subscriptions won't be immediately affected, and they'll keep charging to your original payment method. If that method fails, you'll need to update your payment method and may be asked to acknowledge any pricing differences for your new Country/Region.</p><p><strong>Q: I'm traveling and can't complete a purchase. What do I do?</strong></p><p>A: This is most likely to happen if you haven't made a purchase on Discord before. On desktop, if your payment method's billing address doesn't match your current location, you'll need to wait until you're back home to complete a purchase. Otherwise, you can try making a purchase through the mobile app.</p><p><strong>Q: What if I need to update my Country/Region before 90 days?</strong></p><p>A: Your Country/Region can be updated once every 90 days using the steps above. If you're within that window and need to make a change sooner, please reach out to our support team at dis.gd/contact and we'll do our best to help. </p><p><strong>Q: Why is my payment method not available at checkout?</strong></p><p>A: Payment methods need to match your account's Country/Region. If there's a mismatch, you'll need to use one that matches your current region.</p><p> </p>
