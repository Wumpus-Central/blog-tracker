</div><h1 id="h_01KW0SP1BKMR9AS0WBQXX3VJNG">Is Discord Available on Meta Quest?</h1><p>If you’re a Meta Quest user, you can use Discord while in your VR experience. Through Meta Quest, you can access the full Discord app, like your servers, direct messages (DMs),  group DMs, voice/video calls, without needing a separate device or using the VR headset web browser.</p><p>You can get Discord on your Meta Quest by downloading the app through the Meta Horizon Store.</p><figure class="wysiwyg-image wysiwyg-image-resized" style="width: 90.11%;"><img style="aspect-ratio: 1999/1124;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41504355484695" alt="discord-server-experience-on-meta-quest-headset.png" width="1999" height="1124"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KW0SPDG4VEC5Y0R0534NYJ8G">A Discord server as seen in the Discord app on a Meta Quest device.</h4><h1 id="h_01KW0SPJQVQEDB2WF2RV2YETGF">How to Download Discord on Meta Quest</h1><p>Discord is available to download from the Meta Horizon Store. You can find it directly from your headset or through the Meta website.</p><p>1. Open the Meta Horizon Store on your Meta Quest headset, or visit the <a href="https://www.meta.com/experiences/discord/25956082250713643/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Meta Horizon Store page on your web browser</a>.</p><p>2. Select Install (or Get) to install Discord on your headset.</p><p>3. Once installed, open the Discord app and log in to your existing Discord account, or create a new account if you don't have one yet.</p><figure class="wysiwyg-image wysiwyg-image-resized" style="width: 90.16%;"><img style="aspect-ratio: 1280/800;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41504348418967" alt="discord-in-meta-horizon-store.png" width="1280" height="800"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KW0SQJ13SHDZA7PGY1MWAC6B">The Discord app on the Meta Horizon Store for installation.</h4><div class="info-box">
<strong>Note:</strong> Note: If you’re having issues with the Meta Horizon store or downloading the Discord app to your Meta Quest, please reach out to <a href="https://www.meta.com/help/quest/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Meta Support</a>.</div><h1 id="h_01KW0SQW27WDVHXR6VF57HT42H">What Discord Features Are Available on Meta Quest?</h1><p>Discord on Meta Quest includes all the features you're used to from the Discord app, including:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e3b9cf4c7904311a06de9efc47bc7a257">Your servers, DMs, and Group DMs</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e3b9b474adf4c2933aa3abce9b1c11b27">Voice calls using your headset's built-in microphone</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e09639bc6cbdf2c91bf1b62889a225cc2">Video calls if you’ve set up a Meta Avatar</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e2006fe16f9e23b2a49ec4d02b6106ea7">Streaming Meta Quest gameplay to your friend on Discord</li>
</ul><h1 id="h_01KW0SVJ412XBC0EKFSPWMPA8M">Frequently Asked Questions</h1><p><strong>Q: Do I need a new Discord account to use Discord on Meta Quest?</strong></p><p>A: No. You can log in with your existing Discord account. If you don't have one yet, you can create a new account directly from the app on your headset.</p><p><strong>Q: Is Discord on Meta Quest free to download?</strong></p><p>A: Yes. Discord is free to download from the Meta Horizon Store. Some features require a <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/articles/115000435108" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Discord Nitro</a> subscription.</p><p><strong>Q: Where can I download Discord for my Meta Quest headset?</strong></p><p>A: You can find the Discord app in the Meta Horizon Store on your headset or on your <a href="https://www.meta.com/experiences/discord/25956082250713643/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">browser</a>. </p><p><strong>Q: What Meta Quest devices is Discord available on?</strong></p><p>A: Discord is available to use on the following Meta Quest devices: Quest 2, Quest Pro, and Quest 3 series.</p><p><strong>Q: Can I use Discord voice and video calls while in VR?</strong></p><p>A: Yes. Because Meta Quest headsets have a built-in microphone, you can join Discord voice and video calls directly from your headset without needing a separate device. You can also use your Meta Avatar in video calls if you’ve created one.</p><p><strong>Q: I’m having issues with my Discord app on my Meta Quest device.</strong></p><p>A: If you’re having issues with the Discord app itself, please reach out to our <a href="http://dis.gd/support" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Support team</a>. If you’re having issues with your Meta Quest device, please reach out to <a href="https://www.meta.com/help/quest/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Meta Support</a>.</p><p><strong>Q: Does Meta see my activity in Discord because I use the app on Meta Quest?</strong></p><p>A: No. Using the Discord app on Meta Quest is like using Discord on any other supported device.</p><p><strong>Q: How does Discord use my data when I use the Discord app on Meta Quest?</strong></p><p>A: Discord uses your data consistent with our <a href="https://discord.com/privacy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Privacy Policy</a>, like on any other device.<br> </p>
