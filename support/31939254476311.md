<h1 id="h_01JTNWS5JMTYA1Z0A9WJAWWMSW">What are Tax Exemptions?</h1>
<p>A<strong> tax exemption</strong> is a specific legal provision that releases certain goods, services, organizations, or individuals from paying the standard tax (i.e. sales tax, VAT, and similar taxes) that would normally apply to a purchase. For Discord users and businesses, this means they may be eligible to have certain taxes not charged to their Discord purchases. These exemptions vary by state, country and jurisdiction.</p>
<h1 id="h_01JTNWS5JM35B0EPY92AXCQHKW">Required Documentation</h1>
<div class="info-box"><span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Info</strong>: It is your responsibility to submit all required information for a tax exemption before making any qualifying purchases. Submitting a tax exemption request does not guarantee you will receive this exemption. Discord will review your documents and relevant tax authority records to determine whether an exemption should be applied.</span></div>
<p>To help you submit the correct documentation for your tax exemption request, review the section below that applies to your situation:</p>
<h2 id="h_01JTNWS5JMXSP2XZH7PWK0QZGJ">VAT Exemptions </h2>
<p>If you're requesting a VAT exemption, you'll need to provide:</p>
<ul>
<li>Your valid VAT ID number - this must be active and verifiable</li>
<li>Your country of registration</li>
<li>Complete invoice details including your registered business name and address</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="h_01JTNWS5JMV9S7DK4SBGDZ3YEE">US Tax Exemptions</h2>
<p>For organizations based in the United States, please provide:</p>
<ul>
<li>Your sales tax exemption certificate with all required fields completed. Forms will vary by state, so please be sure to provide us with the form applicable to your state.</li>
<li>Your state code - this is required for proper processing</li>
<li>Complete invoice information matching your tax-exempt organization</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="h_01JTNWS5JM4EXTVEVRM47HJEQZ">Other Tax Exemptions</h2>
<p>For all other types of tax exemptions, you'll need:</p>
<ul>
<li>A valid Tax ID number from your local tax authority</li>
<li>Your country of registration</li>
<li>Complete invoice details that match your registration</li>
</ul>
<div class="info-box">
<strong><span style="color: #434343;">Info</span></strong><span style="color: #434343;">: We verify the documentation for accuracy and validity. If necessary, we may contact the issuing state’s or country’s tax authority to confirm legitimacy.</span>
</div>
<h1 id="h_01JTNWS5JM4WKV0209PE0V34XC">How to Validate Tax Exemption</h1>
<div class="info-box"><span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Info</strong>: Tax exemptions apply only from the date of approval onward—previous purchases cannot be adjusted. Once validated, the exemption automatically applies to all future purchases.</span></div>
<p>To help you get your tax exemption approved quickly, here's what we look for when reviewing your request:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Valid ID Check</strong>: We'll verify your VAT/Tax ID with the official tax authorities to ensure it's current and valid.</li>
<li>
<strong>Regional Verification</strong>: Your tax exemption must be applicable in your specific country or region.</li>
<li>
<strong>Matching Addresses</strong>: For your security, your payment method's billing address needs to match the country where your tax exemption is registered.</li>
<li>
<strong>Documentation Review</strong>: We'll check that all your invoice details align with our verification records.</li>
<li>
<strong>Final Review</strong>: Our Support team will carefully review your application to ensure everything is in order.</li>
</ul>
<div class="info-box"><span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Info</strong>: Having all these items ready before submission can help speed up the approval process.</span></div>
<h1 id="h_01JTNWS5JMFBKRE9FEAY18FW8E">What Happens After Submission?</h1>
<p>Once you submit your tax exemption request, our dedicated Support team will carefully review your application. Here's what you can expect:</p>
<p id="h_01JTNWS5JMVAAS6X80G25WJQ1E"><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-x-large"><strong>If Your Request is Approved:</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li>Our Support team will process your exemption within 5-7 business days</li>
<li>You'll receive a confirmation email with your exemption details</li>
<li>All future purchases will automatically be tax-exempt</li>
<li>We may periodically verify that your tax exemption is still valid</li>
<li>You can view your tax-exempt status in your account settings</li>
</ul>
<p id="h_01JTNWS5JNHE7V2Y7NHN0E49F5"><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-x-large"><strong>If Your Request Needs Additional Information:</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li>Our team will reach out via email with specific instructions</li>
<li>You'll have 30 days to provide the requested documentation</li>
<li>Your application will remain pending during this time</li>
</ul>
<p id="h_01JTNWS5JNTSAWWD2V66ZSAEQ1"><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-x-large"><strong>If Your Request is Not Approved:</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li>You'll receive an email explaining why your request wasn't approved</li>
<li>Your purchases will continue to include applicable taxes</li>
</ul>

<h1 id="h_01JTNWS5JNPGFQJY9D0F3RHNK6">Frequently Asked Questions</h1>
<p><strong>Q: How long does the tax exemption approval process take?</strong></p>
<p>A: The standard processing time is 5-7 business days after submission of a complete application with all required documentation.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Can I get a refund for taxes paid before my exemption was approved?</strong></p>
<p>A: No, tax exemptions only apply to future purchases from the date of approval. Previous purchases cannot be adjusted retroactively.</p>
<p><strong>Q: What happens if my tax ID or status changes?</strong></p>
<p>A: You must notify our Support team immediately of any changes to your tax status or ID. This may require submitting a new application with updated documentation.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Can I use my tax exemption for multiple Discord accounts?</strong></p>
<p>A: Each tax exemption is tied to a specific account and billing address. Separate applications are required for different accounts or organizations.</p>
<p><strong>Q: What if I need to update my business information?</strong></p>
<p>A: Contact our support team with any changes to your business details. Some updates may require new documentation or re-verification of your tax-exempt status.</p>
<p><strong>Q: How do I know if my organization qualifies for tax exemption?</strong></p>
<p>A: Qualification depends on your organization's legal status and local tax regulations. We recommend consulting with your tax advisor or reviewing your local tax authority guidelines.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Can I get a tax exemption for purchases I made through Apple and/or Google Play?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A: </strong>We cannot assist with tax exemption requests for purchases made through Apple or Google Play.</p>
