<h1 id="h_01JTNR3TA6BJG7DA19DG6A5T47">What to Check First</h1>
<p>Before diving into the specific items to check, take a moment to review your recent transactions and purchases. Try these steps to identify your Discord charges and make sure we get you the right support: </p>
<ul>
<li>Look for "<strong>DISCORD*</strong>" or "<strong>PAYPAL DISCORD</strong>" on your bank statement—these are the official Discord payment descriptors </li>
<li>Check if a family member or friend made the purchase</li>
<li>Verify if it's a pending charge that hasn't been fully processed</li>
<li>Check if the charge appears in any of your other Discord accounts</li>
<li>Confirm whether the charge is from an automatic subscription renewal or trial period </li>
</ul>
<div class="warn-box">
<strong>Important</strong>: <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=360000118612" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Please contact our billing team</a><span style="color: #434343;"> before filing a dispute with your bank. Filing a dispute or refund request directly with your bank for any Discord transactions may result in account suspension pending further investigation.<br></span><span style="color: #434343;">For more information, please review our <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/26600878251159-Chargebacks-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Chargeback FAQ</a>.</span>
</div>
<h1 id="h_01JTNR3TA6Y61D91NWXCK2Y2CR">How to Report an Unauthorized Charge</h1>
<p>When reporting an unauthorized charge, choose the following category that best matches your situation to ensure accurate resolution:</p>
<div>
<table style="width: 100%;">
<colgroup> <col> <col> </colgroup>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td><strong>Type of Issue </strong></td>
<td><strong>Description</strong></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>I do not recognize a charge on my Discord account</td>
<td>Select this option if you notice charges on your Discord account that you didn't make. This helps us investigate potential unauthorized access to your account.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>I am unsure of which Discord account is associated with the unauthorized charges</td>
<td>Select this option if you're unable to identify which Discord account is linked to the purchase.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>I did not want to be charged for a subscription renewal</td>
<td>Select this option if you were charged for a subscription renewal on your Discord account that you did not want or approve.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>I do not have a Discord account</td>
<td>Select this option if you see charges labeled "DISCORD*" or "PAYPAL DISCORD" on your bank statement but do not have a Discord account.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Family member of a user</td>
<td>Select this option if a family member used your payment method to make unauthorized purchases on their Discord account.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Other</td>
<td>Select this option if your situation doesn't match any of the options above. When submitting your request, please provide a detailed description of your specific circumstances.</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
</div>

<h1 id="h_01JTNR3TA6XQTC9KJGZ0BMAM7N">Submitting Your Support Request</h1>
<p>To submit your request:</p>
<p>1. Visit<a href="https://dis.gd/request" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> our support form.</a></p>
<p>2. Select <strong>Refunds, Cancellations &amp; Payments </strong>from the <strong>What can we help you with?</strong> dropdown.</p>
<p>3. Enter your email address. </p>
<p>4. For <strong>Type of Billing Issue</strong>, select <strong>I have a question/issue regarding an unauthorized transaction</strong>. </p>
<p>5. Pick the most relevant <a href="#h_01JTNR3TA6Y61D91NWXCK2Y2CR">type of issue</a> you are running into. </p>
<p>6. Include these important payment details in the <strong>Description</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Transaction date and amount</li>
<li>Payment method (e.g., credit card, PayPal, Cash App, or Venmo, etc.)</li>
<li>Payment identifier (e.g., last 4 digits of credit card, PayPal invoice ID, $Cashtag, or Venmo User ID, etc.)</li>
<li>Attach screenshots of the Discord charges on your bank statement</li>
</ul>
<div class="info-box">
<strong>Info</strong>: If any of the payment information is missing, it may delay the resolution of your case.</div>
