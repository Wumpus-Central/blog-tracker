</div><h1 id="h_01KTCJKKZ6MQVKG2CP61BCKWWV">What Are Profile Frames?</h1><p>Profile Frames are decorative borders that wrap around your Discord profile. They're a new way to add personality and style to your profile across Discord or per-server.</p><p>Profile Frames are available in the Discord Shop alongside other collectibles like Avatar Decorations, Nameplates, and Profile Effects.</p><h1 id="h_01KTCJM9FP1H2N2MSQ1YCM2ZRQ">How Can I Purchase A Profile Frame?</h1><h2 id="h_01KTCJMDN52BSEFK4RPG00SRVH">Desktop/Browser</h2><p>You can browse and purchase Profile Frames directly in the Shop.</p><p><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large"><strong>Accessing the Shop via Shop Tab</strong></span></p><p>1. Navigate to your <strong>Direct Messages list</strong> by selecting the <strong>Discord app icon</strong> in the upper-left corner.</p><figure class="wysiwyg-image"><img style="aspect-ratio: 377/386;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006830405399" alt="discord-icon-desktop-for-shop.png" width="377" height="386"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KTCJQXBWPAZ54WRM8QK61CM9">Direct Messages Icon</h4><p>2. Then, press the <strong>Shop tab</strong> [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006830407063" alt="shop-icon.png" width="24" height="24">] to the right of the server list to enter the Shop! </p><figure class="wysiwyg-image"><img style="aspect-ratio: 381/380;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006830408727" alt="desktop-shop-tab-direct-messages.png" width="381" height="380"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KTCJS4YEGP0ZCXWX0TJ4ZV44">Shop tab</h4><p>3. Once directed to the Shop, you can preview and buy Profile Frames to add to your collection. </p><figure class="wysiwyg-image"><img style="aspect-ratio: 800/459;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006817231639" alt="shop-page-profile-frames.gif" width="800" height="459"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KTCJTWX37QSYTREDZQM2BMYM">Example of Profile Frames in the Shop</h4><p><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large"><strong>Accessing the Shop via User Settings</strong></span></p><p>1. To access your User Settings, select the cogwheel icon [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006830413719" alt="cogwheel.png" width="23" height="24">] in the lower-left corner of the app, then navigate to the Edit Profiles section. </p><figure class="wysiwyg-image"><img style="aspect-ratio: 484/104;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006830414487" alt="desktop-cogwheel-user-settings.png" width="484" height="104"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KTCJWDD0P6KHFPNWV1ZJJ61D">Cogwheel icon</h4><p>2. Once there, you will see the banner to the Shop where you can select <strong>Go to Shop</strong>. You can also select <strong>Change Frame</strong> and select the <strong>Shop</strong> icon [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006830407063" alt="shop-icon.png" width="24" height="24">] to be redirected to the Shop. </p><figure class="wysiwyg-image"><img style="aspect-ratio: 800/458;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006817234839" alt="shop-in-edit-profile-frame.gif" width="800" height="458"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KTCJZ125V9XFRD0A90NH0TSZ">Access Shop via User Settings</h4><p>3. Once directed to the Shop, you can preview and buy Profile Frames to add to your collection. </p><div class="info-box">Purchased Profile Frames get added to your collection, which can be found in <strong>User Settings &gt; Profiles &gt; Profile Frames</strong>. Items available for purchase in the Shop will change periodically.</div><h2 id="h_01KTCK4GJV43NNFRHTTZNTSB9N">Mobile</h2><div class="info-box">Profile Frames are available on both desktop and mobile for purchase.</div><p>To enter the Shop on mobile, tap your <strong>avatar</strong> icon in the bottom-right corner, then tap the <strong>Shop</strong> icon [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006830407063" alt="shop-icon.png" width="24" height="24">] to view Profile Frames.</p><figure class="wysiwyg-image wysiwyg-image-resized" style="width: 40.05%;"><img style="aspect-ratio: 720/1566;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006817235479" alt="mobile-access-shop-profile-frames.gif" width="720" height="1566"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KTCK813WHHYY4YBE6VK0S0WN">Accessing the Shop on Mobile</h4><h1 id="h_01KTCKEWE0E42K94PGEWZ5XQDZ">How do I apply or change my Profile Frame?</h1><h2 id="h_01KTCKENYTZTXGFS0YT587SZYR">Desktop/Browser</h2><p>1. On the desktop app or browser, head into your <strong>User Settings</strong> by selecting the <strong>cogwheel</strong> [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006830413719" alt="cogwheel.png" width="23" height="24">]. </p><figure class="wysiwyg-image"><img style="aspect-ratio: 484/104;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006830414487" alt="desktop-cogwheel-user-settings.png" width="484" height="104"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KTCKG5PCVXK1ZY5XKGDXJSSB">Cogwheel Icon</h4><p>2. Once directed to your <strong>User Settings</strong>, select <strong>Edit Profiles</strong> to view your profile customization options. </p><p>3. Then, press the <strong>Change Frames</strong> button to open the selection menu. </p><figure class="wysiwyg-image"><img style="aspect-ratio: 1329/730;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006830419095" alt="edit-frame-button-in-edit-profile-page.png" width="1329" height="730"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KTCKK73F9Z90PNYMEFVAW4A5">Change Profile Frame Button</h4><p>4. Once you have chosen the Profile Frame you would like, select <strong>Apply</strong>.</p><figure class="wysiwyg-image"><img style="aspect-ratio: 800/458;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006830421271" alt="choose-apply-profile-frame.gif" width="800" height="458"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KTCN6GKZFEKW9NCY2VX2PBQB">Selecting a Profile Frame</h4><p>5. After applying your selected Profile Frame, confirm your profile changes by selecting <strong>Save Changes</strong>.</p><figure class="wysiwyg-image"><img style="aspect-ratio: 1542/115;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006817238807" alt="desktop-save-button-for-profile-changes.png" width="1542" height="115"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KTCN7GZ98PP2NPFW28ASSQ28">Save Changes Button</h4><h2 id="h_01KTCKQ94M5QEETEAQB0ZFEDS0">Mobile</h2><div class="info-box">Ensure your mobile device is updated to use Profile Frames on mobile. </div><p>1. On mobile, tap your <strong>avatar</strong> icon in the bottom-right corner, then tap <strong>Edit Profile</strong>.</p><figure class="wysiwyg-image wysiwyg-image-resized" style="width: 38.03%;"><img style="aspect-ratio: 473/984;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006817239447" alt="mobile-avatar-icon-settings.png" width="473" height="984"></figure><figure class="wysiwyg-image wysiwyg-image-resized" style="width: 37.91%;"><img style="aspect-ratio: 478/993;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006817240727" alt="mobile-edit-profile-button.png" width="478" height="993"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KTCNCBB8H1KJ0PQ33TPZTS28">Avatar icon (Upper), Edit Profile Button (Lower)</h4><p>2. In the <strong>Profile</strong> settings, scroll down to <strong>Profile Frame</strong> and tap the field to open the <strong>Change Profile Frame</strong> selection menu. </p><p>3. Choose a Profile Frame and tap <strong>Apply</strong>. </p><p>4. After applying your selected Profile Frame, tap <strong>Save</strong> in the upper-right corner to confirm your changes.</p><figure class="wysiwyg-image wysiwyg-image-resized" style="width: 40.02%;"><img style="aspect-ratio: 720/1566;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006916674071" alt="mobile-apply-profile-frame.gif" width="720" height="1566"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KTCKVQQ5V9BRNJTEXW0Q5P2M">Applying a Profile Frame on Mobile</h4><h1 id="h_01KTCKW5E113KE1KQW869K91RS">Where do Profile Frames appear?</h1><p>Profile Frames appear on your full profile, including your profile pop-out, the full profile card, and your profile as seen by others across Discord.</p><figure class="wysiwyg-image"><img style="aspect-ratio: 1440/900;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/41006930186135" alt="profile-frame-example.png" width="1440" height="900"></figure><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01KTCKXC6T167V8EF222V40TPJ">Profile Frames as seen in pop-out profile cards.</h4><p>Profile Frames are built to look great across different platforms and profile layouts, but you may notice that a Frame looks slightly different depending on where it's being viewed. This is intentional—some Frames may look different depending on whether you're viewing them on desktop, mobile, or in different profile layouts.</p><p>Some Frames also include additional decorative elements that appear based on the size of your profile, meaning adding more to your profile (like bio text or widgets) can unlock more of a Frame's full design. The best way to see a Frame's full design is in the Shop preview before you buy.</p><h1 id="h_01KTCKYB0FTRZM0382NCHV7S3Q">Frequently Asked Questions</h1><p><strong>Q: Are Profile Frames available to everyone?</strong></p><p>A: Profile Frames are currently an experiment, so they are limited to some Discord users.</p><p><strong>Q: Can everyone see my Profile Frame or just me?</strong></p><p>A: Everyone can see your Profile Frame.</p><p><strong>Q: Do Nitro subscribers get discounts on Profile Frames?</strong></p><p>A: Yes, Nitro subscribers get discounts across the Discord Shop, including on Profile Frames. Your discount is applied automatically at checkout—no code needed.</p><p><strong>Q: Are Profile Frames available to purchase and edit on mobile?</strong></p><p>A: Yes, Profile Frames can be purchased and edited on the Discord mobile app.</p><p><strong>Q: Can I get a refund for my Profile Frame purchase? </strong></p><p>A: Profile Frames are non-refundable, as stated in our <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/articles/360012668071" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Refund Policy</a>.</p><p><strong>Q: Can I refund Nitro after buying a Profile Frame?</strong></p><p>A: No, if you’ve purchased a Profile Frame within 5 days of starting or renewing your Nitro subscription, you will not be eligible to refund your Nitro subscription.</p><p><strong>Q: How do Profile Frames interact with Profile Effects?</strong></p><p>A: <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/articles/17828465914263" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Profile Effects</a> are another type of Discord profile decoration that can be purchased in the Shop. While the Profile Frames wrap around your Discord profile, Profile Effects render on the profile itself.</p><p><strong>Q: I purchased and applied a Profile Frame to my profile but I don’t see it.</strong></p><p>A: Make sure your Discord app is updated to the latest version. If you can’t see it, go to your <strong>User Settings &gt; Edit Profile</strong> to confirm the Profile Frame is selected. And if you still don’t see it, restart your app.</p><p><strong>Q: Is localized pricing available for profile frames?</strong></p><p>A: Localized pricing is available for some profile frame purchases and gifts. You should see at checkout whether localized pricing is available. Otherwise, the purchase will default to USD ($). You can learn more about localized pricing <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/articles/4407269525911" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.</p>
