<h1 id="h_01JSQF0NJ8JS94AD9MXS7WCFT7">How to Sign Up for Discord</h1>
<h2 id="h_01JSQF0NJ8QVW7VADZB2DW4PPB">Desktop/Browser</h2>
<p>1. Open the Discord desktop app or visit<a href="https://discord.com/login"> <span class="wysiwyg-underline" style="color: #1155cc;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">https://discord.com/login</span></a> in your browser to create an account. Click on <strong>Register</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/31676852324247" width="624" height="343"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JSQF0NJ8AV8814PV1XM20374" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">
<span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Example of where the </span><strong><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Register </span></strong><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">button is located on desktop/browser</span>
</h4>
<div class="tip-box">
<strong>Tip:</strong> To download the Discord desktop app, visit <a href="https://discord.com/download" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://discord.com/download.</a>
</div>
<p>2. Enter your account registration information including your email, username, password, and date of birth. </p>
<p>Double-check that your email address is spelled correctly— otherwise, you won't be able to change it after registration.</p>
<p>Press <strong>Continue </strong>once you’re finished.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/31676852324887" width="429" height="556"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JSQF0NJ8N6H1N6BQ1GTQC2W2" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Example of the Create an account screen on desktop/browser</span></h4>
<p>3. Complete the CAPTCHA if prompted. Then you'll be logged in and ready to use your new Discord account! </p>
<h2 id="h_01JSQF0NJ827YN6WY6C1XR3HXC">Mobile</h2>
<p>1. Download Discord from your app store and then open the Discord app.</p>
<div class="tip-box">
<strong>Tip:</strong> To download the Discord mobile app for iOS or Android, visit <a href="https://discord.com/download"><span class="wysiwyg-underline" style="color: #1155cc;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">https://discord.com/download</span></a><span style="color: #434343;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">.</span>
</div>
<p>2. Tap <strong>Register</strong> to begin creating an account.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/31676852325911" width="270" height="537"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JSQF0NJ8B45YQ3SHHZZGFA19" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">
<span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Example of the </span><strong><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Register </span></strong><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">button on mobile</span>
</h4>
<p>3. Enter your phone number or email address.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/31676861432599" width="478" height="446"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JSQF0NJ8ZBZ5VSW6F7N8BKGY" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Example of Enter phone or email for registration on mobile</span></h4>
<p>4. Follow the on-screen prompts and enter the required registration information. </p>
<p>Tap <strong>Register</strong> after entering your date of birth. </p>
<p>5. You're now logged in and ready to enjoy your new Discord account!</p>

<h1 id="h_01JSQF0NJ83SY3FD0QWE4DC38D">How to Troubleshoot Registration Errors</h1>
<h2 id="h_01JSQF0NJ8JFRYKEV7VMJYEJ64">Email is Already Registered</h2>
<p>If you see the error message <strong>Email is already registered</strong>, it means this email address is already linked to a Discord account. </p>
<p>Remember, you can only use an email address with one Discord account at a time. </p>
<div class="tip-box">
<strong>Tip:</strong> If you’re already logged into another Discord account, you’ll need to log out to be able to register a new account.</div>
