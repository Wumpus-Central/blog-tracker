</div><h1 id="h_01K7T0XX0AJGF2RED515R4RPJT">Support Account vs. Discord Account</h1><p><strong>Your Discord Support account for the Help Center is separate from your Discord app account.</strong> </p><p>This means:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="eac77f999e97cae85157438d5a363ae6a">You'll create different login credentials for the Discord Help Center</li>
<li data-list-item-id="eeb67cbd499d95a9245b88fa4392057bc">Your Discord Help Center password is separate from your Discord app password</li>
<li data-list-item-id="ee9d9b8121e43726cbed4d836ac2f2cc9">You'll need to sign in specifically to the Discord Help Center to submit or view tickets</li>
</ul><hr><h1 id="h_01K7T0XX0BMGNEJDJMVW455Z3K">Submitting Your First Ticket</h1><p>If you've never submitted a ticket to our Help Center before, follow these steps:</p><h2 id="h_01K7T0XX0C2ZCXHSBSB1PDMG1F">Create your Discord Support Account</h2><p>1. Go to <a href="https://support.discord.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://support.discord.com/</a> in your browser.</p><p>2. Select <strong>Sign in</strong>, located in the top-right corner of the page.</p><p>3. On the sign-in page, press on the <strong>Sign Up</strong> hyperlink next to <strong>"New to Discord Support"</strong> and you’ll see a new page to:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e5db94478ec7869567f721de1004c20b6">Enter your full name</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e524b31ceb5b240132e54f5509be0858f">Enter your email address</li>
</ul><p>Then once you’ve reviewed your sign-up information, select the <strong>Sign up</strong> button.</p><figure class="wysiwyg-image wysiwyg-image-resized" style="width: 66.3%;"><img style="aspect-ratio: 1247/1572;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/35715306915479" alt="In the sign in box for Discord Support, press on Sign up if you don't have a Support account yet." width="1247" height="1572"><figcaption>In the sign in box for Discord Support, press on Sign up if you don't have a Support account yet.</figcaption></figure><figure class="wysiwyg-image wysiwyg-image-resized" style="width: 66.62%;"><img style="aspect-ratio: 1240/1556;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/35715306916887" alt="In the Sign Up box for Discord Support, input your name and email in their respective text fields." width="1240" height="1556"><figcaption>In the Sign Up box for Discord Support, input your name and email in their respective text fields.</figcaption></figure><div class="tip-box">
<strong>Tip:</strong> At this stage, you won't create a password yet. Discord Support will send you a welcome email to set up your account.</div><p>4. After pressing <strong>Sign up</strong>, you'll see a message that says <strong>Check your email</strong> with instructions to look for a link to set a new password. Look for an email from <strong>Support (</strong><a href="mailto:support@discord.com"><strong>support@discord.com</strong></a><strong>)</strong> with the subject line <strong>Activate account for Discord Support.</strong></p><figure class="wysiwyg-image wysiwyg-image-resized" style="width: 63.3%;"><img style="aspect-ratio: 1228/782;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/35715306917527" alt="You'll receive a notice to check your email for a link to set a password for your Support account." width="1228" height="782"><figcaption>You'll receive a notice to check your email for a link to set a password for your Support account.</figcaption></figure><div class="tip-box">
<strong>Tip:</strong> Check your spam folder if you don't see the email in your inbox within a few minutes.</div><p>5. Open the email and press the <strong>Activate account</strong> link. This will:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e3d690fdface70cf7c3d9252ec0f87fb1">Verify your email address</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e00cb485d013c7582ac4d797f20fa2fc0">Allow you to set your Help Center password (remember, this is separate from your Discord app password)</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e10309e934726a1f13fa9e1d17d62a09f">Complete your account setup</li>
</ul><figure class="wysiwyg-image wysiwyg-image-resized" style="width: 74.24%;"><img style="aspect-ratio: 1448/1070;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/35715203302679" alt="Press on Activate account in the confirmation email you receive for the creation of your support account." width="1448" height="1070"><figcaption>Press on Activate account in the confirmation email you receive for the creation of your support account.</figcaption></figure><hr><h2 id="h_01K7T0XX0V3WW283VKWR7EVMVQ">Submit your Discord Support Ticket</h2><p>Once your Discord Support account is activated and you're signed in, you can now submit your ticket:</p><p>1. Choose Your Category</p><p>First, you'll see <strong>"What can we help you with?"</strong> with several category options, such as: <em>Help &amp; Support, Bug Reporting, Refunds, Cancellations &amp; Payments, Feedback</em>, and more.</p><p>Select the category that best matches your issue.</p><figure class="wysiwyg-image"><img style="aspect-ratio: 1052/440;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/35715264488471" alt='When submitting a support ticket, choose from the dropdown menu under "What can we help you with?" to get started.' width="1052" height="440"><figcaption>When submitting a support ticket, choose from the dropdown menu under "What can we help you with?" to get started.</figcaption></figure><p>2. Complete the Form</p><p>Depending on the category you select, you'll see different form fields. Common fields include:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e4ff79159d68422fad29a48b0bbdafa36">
<strong>Type of question</strong>: Select the specific type of support you need (this dropdown changes based on your chosen category)</li>
<li data-list-item-id="ec66200810f765cf4a38fee142d5f6770">
<strong>Additional category fields</strong>: Some categories may ask for more details like "What type of issue are you experiencing?" or "Platform" (Android, iOS, Desktop, etc.)</li>
<li data-list-item-id="ec5aef58baa13c8e0deaf820dcf1f0a2f">
<strong>Subject</strong>: Provide a brief, clear description of your issue</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e5032b638ce6710db393a9f51d4e579ad">
<strong>Description</strong>: Explain your issue in detail. Include relevant information like:<ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e66d71ef109c39ea2c2966c764f720b9f">What happened and when</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e520b2d2202ef78be7ebe571ba008f08f">Steps you've already tried</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e4c4f1a8f3ca4a8f4010a806e044556bf">Any error messages you received</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e38f04beb7405d181e76465b198bc6ff0">
<strong>Attachments (optional)</strong>: Add any relevant screenshots or files by pressing "Add file" or dragging files into the designated area</li>
</ul><figure class="wysiwyg-image"><img style="aspect-ratio: 3032/1198;" src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/35715264489623" alt="There are numerous fields to fill out when submitting a support request." width="3032" height="1198"><figcaption>There are numerous fields to fill out when submitting a support request.</figcaption></figure><p>3. Submit Your Ticket</p><p>Review all your information and press the <strong>"Submit"</strong> button to send your ticket to our Support team.</p><p>4. Check Your Email</p><p>You'll receive a confirmation email with your ticket number. Save this for your records and use it to track your ticket status.</p><hr><h1 id="h_01K7T0XX13DCZ8WTY2EH83YJXK">Submitting Additional Tickets</h1><p>If you've previously submitted a ticket, the process is even simpler:</p><p>1. Head to <a href="https://support.discord.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://support.discord.com/</a> and press on <strong>Sign in</strong> in the top-right corner</p><p>2. Enter the email and password you created for your Support account</p><p>3. Once signed in, press <strong>Submit a request</strong> and fill out the ticket form with details about your new issue</p><p>4. Once you're finished filling out the details, press on <strong>Submit to submit your ticket.</strong></p><p>5. When you're signed in, you can view all your previous tickets and their status by pressing on your profile or navigating to <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/requests" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>My activities</strong></a> or <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/requests" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Requests</strong></a>.</p><div class="tip-box">
<strong>Tip:</strong> If you've forgotten your password or have never set a password for your support account, use the "<strong>Forgot password?</strong>" link and follow the prompts to reset your password</div><h2 id="h_01K7T0XX19859KMWV8FPZ56GZB">If You've Submitted Tickets Before But Never Set Up Your Support Account</h2><p>If you previously submitted tickets via email but never activated your Help Center account or set a password, and attempt to sign up using that email address, you'll receive a message stating: </p><p><strong>"Discord Support sign-up attempt"</strong></p><p>In this case:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="ea2d5050e26dab11945942eed1456efcb">Press <strong>"reset your password"</strong> from the email you receive</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e5c4262c3e8ba5cea3d93a83207a038a2">Complete the password setup process to gain full access to your Discord Help Center account</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e91ddd3cbbc17954d8033d2d76250e97e">Once set up, you can view all your previous tickets and submit new ones through the portal</li>
</ul><hr><h1 id="h_01K7T0XX1A4B3CA9PJJPBHT5B0">Sending an Email for Support</h1><h2 id="h_01K7T0XX1A0GCMY5FSHZGZRFAP">First-Time Email Submission</h2><p>If this is your first time contacting Discord Support via email and you don't have a Help Center account yet:</p><p><strong>1. Send your email</strong> to the appropriate Discord Support email address (for example: <a href="mailto:samplesupportemail@discord.com">sample-support-email@discord.com</a>)</p><p><strong>2. Check for the activation email</strong>: You'll receive an email from <strong>Support </strong><a href="mailto:support@discord.com"><strong>support@discord.com</strong></a> with the subject line "<strong>Discord welcome email</strong>"</p><p><strong>3. Activate your account</strong>: Press the link in the bottom of the email to verify your email address and set your Discord Help Center password</p><p><strong>4. Your ticket is created</strong>: Once activated, your email will be converted into a support ticket that you can track in your Discord Help Center account</p><div class="warn-box">
<strong>Important</strong>: The email verification step is required for first-time senders to prevent spam and ensure secure communication with our support team.</div><h2 id="h_01K7T0XX1DDXHAFSCQ79QAK7ZT">Already Have a Help Center Account?</h2><p>If you already have a Discord Help Center account with the email address you're sending from, your email will automatically create a ticket associated with your account.</p><p>You'll receive a confirmation email with your ticket number.</p><hr><h1 id="h_01K7T0XX1E4XW07N1VN3T7VBK1">Tips for Submitting a Support Ticket</h1><p>To help our support team to resolve your issue:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e530ed3006a171c063f118f55989cf60a">
<strong>Be specific</strong>: Provide as much detail as possible about your issue and include screenshots when applicable</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e6018191d8086fda9b49c3ee2c12d4dda">
<strong>Reply to the same ticket</strong>: If you have follow-up information about an existing issue, reply to the original ticket email rather than creating a new ticket</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e73b75e33c4863db8c334d98330ac47d1">
<strong>Check your email OR your Requests &amp; My Activities tab for updates</strong>: Our support team will respond via email, so monitor the inbox associated with your Support account. You can also check your <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/requests" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Requests</a> or <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/requests" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">My Activities</a> tab to review any updates to your ticket.</li>
</ul><hr><h1 id="h_01K7T0XX1GJT1BNP6BWYBGC8AQ">Frequently Asked Questions</h1><p><strong>Q: I forgot my password and I can't access my Help Center support account!</strong></p><p>A: If you've forgotten your Help Center password, use the "Forgot password?" link on the sign-in page. The reset link will be sent to the email address associated with your Support account.</p><p><strong>Q: Why am I not receiving confirmation emails for my support account/support tickets?</strong></p><p>A: Check your spam or junk folder. Add <a href="mailto:support@discord.com">support@discord.com</a> to your contacts to ensure future emails arrive in your inbox.</p><p><strong>Q: I need to update support ticket information! How do I do that?</strong></p><p>Reply to the confirmation email you received when you submitted your ticket, or sign in to your Support account and add a comment to your existing ticket through the <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/requests" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Requests</a> or <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/requests" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">My Activities</a> tab.</p><p><strong>Q: Help! I can’t find my support ticket.</strong></p><p>If you're having trouble locating a ticket you submitted:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e2750fa176cf65cd5d3512d0250fb66f8">
<strong>Check your email:</strong> Search your inbox for emails from <a href="mailto:support@discord.com">support@discord.com</a> with your ticket number</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e339ef1995fdf6c06150dc7ff4306fb3b">
<strong>Search by ticket number:</strong> If you have your ticket number, use it to search your email or reference it when contacting our Support team</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e5b3603be5fa0404c5b1e57a268167137">
<strong>Check spam/junk folder:</strong> Ticket confirmation and update emails may have been filtered</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e8c15b0b32f0fd8a0d4c95ac60a6e5a64">
<strong>Sign in to view your tickets:</strong> Log in to your support account at <a href="https://support.discord.com/">https://support.discord.com/</a> and navigate to "<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/requests">My activities</a>" or "<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/requests">Requests</a>" to see all your submitted tickets</li>
<li data-list-item-id="eada21b0a76cb0db754575e07354b31bf">
<strong>Verify the email address:</strong> Make sure you're signed in with the same email address you used to submit the ticket</li>
</ul><div class="warn-box">
<strong>Important:</strong> Some in-app flows or internal processes may generate tickets automatically, and those tickets will not be available in your email or on the support portal.</div><hr><h1 id="h_01K7T0XX1PKXDSA1J8QM32EKE3">Still Need Help?</h1><p>If you're experiencing issues submitting a ticket or accessing your Discord Support account, try:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="eae60d7d7277fb8d36274c69df2048386">Clearing your browser cache and cookies</li>
<li data-list-item-id="ee10b9a2c0f54a51a96d2ab682ced11db">Using a different browser</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e663ba580ed618516780c74e04cd02824">Disabling browser extensions temporarily</li>
</ul><p><em>Also, check out our </em><a href="https://support.discord.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><em>Help Center</em></a><em> for support articles that might answer your question!</em></p><hr><div class="warn-box">
<strong>Important:</strong> Your Discord Help Center login is separate from your Discord app login. Keep your Discord Help Center credentials secure and accessible for future support needs.</div>
