<h1 id="h_01JTNT1QD45H07P8Y2MVMM1NBS">How do I use Google Pay and Apple Pay?</h1>
<p>When you're prompted to add a payment method, you'll see Google Pay or Apple Pay buttons if your browser and operating system support them.</p>
<h2 id="h_01JTNT5WQ83KAE3C9YNQRKK6KX">Desktop </h2>
<p>When purchasing <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/115000435108-What-are-Nitro-Nitro-Basic" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Nitro</a> for yourself through the Discord desktop app, you'll see Google Pay and Apple Pay as payment options.</p>

<h4 id="h_01JTNT4AXKYKAQ46XFB6R9635Z" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">Payment method window on desktop</h4>
<p>When selecting either of these options, a browser window will open to verify if your system supports the selected payment method. </p>

<p>If your desktop or mobile browser supports Google or Apple Pay, you can complete your checkout using that payment method! </p>
<p>If these options aren’t available, you’ll be redirected to our standard credit card form to complete your purchase.</p>

<h1 id="h_01JTNT1QD4DQ549Q26J9AVKMSF">System Requirements </h1>
<p><strong>To use Google Pay: </strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Chrome version 61 or newer</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>To use Apple Pay: </strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Safari browser on macOS Sierra or later (desktop)</li>
<li>Safari on iOS 10.1 or later (mobile)</li>
<li>Active Apple Pay setup with a valid payment card (<a href="https://support.apple.com/en-us/108398" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">learn how to set up Apple Pay</a>)</li>
</ul>
<h1 id="h_01JTNT1QD427BFBM5J2RWWXK2J">Frequently Asked Questions</h1>
<p><strong>Q: Can I use Google Pay on any browser?</strong></p>
<p>A: No, Google Pay is only supported on Chrome browser version 61 or later.</p>
<p><strong>Q: What devices support Apple Pay?</strong></p>
<p>A: Apple Pay works on:</p>
<ul>
<li>Desktop: Safari browser on macOS Sierra or later</li>
<li>Mobile: Safari browser on iOS 10.1 or later</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Q: What happens if my browser doesn't support Google Pay or Apple Pay?</strong></p>
<p>A: You'll be redirected to the standard credit card payment form to complete your purchase.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Where can I use Google Pay and Apple Pay on Discord?</strong></p>
<p>A: Currently, these payment methods are supported for select billing flows, including purchasing Nitro for yourself on the Discord desktop app.</p>
<p><strong>Q: Do I need to set up anything before using Apple Pay?</strong></p>
<p>A: Yes, you need to have Apple Pay already set up with a valid payment card on your device before using it on Discord.</p>
<p><strong>Q: How does the payment process work?</strong></p>
<p>A: When you select Google Pay or Apple Pay as your payment method, a browser window will open to verify your system's compatibility and process the payment securely.</p>
