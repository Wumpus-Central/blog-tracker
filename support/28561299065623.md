</div><h1 id="h_01JF805XA5HMY9Z3X6XGXGV3H7">Why is my Payment Showing As Failed?</h1><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span style="color: #5765f2;"><span data-darkreader-inline-color=""><strong><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/28561299053463" width="624" height="249"></strong></span></span></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01JF80331282DENRTXZC0QFHV5"><span style="color: #666666;"><span data-darkreader-inline-color="">Billing tab under User Settings on desktop/browser</span></span></h4><p>There are several reasons that can cause a payment to fail. To figure out what’s going on, we recommend following the steps below:</p><p>1. Ensure that the billing address associated with the card being charged is current and matches the information filed with your bank. You can learn how to manage your billing information <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360017693772-Payments-and-Billing-FAQ"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">here</span></a>.</p><p>2. Ensure your card details are entered correctly.</p><p>3. Ensure the card hasn’t expired.</p><p>4. Depending on the card, ensure there are sufficient funds in the account or that the credit limit hasn’t been exceeded.</p><p>5. It’s possible a pre-set spending limit on the card was reached. Double-check if a spending limit has been set.</p><div class="info-box">
<strong>Note:</strong> If issues persist after following the steps above, Discord's Support team cannot provide further assistance.
