</div><h1 id="h_01K6GXYZY6Z3JTZ1EE09GHRS9V">What are Widgets?</h1><p>The <strong>Profile Board</strong> is a surface on the user profile that allows users to express different aspects of their personalities. Widgets are customizable sections on your Profile Board that allow you to showcase your interests to other users, enabling you to express your identity in different ways.</p><p>There are currently five types of Widgets you can add to the Board: </p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="ec431ddfb49f911c10ffb9a17cad60a40">
<strong>Favorite Game:</strong> Highlight your ultimate favorite game with an optional short description about why you love it.</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e50a5e8ebec4265fe0d2d33688be69508">
<strong>Currently Playing:</strong> Share up to 5 games you're currently playing, with optional tags to give more information about how you play. </li>
<li data-list-item-id="e10158559457de0ada93f2b16d0160b1e">
<strong>Want to Play:</strong> Display up to 20 games on your wishlist that you're interested in trying in the future.</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e037939aebac266543ae28fd021a327a2">
<strong>Games I've Played:</strong> Showcase up to 20 games from your gaming history.</li>
<li data-list-item-id="eb0f8144fe94bef340a665ed13588747c">
<strong>Game Stats: </strong>Showcase your stats, achievements, and progress from supported games. Currently available for select games.</li>
</ul><p>Each Widget type allows you to select games from our database and personalize how they appear on your profile. If you can’t find a specific game, don’t worry– we’re actively expanding our games database! </p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/35372922066711" width="957" height="800"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K6GXYZYABDBDD7YQ0A8D8ESP"><span style="color: #666666;">Widgets in Your Profile Board</span></h4><div class="exp-box">

</div><h1 id="h_01K6GXYZYAKWV5FTZ017YRKEMR">How to Add Widgets to Your Profile Board</h1><div class="info-box"><span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Note</strong>: Widgets are currently only available on desktop or browser and won't appear on mobile for you or anyone viewing your profile. Some users may also have special privacy settings for Widgets.</span></div><p>You can add Widgets to your profile in your Profile Board and in your <strong>User Settings &gt;</strong> <strong>Profiles</strong> tab.  </p><h2 id="h_01K6GXYZYC7W03Y0Z70E7G87C7">In Profile Board</h2><p>1. Press your <strong>avatar</strong> icon in the bottom-left corner of the app, then select your <strong>avatar</strong> again to open your profile.</p><p>2. Choose any Widgets you want to add to your Profile Board. Select a Widget and press the <strong>Add</strong> <strong>game</strong> button in the upper-right corner. You can also add games from the <strong>Suggested for you</strong> section.</p><p>3. Once finished, press <strong>Save</strong> to confirm your changes!</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/35372965164439" width="800" height="571"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K6GXYZYF4E9F1EZ4QBRG8SH1"><span style="color: #666666;">Adding a Widget Directly in Your Profile </span></h4><h2 id="h_01K6GXYZYFTDPKR9R4D0F48SK2">In Profiles Tab</h2><p>1. Navigate to <strong>User Settings</strong> by pressing the <strong>cogwheel</strong> icon [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/35344653073431" width="24" height="24">].</p><p>2. Select the <strong>Profiles</strong> tab and scroll to <strong>Profile Widgets</strong>.</p><p>3. Press <strong>Add</strong> <strong>Widgets</strong> to begin customizing your profile.</p><p>4. Choose from any available Widgets. Simply select a <strong>Widget</strong> and press the <strong>Add</strong> <strong>game</strong> button in the upper-right corner. You can also add games from the <strong>Suggested for you</strong> section.</p><p>5. Once finished, press <strong>Save</strong> to confirm your changes!</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/35372922069527" width="800" height="571"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K6GXYZYR1SZ3G07Q7A4VSCMD"><span style="color: #666666;">Adding a Widget through Profiles Tab</span></h4><h1 id="h_01K6GXYZYRS85BQERKFHC0SGEJ">Managing Widgets on your Profile Board</h1><h2 id="h_01K6GXYZYRBSFE0ZQGX660TTP3">Reordering a Widget</h2><p>Hold and drag a Widget to reorder it.</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36286363339159" alt="ezgif.com-cut.gif" width="800" height="562"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K6GXYZYS01B5BBBQMWHSTYTW"><span style="color: #666666;">Example of Reordering a Widget</span></h4><h2 id="h_01K6GXYZYSDAJE5C7SHHP33BB7">Removing a Game from a Widget</h2><p>To remove a game from a Widget, hover over the game and select the <strong>Remove Game </strong>icon [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/35344653075479" width="24" height="24">].</p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36286352869399" alt="image6.gif" width="800" height="558"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K6GXYZYT2RP0PE28T7TC9TG6"><span style="color: #666666;">Example of Removing a Game in a Widget</span></h4><h2 id="h_01K6GXYZYT738GTCZA0WD118JN">Removing an Entire Widget</h2><p>To remove an entire Widget, press the <strong>Widget menu</strong> icon [<img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/35344672303895" width="24" height="24">] and select <strong>Remove Widget</strong>. A confirmation pop-up will appear where you must verify that you want to remove the Widget.  </p><p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/36286352870935" alt="image1.gif" width="800" height="558"></p><h4 class="wysiwyg-text-align-center" id="h_01K6GXYZYVH8V7PDBZCBE5S7DG"><span style="color: #666666;">Example of Removing an Entire Widget</span></h4><h1 id="h_01K6GXYZYWTDKR68JAX4TK7RPK">Frequently Asked Questions</h1><p><strong>Q: Who can see my Widgets?</strong></p><p>A: Your Widgets are visible to anyone who can view your Discord profile, including friends and server members where you share mutual servers. </p><div class="info-box"><span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Note</strong>: Widgets are currently only available on desktop or browser and won't appear on mobile for you or anyone viewing your profile. Some users may also have special privacy settings for Widgets.</span></div><p><strong>Q: Can I add the same game to multiple Widgets?</strong></p><p>A: Yes, you can feature the same game across different Widget types. For example, a game could appear in both <strong>Currently Playing</strong> and <strong>Favorite Game</strong> Widgets.</p><p><strong>Q: What if I can't find a specific game in the database?</strong></p><p>A: Currently, there are limitations on the games which can be added to Widgets. We're working on expanding our database to give you more options. </p><p><strong>Q: How many Widgets can I add to my Profile Board?</strong></p><p>A: You can add one Widget of each type (e.g. Favorite Game, Currently Playing, Want to Play, Games I've Played).</p><p><strong>Q: Do Widgets work on mobile?</strong></p><p>A: Currently, Widgets are only available to edit and view on desktop and browser versions of Discord. </p><p><strong>Q: Can I hide specific Widgets from certain people?</strong></p><p>A: No, Widget visibility follows your general profile privacy settings. You cannot selectively hide individual Widgets from specific users.</p><p><strong>Q: Do Widgets show my real-time gaming activity?</strong></p><p>A: No, Widgets display manually curated game lists. They don't automatically update based on your current Discord activity status. However, real-time gaming activity is shown in the <strong>Activity</strong> tab.</p>
