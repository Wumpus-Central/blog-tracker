<p>If someone you know, including a family member, friend, partner, etc., has used your payment information to make a purchase on <em><strong>their</strong></em> Discord account without your authorization, we’re here to help.</p><p>Before you proceed, please refer to our<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360045765792-Reporting-Unauthorized-Discord-Transactions"> <span class="wysiwyg-underline">Reporting Unauthorized Discord Transactions</span></a> article for more information on how to submit your support request accurately.</p><div class="warn-box">
<span style="color: #434343;"><strong>Important</strong>: </span><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=360000118612"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Please contact our billing team</span></a><span style="color: #434343;"> before filing a dispute with your bank. Filing a dispute or refund request directly with your bank for any Discord transactions may result in account suspension pending further investigation. </span><br><span style="color: #434343;">For more information, please review our </span><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/26600878251159-Chargebacks-FAQ"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Chargeback FAQ</span></a><span style="color: #434343;">. </span>

</div><h1 id="h_01K0D59AM27SKDWYWH2SHAB1R2">What To Check First</h1><p>Before submitting a support request, take a moment to review your recent transactions and purchases. Follow these steps to identify the Discord charges to ensure we provide the right assistance:</p><ul>
<li>Look for "<strong>DISCORD*</strong>" or "<strong>PAYPAL DISCORD</strong>" on your bank statement—these are the official Discord payment descriptors.</li>
<li>For any unrecognized purchases made on Discord using your iOS device, you will need to request a refund for those purchases directly from the<a href="https://support.apple.com/en-us/118223" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> <span class="wysiwyg-underline">iOS App Store</span></a>.</li>
<li>For any unrecognized purchases made on Discord using Google Play, you will also need to request a refund directly from the<a href="https://support.google.com/googleplay" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"> <span class="wysiwyg-underline">Google Play Store</span></a>.</li>
</ul><h1 id="h_01K0D59AM4YZKHHCDCTF4AG4HC">What to Do When Someone You Know Makes an Unauthorized Discord Charge on Your Account</h1><p>To report unrecognized charges on your Discord account made by someone you know, follow the steps that match your situation:</p><h2 id="h_01K0D59AM5EJWQ8B19FQBBCTPR">If the Charges are on Your Own Discord Account</h2><p>Check if the unrecognized charges appear on your own Discord account. If so, follow these steps: </p><p>1. Submit a ticket using our <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=360000118612" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">support form</span></a>.</p><p>2. Select <strong>"I have a question regarding my purchase/payment method</strong>" under <strong>Type of Billing Issue</strong>.</p><p>3. Select "<strong>I want a refund</strong>" under “<strong>How can we help you with your purchase?</strong>”.</p><h2 id="h_01K0D59AM6W3SBVFK2CP4WTGBY">If the Charges are on Someone Else's Discord Account</h2><p>If the charges appear on another person's Discord account, have them submit a refund request at<a href="https://dis.gd/billing" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline"> https://dis.gd/billing</span></a> using the email address associated with their account. </p><h2 id="h_01K0D59AM676YV2R8RXXB6QZY7">If You Have Access to Their Email</h2><p>If the charges are on someone else's account and they can't submit a support request themselves, you can submit one on their behalf using the email address linked to their Discord account: </p><p>1. In the <a href="https://dis.gd/billing" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">support form</span></a>, select “<strong>I have a question/issue regarding an unauthorized transaction</strong>” under <strong>Type of Billing Issue</strong>.  </p><p>2. Select “<strong>Family Member of a user”</strong> under <strong>Type of Issue</strong>.<strong> </strong></p><p>3. Select “<strong>Yes</strong>” when asked “<strong>Is the email provided associated with your family member's Discord account?</strong>”. </p><h2 id="h_01K0D59AM9J58XZQQ4CY4NHHPV">If You Cannot Access Their Email or Contact the Account Owner</h2><p>If you <em>can't</em> submit a support request from the email address associated with the Discord account or contact the account owner directly, follow these steps:</p><p>1. In the <a href="https://dis.gd/billing" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">support form</span></a>, select “<strong>I have a question/issue regarding an unauthorized transaction</strong>” under <strong>Type of Billing Issue</strong>.  </p><p>2. Select "<strong>Family Member of a user</strong>" under <strong>Type of Issue</strong>. </p><p>3. Select “<strong>No</strong><em>”</em> when asked “<strong>Is the email provided associated with your family member's Discord account?</strong>”.</p><p>4. Select “<strong>No</strong>” when asked “<strong>Do you have access to the email associated with your family member’s Discord account?</strong>”.  This will help us direct your request to the appropriate support team for further assistance.</p><div class="warn-box">
<span style="color: #434343;">Any refunds outside </span><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360012668071-Refund-Policy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">our standard refund policy</span></a><span style="color: #434343;"> may result in disabling your Discord account per our </span><a href="https://discord.com/terms#10:~:text=Our%20right%20to%20terminate" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">terms</span></a><span style="color: #434343;">.</span>
</div><h2 id="h_01K0D59AMCDPRHX1806PS83PC9">What to Include in Your Support Request</h2><p>To help us resolve your case quickly, please provide:</p><ul>
<li><strong>Transaction date and amount</strong></li>
<li>
<strong>Purchase method</strong> (iOS App Store, Google Play Store, Other)</li>
<li>
<strong>Payment method </strong>(credit card, PayPal, Cash App, Venmo, etc.)</li>
<li>
<strong>Payment identifie</strong>r (e.g., last 4 digits of credit card, PayPal invoice ID, $Cashtag, or Venmo User ID, etc.)</li>
<li>
<strong>Screenshots</strong> of the Discord charges on your bank statement or PayPal statement</li>
</ul><div class="info-box">
<p><span style="color: #434343;">Please ensure the screenshot or photo displays the amount, date of transaction, and the merchant descriptor showing as <strong>DISCORD*</strong> or <strong>PAYPAL DISCORD</strong> on your bank statement.</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #434343;">For security and privacy reasons, unless you are writing in from the Discord account associated with the unrecognized charges, we do not accept Discord receipts as proof of payment. This screenshot must be directly from the payment source.</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #434343;">Any missing payment information may delay the resolution of your case.</span></p>
