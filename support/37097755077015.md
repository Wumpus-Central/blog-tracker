</div><h1 id="h_01KGQHPPA8G1R87AT5H5R6FYVY">What are Monthly Orbs Drops?</h1><div class="book-box">
<strong>Want to learn more?</strong> <br>You can learn more about Discord Orbs <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/30593690165783"></a><a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/30593690165783" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">here</span></a>.</div><p>Monthly Orbs Drop is a benefit for Nitro subscribers that provide additional Discord Orbs on a monthly basis. As a Nitro member, you'll automatically receive monthly Orbs that you can use in the <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/17162747936663" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Shop</span></a> to get <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/26362805097623-Nitro-Credits-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Nitro Credit</span></a>, <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/13410113109911" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Avatar Decorations</span></a>, <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/17828465914263" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Profile Effects</span></a>, and other eligible Shop items. </p><p>These Orbs are added directly to your total Orbs balance. They're in addition to any Orbs you earn through <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/22225719947543-Discord-Quests-FAQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Discord Quests</span></a>, giving you more opportunities to personalize your Discord experience. </p><h1 id="h_01KGQHPPABZTRH8NTAAR1CDQZ1">How to Earn Monthly Orbs with Nitro </h1><h2 id="h_01KGQHPPABEQW76BEMAQKZBQQG">Eligibility Requirements</h2><div class="tip-box">
<strong>Tip:</strong> <span style="color: #434343;">For eligible users who subscribe to Nitro, it may take up to 1 week until your first Monthly Orbs drop.</span>
</div><p>To receive a Monthly Orbs Drop, you need:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e846d2cdf54407701000bd7f8fdebadb9">An active paid Nitro subscription </li>
<li data-list-item-id="e00f6c72b31c3eff27a8c981107d2fdc2">Your subscription must be in good standing (for example, subscription is not in a grace period due to payment issues)</li>
<li data-list-item-id="ec5b4b6d89f65aff1e77dc59fc895604e">Be part of the Monthly Orbs Drop experiment group</li>
</ul><p>Monthly Orbs Rewards are included for:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="eced039e741291e9541a830159d14acd6">Monthly Nitro subscriptions</li>
<li data-list-item-id="efcb483d3be272649790f556491b4f581">Annual Nitro subscriptions</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e6d204192c9019c3fea047e740cecbd81">Subscriptions with selected promotional discount (including BOGO offers)</li>
</ul><p>Monthly Orbs Rewards are <strong>NOT</strong> included for:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="ea81989d7d7b28981f5ab57e0d7f05dcc">Gifted Nitro subscriptions</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e022a02d4208f3bbc310628571bf26cf2">Nitro through Nitro Credits</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e290ff3dda31445edc0cc9e775a46069a">Nitro Basic or Classic plans</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e173e9ed98bffbc2756686a45aea335c9">Accounts with suspended or terminated subscriptions</li>
<li data-list-item-id="ee9ddffa78b05a65b5ad4118c8c705eb0">Nitro trials from partnership promotions</li>

We appreciate your patience as we refine this feature! </p><p><strong>Q: Do Orbs expire?</strong></p><p>A: No, your Orbs will not expire! Once they're granted to your account, you can hold onto them for as long as you like. </p><p><strong>Q: What happens to my Orbs if I cancel my subscription?</strong></p><p><strong>A:</strong> You keep any Orbs you’ve already earned from previous months. After you cancel, you’ll stop receiving future Monthly Orbs drops.</p><p><strong>Q: My payment failed—will I still get my Orbs?</strong></p><p>A: If your payment failed, you will not receive your next Monthly Orbs drop.
