<h1 id="h_01JWVA4Z8YM31MXSRDYKDKQ2HB">Invalid Email Error Message</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32557355027991" width="477" height="418"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JWVA4Z8YG2DX8HDJD1Q9BVQ8" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">
<span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Example of the error message </span><strong><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Invalid Email</span></strong>
</h4>
<p>If you see an <strong>Invalid Email</strong> error when trying to add an email address to your Discord account, we recommend waiting 24 hours before attempting to use the same email again.</p>
<div class="form-box">
<strong>Contact Us:</strong> If the error persists after 24 hours and you don’t have an alternative email address to try, our support team is happy to help! Please reach out to our Support team: <a href="http://dis.gd/contact" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">http://dis.gd/contact</a> </div>
<h1 id="h_01JWVA4Z8YM2ATA20XVPS62VPY">Email is Already Registered</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32557355028375" width="441" height="433"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JWVA4Z8Y9677M6XEAE38GJK7" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">
<span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Example of the </span><strong><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Email is already registered </span></strong><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">error message</span>
</h4>
<p>If you receive the error message <strong>Email is already registered </strong>when trying to change the email address on your account, this is because the email address you are entering is already tied to a Discord account. Please try another email address.</p>
<div class="form-box">
<strong>Contact Us: </strong>If the email address is not tied to a Discord account, please reach out to our Support team: <a href="http://dis.gd/contact" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">http://dis.gd/contact</a> </div>
<h1 id="h_01JWVA4Z8Y60174XVX2VGXVSE7">Email Does Not Exist</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32557375602839" width="462" height="374"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JWVA4Z8YWXS59SMB6BH8K4EJ" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">
<span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Example of the </span><strong><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Email does not exist</span></strong><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color=""> error message</span>
</h4>
<p>If you are trying to log into your Discord account, but you’re getting the error message <strong>Email does not exist,</strong> this is because the email address you are entering is not tied to a Discord account. If you’re unsure what email address your account is tied to, please check out the <a href="#h_01JWVA4Z8Y4VS4FYAR83VSPCS2">Forgot Email section</a>. Our Support team is not able to tell you what account your email address is tied to.</p>
<h1 id="h_01JWVA4Z8YPB354Z33HGH3W3MW">Changing Email Too Fast</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/32557355033367" width="475" height="570"></p>
<h4 id="h_01JWVA4Z8YZS4FDTRP384CAWV7" class="wysiwyg-text-align-center">
<span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Example of the </span><strong><span style="color: #666666;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">You are changing your e-email too fast. </h4>
<p>If you are trying to verify or change your email address, but you’re getting the error <strong>You are changing your e-mail too fast. Try again later</strong>, please wait 24 hours and then try verifying or changing again.</p>
<div class="form-box">
<strong>Contact Us:</strong> If you’re still getting the error message even after waiting 24 hours, please reach out to our Support team: <a href="http://dis.gd/contact" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">http://dis.gd/contact</a> </div>
<h1 id="h_01JWVA4Z8Y4VS4FYAR83VSPCS2">Forgot Email</h1>
<div class="info-box">
<strong>Info:</strong> Our Support team is not able to disclose to you what email address is tied to your account.</div>
<p>If you can’t remember the email address that is associated with your Discord account, there are a few steps that you can try to find this out. </p>
<p>1. Check if you're logged into the Discord account on another device/browser. You can then see what your email address is by accessing the <strong>My Account </strong>tab via <strong>User Settings</strong>.</p>
<p>2. At the login page, type in the email address that you think might be associated with your Discord account and then press on <strong>Forgot your Password</strong>. If that email address is tied to an account, you should receive an email to reset your password.</p>
<p>3. Check the inboxes of all your email addresses and search for emails from Discord.</p>
<p>4. If your account also has a phone number tied to it, you can try logging in using the phone number through the login page instead. Once logged in, you can check the <strong>My Account</strong> tab under <strong>User Settings</strong> to see what email address is on the account.</p>
<h1 id="h_01JWVA4Z8YW936N23EFKCKAFPB">Lost Access to Email</h1>
<div class="info-box">
<strong>Info:</strong> Our Support team is not able to change the email address associated with your Discord account.</div>
<p>Unfortunately, if you’ve lost access to the email address that is tied to your Discord account, you’ll need to be able to access it to be able to change the email address. You can try reaching out to your email provider to see if you’re able to obtain temporary access to your account.</p>
