</div><hr><h1 id="h_01KK2GPZ0PM0J82JD3APNJS8JP"><strong>Introduction to Australia’s eSafety Commissioner</strong></h1><p>The eSafety Commissioner is Australia’s independent regulator for online safety. eSafety works to protect all Australians from the most serious forms of online harm and promotes safer, more positive online experiences.</p><ul>
<li class="wysiwyg-list-bold" data-list-item-id="e33f3c457a7f26eaadfa827548ef89f3a">
<strong>Learn more about eSafety:</strong><ul><li data-list-item-id="e511dcd0e73e2452d07df1c9c069107d7">Visit <a href="https://www.esafety.gov.au/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">eSafety’s homepage</a> to understand their role, functions, and the protections available to you at: <a href="https://www.esafety.gov.au/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://www.esafety.gov.au/</a>
</li></ul>
</li>
<li class="wysiwyg-list-bold" data-list-item-id="efea945bbed9d31051c3f454f025348fa">
<strong>Contact eSafety:</strong><ul><li data-list-item-id="ecca1c143f3b53f13f173e9aeff45458f">If you’ve experienced serious online harm and feel it hasn’t been adequately addressed, you can submit a complaint directly to eSafety. Find out how to contact them at: <a href="https://www.esafety.gov.au/report" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://www.esafety.gov.au/report</a>
</li></ul>
</li>
<li class="wysiwyg-list-bold" data-list-item-id="efc0e71750177d3cedf875bd58b48c174">
<strong>Get support:</strong><ul><li data-list-item-id="ee77806ba2705fab2499ee1fcbcd34c03">If you or someone you know needs counseling or support services after exposure to online harm, you can find a list of support services at: <a href="https://www.esafety.gov.au/about-us/counselling-support-services" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://www.esafety.gov.au/about-us/counselling-support-services</a> </li></ul>
</li>
</ul><div class="info-box">Australian users who believe Discord has not met its obligations under the Online Safety Act may submit a compliance complaint <a href="mailto:onlinesafetyau@discord.com"><strong>onlinesafetyau@discord.com</strong></a>. <br>To learn more about Australia's Online Safety industry codes and what they require of platforms like Discord, visit <a href="http://esafety.gov.au" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">esafety.gov.au</a>.</div><h2 id="h_01KK2GPZ0V5SZWVP3RCZCT9E4E"><strong>Default Safety Settings for Australian Users</strong></h2><p>All Australian users have a default teen-appropriate experience with enhanced safety settings.This means updated communications settings, restricted access to age-gated spaces, and content filtering, nearly all of which require age assurance to modify or access, including:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e75ad90f4d9e43afef9f33e1d5bb35676">
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/7924992471191" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Message Requests</strong></a> turned on by default, so messages from people you don’t know go to a separate inbox before reaching you</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e5098748f9f64657cd3c78b7f1f361424">
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/18210995019671" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Sensitive content filters</strong></a> enabled by default</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e2fc634d80566348eb5ec21bfcac9cda0">Age assurance is required to access age-gated content like age-restricted servers and channels.</li>
</ul><p>Users can complete <strong>age assurance to update these default safety settings or access age-gated content and features.</strong></p><div class="book-box"><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e908239239881f88539398467adb64d20">Want to learn more about how age assurance works? Read this article here: <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/30326565624343" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">How to Complete Age Assurance on Discord</a> </li>
<li data-list-item-id="e75052457cefd4811906f89f43715f9a5">Want to learn more about what's different for Australian users? Read this article here: <a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/38832004637207" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Age Assurance for Australia</a>
</li>
</ul></div><h2 id="h_01KK2GPZ0XR574NH6B7GQCB2B2"><strong>Additional Protections for Teens</strong></h2><p>Discord has additional default protections in place for teen users, including:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="eb05c14acd6d25280b008673977c0e19a">Direct messages from unknown users, including adults, are filtered by the Message Request feature and teen users cannot disable Message Requests (users must be confirmed adults via one of Discord's methods of age assurance).</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e82058fcf6e43ba1e97e1948a892e8ae5">Safety alerts when interacting with unfamiliar accounts</li>
<li data-list-item-id="ee1442cba0c217ff493b1f789eb0c7d7f">Stricter content filters</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e9b4b36ed4b2bcff3ad9deca4c36ab5f6">Family Center, which lets parents and guardians stay connected to their teen’s Discord activity</li>
</ul><div class="book-box">

</div><hr><h1 id="h_01KK2GPZ0ZTFVB32CVSDZ8K1XA"><strong>Safety Tools You Can Use</strong></h1><p>Discord gives you control over who can contact you and what content you see. In addition to your default settings, you can actively manage your experience using these tools:</p><ul>
<li data-list-item-id="e675ceea543d65a6e64e85a20b6ac384d">Stop any user from messaging you</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e0c99174a237d135047bab909451dd055">Manage who can send you direct messages</li>
<li data-list-item-id="ea28072b695314f1da90e65409103d4b0">Filter messages from people you don't know</li>
<li data-list-item-id="e47eba5f0a77f9d793e797df14b98615b">Set your preferences for sensitive content</li>
<li data-list-item-id="ebd785e1809c3f0ffbc60021a24aadfaf">Leave any group DM at any time</li>
</ul><div class="book-box">
